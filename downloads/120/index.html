After a while.

Everyone in the arena is gone. As for the big adjudicator, he is going to receive a decree, but Bai Donglin is the only one left in the huge arena.

Bai Donglin sat cross-legged on the metal stand, frowning slightly, and all kinds of experiences flashed in his mind so far.

He has never had a relationship with the people of the Divine Blood Saint Sect, and there are already some speculations in his heart that he has not offended many people, and every time he has been clean and neat, without leaving a tail.

First of all, Xiao Hongmao Xuan Ye was ruled out. This person was killed by him in the ancient world. There is absolutely no possibility of leaking the secret of the process. Moreover, he has also learned about Xuan Ye's background before, and he has not so much energy and blood. The people of Shengzong colluded together.

Then there are only two possibilities. One is a battle on the Nu River. He destroyed the coming of the unknown evil **** behind the black hole, but judging from the situation last time, the coming of the evil **** was so difficult, obviously in Qianyuan Realm. There is no hidden power.

There was only one enemy left, the Black Underworld, in the ancient world, he killed hundreds of thousands of them, almost killing them all, and the people behind it must hate him deeply.

It is not impossible for such a big force to have a way to deduce his identity. Maybe he knows a lot, otherwise he would not be so anxious to activate the dark child in the Divine Blood Sacred Sect to engage him.

His ability to restrain the soul of the dark underworld may have been exposed.

There are various possibilities deduced in his mind, he has nine levels of certainty, all this is the revenge of the black underworld.

The cold light in Bai Donglin's eyes flickered, is he trying to get him out of the Saint Sect, and then find a chance to kill him?

Very good, you can't kill me, I'm going to kill you! Bai Donglin's thoughts were rolling, and all kinds of cruel methods flashed one by one.

"What are you thinking about? So absorbed."

The big man wearing a bronze mask suddenly appeared in front of Bai Donglin. Bai Donglin restrained the killing intent in his eyes and stood up to salute:

"My lord, can you set off now?"

"Well, yes, the procedures are all handed over, Zongmen decided to use the cross-domain teleportation array to send you to the black prison."

"This is also to prevent someone from intercepting and killing you on the way. After all, the Divine Blood Saint Sect has worked hard to calculate you, and there must be follow-up methods."

Bai Donglin nodded slightly, Shengzong considered it well, and the opponent did have a high chance of ambushing him halfway.

"So, it will trouble your lord."

"It's not troublesome, it's also that we can't help you in the Infinite Arena. The dead rules left by these ancestors..."

The big man murmured slightly. At the end of his speech, his voice was low and inaudible, and Bai Donglin followed him with a smile. It seemed that the adjudicator's concept was still relatively young, not as pedantic as those old monsters.

It didn't take long for the two to come to a hall. There was only a huge formation in the huge hall, built from countless radiant and precious materials.

What was engraved on the formation was not an inscription, but an unpredictable space law. A strong spatial fluctuation filled the entire hall. This was a cross-domain teleportation formation that belonged solely to the Infinite Arena.

The two came to the center of the formation, the judge took out a jade card, the mind sank into it, determined the position coordinates, and activated the formation.

Bai Donglin clearly felt that the surrounding spatial laws were rolling fiercely, a wave of spatial fluctuations began to condense, and a transparent mask slowly rose up, wrapping the two of them.

Cross-domain teleportation, the distance is extremely long, this can be greatly different from the ordinary teleportation array. Without the protection of the formation, the low-level cultivators will be torn apart by the violent spatial fluctuations in an instant. When the teleportation passes, they will become a mess Meat!

The formation was completely activated, a huge beam of light rose into the sky, and another formation emerged above the dome of the main hall, absorbing the beam of light and rebounding, and the two of Bai Donglin disappeared in the beam of light.

Sin domain, one of the seven great prisons, the black prison.

A huge city built on a black barren plain. In a large hall in the center, the cross-domain teleportation array lit up with a strong white light, and Bai Donglin and the adjudicator appeared in it.

"Chi Yan, long time no see!"

Outside of the formation, a man who was as majestic as an iron tower, Bai Donglin was afraid that he was eight feet tall.

"You are the same, Kuang Zhen."

Chi Yan stepped forward, and the two of them hugged each other, their fists clacking behind each other.

"Is it this kid? I asked you to deliver it in person. It seems that the sect values ​​him very much."

"Yes, in the next five years, take care of him."

Bai Donglin stepped out of the formation, slightly surrendered, and said:

"My lord, it's hard work."

"Hahaha, don't worry, don't talk about the relationship between the sect, Chi Yan and I are the iron buddies who crawled out of the corpse on the battlefield together, if he speaks, I will naturally follow you!"

Then the three of them walked and talked, and while walking towards the underground core, they introduced the black prison to Bai Donglin.

There are seven great prisons in the entire barren crime domain, all of which are ancient things that have existed since a long time ago. It is no longer a question of who built them. It is only known that the only purpose of these great prisons is to imprison criminals with the most sinister crimes.

Unlike Bai Donglin, who walks through the scene, the monks who are generally imprisoned have no chance of seeing the sky again for life.

The black prison was built deep in the ground and formed an inverted pyramid structure as a whole, divided into ninety-nine floors, each of which was a huge space like a small world.

The lower the space, the smaller the prisoner’s strength, or the heavier the crime committed.

This black prison is not only the imprisonment of human monks, there are all kinds of messy creatures, monsters and ghosts, and many alien creatures.

"Boy, according to the above decree, you will be imprisoned in the twenty-ninth level of black prison. Take it. This is my token. If you encounter any trouble, it will show up. No one will dare not give Lao Tzu face to the mess below."

"It really doesn't work, you can communicate the jade card with your mind and call me down."

The three of them were very fast, and it didn't take long for them to come to the depths of the earth. All the way, Bai Donglin could be regarded as seeing what is meant by tight defense.

There are nine giant gates made of precious materials, and countless arrays are layered on top of each other. Guards can be seen everywhere, and guards hidden in the dark. With a glance, Bai Donglin's heart beats wildly.

Under the leadership of Kuang Zhen, after repeated verifications, he finally came to the body of "Black Prison".

The various blockade defenses above are the icing on the cake, and it is this huge "black prison" body that really plays a suppressing role.

Standing on top of the dark "black prison" of unknown materials, Bai Donglin's eyes flashed, and the dark body in this space was completely visible to him.

This underground space only exposes a part of the "Black Prison" body, and more parts are still hidden in the depths of the rock. On this huge body, it is engraved with mysterious patterns, not secret patterns or inscriptions~www. mtlnovel.com~ It's not even a formation method, but something that Bai Donglin has never seen before, which is mysterious and unusual.

Kuang Zhen took out a golden decree, which was exactly what Chi Yan had brought from Shengzong. The content inside was invisible to the east, except for the big "polar" behind the decree.

Kuang Zhen placed the decree on the body of "Black Prison", and the golden characters slowly jumped out and merged into the mysterious pattern below.

The pattern absorbed the golden characters as if it came to life, and began to converge and rotate, gradually forming a dark vortex channel.

Kuang Zhen looked up at Bai Donglin, his huge tiger eyes became solemn, and said in a serious tone:

"Boy, in order to avoid suspicion, the verdicts and punishments made by the Infernal Arena since ancient times are all neutral, or a place under the common control of the entire cultivation world. Black Prison is such a place."

"On the twenty-ninth floor of your detention, the person with the highest cultivation level does not exceed the supernatural power state, and the environment in the black prison is special, which is very suitable for physical cultivation. You will know when you enter."

"Plus the token I gave you, five years will soon pass, and nothing will happen."

"Remember, all levels in the black prison communicate with each other, but you can't go up, don't run to the next level if you don't want to die!"

"After five years, I will pick you up and personally send you back to the Holy Sect."

"Disciple Bai Donglin, thank you two adults!"

Bai Donglin bowed slightly to salute, and said with a grateful expression. After speaking, he jumped into the dark passage and instantly entered the "Black Prison" body.

"Walk, long time no see, our brothers have a good drink!"

"Don't worry, if I watch, this little guy will be fine."

Chi Yan Kuangzhen's two big guys hooked up their shoulders, talked and laughed, and between their breaths, the two figures left the ground.
