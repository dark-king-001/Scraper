Mother river, true spirit...

After Bai Donglin pondered slightly, he decided to give up and completely put out the idea of ​​cultivating the "Qi Cultivation Route God Demon". In fact, this can be regarded as an obsession when he was detected by his second brother that he did not have the talent of Qi Cultivation Spirit. .

Why can't I become the obsession of Qi Xiu!

Now I decided to let go of this obsession, not only because I couldn't explore the taboo secret of the true spirit, but also because of the fierce reaction of the mother river, this series of weird changes.

He didn't want to keep in touch with the real spirit anymore, he felt that there was an unknown hidden danger in it, and he didn't like this kind of thing beyond his control in his body.

Sure enough, my second brother was right. I am just suitable for physical cultivation, and I can only follow the physical cultivation way. In this life, Bai Donglin can only catch the physical cultivation road and die!

After Bai Donglin figured it out, the backlog of miscellaneous thoughts in his heart was cut into nothingness, and he suddenly felt an indescribable relief in his heart.

To be willing to be willing, sometimes to abandon, let go, is also a kind of gain.

Putting down the obsession, Bai Donglin, who understood his original mind, immediately felt that his soul was clear, the powerful soul had grown inexplicably, the Taoist heart became more and more crystal clear, his thoughts were pure, and his will became more tenacious.

The golden light of the source of life in the body was shining, and countless spiritual apertures seemed to be calling for joy, and the induction of the laws of heaven and earth became clearer!

Bai Donglin smiled slightly. Does "The Way of Physical Cultivation" still know how to be jealous? Ann, Ann, I will spoil you from now on!

When the mind moved, the Jidao bracelet flew out of the original world and was automatically worn on the wrist. Two soul-swallowing gourds were also suspended in the void, pinching the magic with one hand, leaving more than two million unused. The soul spewed out, and then many souls dissipated between the heaven and the earth, returning to the mother river like a starry true spirit.

After doing all this, Bai Donglin slightly offered his hand to the sky, silently thinking in his heart, "The mother river is big, I apologize to your old man, don't mess with me in the future!"

Your old man is a real critic. If he doesn't agree with him, he will hit me with the sun. Bai Donglin is really convinced!

At this time, dense and mysterious patterns began to emerge in the void around Bai Donglin, exactly the same pattern on the body of the black prison.

Bai Donglin's heart moved, and when the time came, he quickly took the Omen of Death Gourd back into his body, and an inexplicable wave suddenly came, scanning Bai Donglin both inside and out, including the bracelet space, the original world of Death Omen could not escape. scanning.

In the original world of Omen of Death, the bronze pagoda was swept by waves. There are no living things inside. Except for the various experimental samples piled into the mountain, most of them are the monsters and ghosts captured by Zhang Shanren, and they have been dissected one by one. After the experiment, only some useful materials remain.

Bai Xiaoxiao, who was chasing and fighting with Xiao Zi in the drug garden, was focused on by the mysterious fluctuations. After scanning back and forth more than a dozen times, the fluctuations slowly withdrew from the world of the Omen of Death.

Fortunately, Bai Donglin released the souls of more than two million criminals, otherwise he would definitely not be able to pass the mysterious wave scan.

After the mysterious wave receded, in the next instant, countless mysterious and complicated strange patterns wrapped Bai Donglin, and the black light disappeared with a slight flash.

The outside world, above the black prison body.

But he promised Chi Yan, a good brother, to take care of Bai Donglin. If there is an accident at the last moment, his old face won't hold back.

However, this kid Bai Donglin was really honest in the past five years. He didn't find him once. He seemed to be a person who could bear loneliness, and he did not have the impetuous heart of a young man.

Bai Donglin looked around and saw Kuang Zhen standing outside the cave space. His figure appeared in front of Kuang Zhen as soon as he moved, and said slightly:

"Thank you, sir, for waiting here."

"Hahaha, boy, you are very good, I did not read you wrong, you can bear for five years without going out to cause trouble, and you are much calmer than the average young man!"

Bai Donglin: "..."

Kuang Zhen patted Bai Donglin on the shoulder, and his expression admired. Although the twenty-ninth floor of the Black Prison had a small number of power leaders in addition to dozens of city owners, the remaining ninety-nine percent were monks who had just entered the realm of supernatural powers.

But the prisoners were all murderous criminals. Even if Bai Donglin was cautious and didn't take the initiative to cause trouble, he might have trouble and come to him automatically.

In the past five years, Bai Donglin has not looked for him. Obviously, he has suffered a lot. He is also an arrogant man. He would rather forbear not look down to seek his help. Kuang Zhen felt that he had seen everything. .

"Thank you for your token, let the younger generation avoid a lot of trouble."

Bai Donglin took out a jade plaque from the bracelet and handed it to Kuang Zhen. Although he didn't use it, it could be regarded as Kuang Zhen's wish. He Bai Donglin accepted this love.

"Boy, let's get out of here first. There has been a big change in the black prison, and there will be many big people coming to deal with this matter. It is better to leave as soon as possible in order to avoid accidents."

Kuang Zhen took the jade card in his hand, and quickly left with Bai Donglin, passed numerous checkpoints, and returned to the stronghold of the Jidao Sacred Sect in the Black Prison City.

"Do you want to go back to the Holy Sect directly, or stay here for a few more days?"

With a move in Bai Donglin's heart, a faint light flashed in his eyes, and pictures flowed through his eyes, deliberately avoiding the powerful guards, focusing on the stronghold of the Holy Blood Sect.

With his spirit realm, just be careful, except for the power of the gods and demons, you are not afraid that someone will find him using magical powers to spy.

‘Sure enough, have Yundi and the boy surnamed Mu have left? ’

Bai Donglin, who searched for no results, could only hold down his mind for a while. He just wanted to try his luck and didn't care too much. Then he confessed to Kuang Zhen Weiwei:

"My lord, I've stayed enough for this place. I want to return to the Holy Sect to practice as soon as possible.

"Hahaha, that's right, this ghost place doesn't shit, and it's really nothing fun, so I will send you back to the sect."

He could understand Bai Donglin's thoughts, but he was imprisoned in the Absolute Black Prison at the most important time. At this time, he must be anxious to cultivate to make up for the lost cultivation time.

After speaking, Kuang Zhen took Bai Donglin into the cross-domain teleportation array. The teleportation array that could directly enter the Saint Sect was extremely rare. Bai Donglin remembered that the main city of Taishang did not have a direct teleportation array to the Saint Sect.

It is said that Shengzong suffered a big loss in this regard a long time ago, so many restrictions were placed on the teleportation array. In addition to reducing the number of teleportation arrays, the disciples of the holy sect with the extreme bracelet must be worn in order to activate the use of the direct saint. Zong’s teleportation array.

The bracelet on Bai Donglin's wrist flashed a little red light, and under the urging of Kuang Zhen, the cross-domain teleportation array was slowly activated, the law of space was diffused, and white light beams gushed out.

"Boy, goodbye."

"Thank you, sir!"

Bai Donglin offered his thanks slightly, and then was wrapped in a strange energy, and instantly disappeared into the beam of light.

Bai Donglin's sight resumed, and he looked around and found that he was not in the teleportation hall of the Infinite Arena, but in another larger teleportation formation.

With a movement of Bai Dong's figure, he exited the teleportation formation. Looking at the formation that was lit up from time to time, people came out of the teleportation formation from time to time. It must be a teleportation hub of the sect.

He shook his head and didn't think much, his mind moved slightly, and the communication bracelet marked the light gate leading to Zizhuju. Looking at the familiar light gate, Bai Donglin looked inexplicable in his eyes. It was a long absence.
