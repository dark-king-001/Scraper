Early the next morning.

Bai Donglin, who had slept on the recliner all night, slowly opened his eyes, stretched his waist, and thought of the communication bracelet.

He didn't have a clue about the "Law Seed", but he had an idea about the cultivation resources. He remembered a magical power he had seen in the stele world, which should greatly alleviate his need for energy.

Raising his hand to draw out the light gate, he stepped out and came to the mysterious stele world.

The powerful light of the source rose up, and instantly locked that magical power, while the figure flickered, at his current speed, it only took a cup of tea time to arrive in front of a heritage stone monument.

The stone stele is completely black, like a deep black hole, the surrounding space is slightly distorted, and all the nearby light is absorbed and swallowed by the stone stele, making the place appear pitch black.

With Bai Donglin's thoughts moving, the bracelet radiated red light, reaching out to break through a strange force field, and touched on the inheritance stele.

The contribution points were deducted by 100,000, and Bai Donglin felt a little bit painful. It really is the top inheritance, and the price is directly capped!

Cross-legged and concentrating, the pitch-black and deep characters were beating, digging into the soul and imprinting on the soul.

Supernatural powers-devour the sky and the earth!

This is a supernatural power that combines space and the law of swallowing. It has the powerful ability to swallow all things and turn them into energy to nourish one's body.

Such a powerful magical power can be used as a plug-in for the protagonist in some new mobile phones, but only one hundred thousand points are needed in this monument world. Everyone can learn it. It can be said that it is a good value for money!

After a long time, when the inheritance was completed, Bai Donglin continued to stick his palms on the stone tablet, deducting points and began to use the assistance of the inheritance stone tablet for the first practice.

His comprehension is now extremely powerful, and coupled with the extremely high level of the evil spirits of this divine power, it is like a fish in water during cultivation.

Three days later, Bai Donglin had already started cultivation, slowly opened his eyes, his eyes were like two deep black holes, swallowing all the surrounding light.

Sensing the strengthening of a lot of the laws of space and the seed of the law of swallowing appearing in a spiritual aperture, Bai Donglin nodded in satisfaction, yes, buy one get one free, which is equivalent to getting a seed of the law in vain.

The Spirit Aperture of the Law of Swallowing is located in the stomach, and Bai Donglin can clearly sense that his stomach has become a small black hole, rotating slightly, as if it can swallow everything.

What should I do if I don’t have training resources in the future? Then eat! What to do if there is no energy? Still eat!

Eat a Tongtian Avenue!

Bai Donglin looked solemnly, let him come and reverberate the name of Huaxia Foodie through the world!

Suppressing the enthusiasm in his heart, Bai Donglin hurriedly withdrew from the stele world. He still has an important experiment to do, and there are not many points that can be wasted. Other supernatural powers.

Back at Zizhuju, Bai Donglin looked a little nervous. If that idea works, he will never lack energy again!

Raising his hand to condense energy, the scarlet shattered sword intent flickered, and he cut down his left arm cleanly, and with a movement of his mind, his mouth opened like a black hole. The arm on the ground shrank quickly and was swallowed into the black hole in the stomach.

His experiment is, I eat myself, if it succeeds, it will be unlimited energy!

Bai Dong sat cross-legged, carefully sensing the changes in the black hole in his stomach, and the arm he swallowed during his breath was completely digested.

The brows were frowned, but the energy that should have appeared did not appear, the black hole in the stomach vibrated slightly, and there were signs of collapse!

How could this be? Bai Donglin took a piece of spirit stone from the bracelet and threw it into his mouth. The black hole swallowed the spirit stone, and a stream of pure energy spewed out instantly.

There is no problem. The problem is not with supernatural powers. Bai Donglin cut off the arm that grew out again, and continued to swallow it into his abdomen, feeling carefully.

The black hole oscillated and still had no energy ejected, and continued to cut off his arms and legs and swallowed it into the abdomen. This time it finally changed. I saw that the black hole began to oscillate violently, and then it collapsed directly along with the spirit orifice.

After a while, the immortality was triggered, and the Ling Aperture and the black hole resumed the same.

Failed!

Bai Donglin's face became ugly, the Infinite Energy Plan, also known as the plan of eating and waiting for death, fell through. After thinking slightly for a moment, he figured out why this happened.

To eat yourself, I am afraid that the creator of this magical power has never thought of it, and naturally this strange situation has not been mentioned in the inheritance.

The Spirit Aperture where the swallowing black hole is located, and his own arms and legs, are both born with the same source of light, with the same root, and containing the same source of life.

If it is swallowed, it will be impacted by the same source of light on the spirit orifice, and it is indigestible, and there is a danger that the spirit orifice will collapse.

A look of disappointment flashed in Bai Donglin's eyes, it was that he was too greedy, and this can't be blamed on him. It was really that he needed too much energy. If it could be done once and for all, how much work would have to be saved.

There was another thought flashing in his mind, and he shook his head and rejected himself. He thought that he could catch a few tool people, let them practice, devour the sky and the earth, devour his flesh and blood, and then give him the energy back. It's a pity Let alone the difficulty of implementation.

This magical power also has a limitation. The energy gushing out of the swallowing spirit orifice will be contaminated with its own original aura. This energy can only be used to enhance the spirit orifice of the same root and the same source, and it is useless for others.

In this way, Bai Donglin also put out the idea of ​​drilling loopholes, but this magical power still holds a high position in his heart. Although he can't achieve unlimited energy, it can greatly alleviate his demand for cultivation resources.

When Bai Donglin's thoughts moved, he swallowed the sky and turned around automatically, and the aura of heaven and earth was sucked into the black hole, and then transformed into pure energy, which contained the spiritual orifice.

This was the first time in so many years that he took the initiative to absorb the aura of heaven and earth, and the effect of the black hole swallowing was much better than the effect of his meditation practice.

And don't have to worry about him, the black hole in the stomach will operate by itself to absorb the aura of heaven and earth, continue day and night, and the accumulated energy is also considerable.

Bai Donglin's mind moved, and many materials containing spiritual energy in the bracelet were taken out. These "garbage" are useless to keep. Most of them are the flesh and blood of some monsters, materials shaved from monsters, ghosts and gods, and so on.

He opened his mouth and swallowed all the materials until the black hole in the stomach faintly reached the upper limit before stopping.

Sensing a stream of pure energy being gushing out, and the thirsty spirit orifice swallowed with big mouths, Bai Donglin nodded in satisfaction, so that there will be no waste in the future, squeezing the opponent's surplus value. Do it, and then devour its body, it's perfect!

But thinking about cannibalism, it’s still a bit disgusting. Unlike monsters and monsters, it’s better to refine it before eating, so as not to be regarded as a pervert. Bai Donglin nodded solemnly. He still pays attention to his own image. He is different from evil spirits.

Thoughts swept across the bracelet space. At the beginning, I thought it could use decades of cultivation resources. Now there are only a half of them left~www.mtlnovel.com~ There are too many hidden spirit orifices opened up, and the potential of strength is naturally strong, but the consumption of resources is even greater. It's horror.

Looking at the more than one million magic weapons floating in the space, most of these trophies from the ancient world were magic weapons, and a small part were low-level magic weapons, except for a few top-quality magic weapons that were useful to him.

The remaining magic weapons are completely useless to him. They are piled up in the bracelet space like rubbish. These things are treated at a low price, but they can be exchanged for some cultivation resources.

The idea communication bracelet, drew out the light gate, and Bai Donglin stepped over and came to the huge public area of ​​Shengzong.

Those who can see these low-level magic weapons are only the new disciples, but the number of new disciples in the holy sect is only so many, let him just set up a stall on the street and don't know how much he can sell, it is a waste of time.

So Bai Donglin went directly to the Lianbao Pavilion, the largest trading center, and prepared to deal with it all at a low price.
