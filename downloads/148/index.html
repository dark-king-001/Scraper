The next day.

Bai Donglin opened the light gate through the map in the bracelet and came under Duobao Peak.

Looking up, the huge mountain peaks linger with light, the mountain peaks are deep above the tens of thousands of feet of clouds, and there are powerful formations and restrictions on the whole body.

Before coming to the mountain gate, Bai Donglin raised his left arm, the bracelet flashed red, and a golden invitation letter floated out automatically. Duobaofeng’s formation restriction was touched, and a wave of fluctuations enveloped Bai Donglin. The space was slightly distorted and his figure disappeared instantly.

When Bai Donglin regained his sight again, he had already come to a huge hall, and found that the hall was full of special restrictions. He could not use his spiritual thoughts, heaven, sight, and earth. It must be to protect everyone. Privacy.

Bai Donglin complied with kindness and could only use his eyes to start to look around. The entrance to the hall was pitch black. According to the explanation in the invitation letter, except for the disciples who belonged to Duobaofeng, everyone else could only stay in this hall for trading, and could not walk out of the hall and enter. Duobaofeng wandered around.

He didn't care about it either. He was here to participate in the trade fair and was not interested in other places in Duobaofeng.

At this time, there were quite a few people in the hall, roughly estimated to be no less than a thousand people. After careful sensing, all of them are not weak, most of them are in the second and third heavens of the supernatural power realm, and there are many from the fourth and fifth heavens.

In terms of surface strength, he should be the one with the lowest strength here. The Nine Miss seems to know his situation very well and specially invited him to come, thinking of coming to give him the opportunity to buy the "Law Seed"!

With that said, he hasn't even seen the other person's face yet, so he owes two favors?

But with this little grace, Xiaohui wanted to impress his heart of stone, but it was impossible!

Depressing his mind, Bai Donglin began to wander around in this huge hall.

There are neat rows of stalls built around the main hall and in the center. The stalls are not big but also very delicate. Disciple who comes to the trade fair can use it at will, which is much better than just setting up a stall on the ground.

As more and more people began to enter the hall, more and more disciples took out various treasures to set up stalls, a wave of law fluctuations rose from some stalls, which was clearly sensed by Bai Donglin. The seed of the law"!

As soon as his figure moved, Bai Donglin appeared in front of a booth, ignoring some weird things on the booth, and staring at a crystal ball.

This crystal ball is made of a kind of crystal called "emptiness", and it is specially used to make a container that carries the "seed of the law".

There was a little green light in the empty crystal ball, exuding the aura of the law of trees.

Bai Donglin nodded slightly. Sure enough, as he expected, laws like the Five Elements Rule are very common and normal to be sold. He glanced at the stall owner who was sitting cross-legged in the void. He was still a beautiful female disciple, Bai Donglin asked:

"Hello Sister, how do you sell this Law of Wood?"

"I only accept points transactions, 10,000 points."

The woman carefully looked at Bai Donglin. Although this person called her senior sister, she could not tell the depth of her cultivation. She thought it was a special hobby, but it was not good for the lion to speak out, but to say A normal price.

Under Bai Donglin's body surface, in order to conceal his ongoing cultivation, various secret patterns were inscribed, and all auras were blocked. Naturally, this female disciple of the supernatural power realm could not see anything.

Bai Donglin pondered slightly, although the Five Elements Rule is the most common rule, there are only some special places to get the seed of the rule, and ten thousand is not too expensive.

"Deal, I want this rule of wood!"

Seeing that Bai Donglin is so happy, the woman also looks happy. The disciples participating in this kind of trade fair are not low-strength. Such ordinary rules have long been engraved into the spirit, but I did not expect to sell it so easily. , I think this weird guy bought it for my younger brother and younger sister.

The bracelets of the two touched, the money and goods were cleared, Bai Donglin carefully put away the crystal ball, and went to the next stall as soon as his figure moved.

"Brother, how do you sell the Law of the Earth?"

"Ten thousand points, no second price."

"make a deal!"

It’s so easy. It took 20,000 points to get the Five Elements Rule. Bai Donglin nodded in satisfaction. Other slightly rare rules are not as common as they are, and the price is not so beautiful. This trade fair has just begun. , As long as they can meet them, he will still buy them if the price is not excessive.

The laws of heaven and earth are intertwined with principles and principles. All things in heaven and earth have their own truths. They all contain the power of laws and are the essence of everything in heaven and earth. To perceive and engrave the laws is to perceive the great road of heaven and earth!

The seed of the law is just like its name. It is a seed, planted in the spirit orifice, you can easily feel the power of the law, and use the power of the law to water the seed day and night. The future One day it will grow into a towering tree!

Planting trees is planting Tao, planting is the avenue of law of heaven and earth!

Nurturing one's own Tao with the Great Dao of Heaven and Earth, and achieving one's own "dao fruit", so that you can step into the realm of Hunyuan Wuji!

It was noon, and the fair in the hall had reached its busiest time. Many stalls were almost full. Bai Donglin was in a good mood. Walking around among the crowd, he bought a lot of interesting things.

At one booth, a pitch-black bead attracted Bai Donglin's attention, and it exuded a weird and insidious atmosphere, corroding the surrounding air.

"Old man, what treasure is this bead?"

It's not that Bai Donglin said that this stall owner is indeed an old man with white beard and hair. The monk has a long life span and his appearance is difficult to define, but most people will maintain their most perfect, youngest and peak physical appearance.

Of course, there are special circumstances. Some people stepped into the world of cultivation relatively late. Perhaps a seven-year-old, eighty-year-old, by chance, stepped into the cultivation practice. He got used to his appearance and was too lazy to reshape his body.

Some looked like a child, maybe it was an old monster who had lived for a million years, so it's hard to say this kind of thing. Bai Donglin didn't bother to care about it, and it was done by judging people by appearance!

"Haha, my little brother has good eyesight. This is the'Wan'e Poison Pearl'. It is made from the inner alchemy of a ten thousand poisonous scorpion. It is made by a master craftsman who is proficient in the law of poison and supplemented by various precious materials. It is a rare high-grade spiritual weapon!"

Bai Donglin's heart moved, this is a good thing to replace the Thunder and Fire double beads. If he keeps using the same attributes to brush energy, his body will slowly develop resistance and the effect will get worse and worse, so it is better to find something to replace. Treasures with different attack attributes.

"Can I touch the old man?"

"Please!"

Bai Donglin asked for permission, picked up the Wan'e Poison Pearl with his bare hands, closed his eyes and focused on it, and even absorbed a trace of its overflowing breath into his body.

After a while, Bai Donglin opened his eyes and flashed a hint of joy. This poison bead was better than he thought. He was mainly afraid that its toxicity would be too single, so that his body would easily develop resistance.

Unexpectedly, this small poison bead has tens of thousands of different toxicities, and it can produce different toxicities when combined with each other. It is a good treasure. These tens of thousands of toxicities should be the inherent characteristics of the Ten Thousand Poison Flood Inner Pill.

"Old man, I like you baby very much, I don't know how you want to sell it?"

Unexpectedly, the old man with white beard and hair shook his head slightly, with a trace of desire in his eyes, and said firmly:

"The old man does not sell this thing, the old man has to barter!"

Bai Donglin was taken aback for a moment. There was indeed this rule in this trade fair. It was possible to barter, and it was up to the seller to decide what transaction method to use.

"I wonder what do you need for the elderly?"

Bai Donglin really likes this poison pearl. If there is no way to satisfy the old man, he can only give up. He still can't do this kind of grabbing. The main reason is that people have no grievances with him, and I am embarrassed to do it!

"The old man needs a treasure to increase his life, at least two hundred years!"

Bai Donglin's expression hesitated slightly when he heard the words, the old man saw that his hand on the beard was slightly stagnant, and he was making a head!

He immediately waved his hand to put away the many things on the booth, suppressing the excitement in his heart, and whispered to Bai Donglin:

"Little brother, please come with me!"

Bai Donglin nodded slightly, and followed the old man to a side of the bronze wall. The old man pinched Faju with one hand, the wall shook slightly, and then opened a door. Inside was a small room.

This is a secret room that Duobaofeng prepares for disciples who need to conduct some private transactions~www.mtlnovel.com~ You can use it at will if you need it. There is a forbidden formation inside to isolate it from prying eyes.

The two walked into the secret room one after the other, the bronze door closed suddenly, and the old man rubbed his hands and asked excitedly:

"Little brother, the old man hesitated to see you just now. I don't know if you have a treasure to increase your lifespan? Or do you have relevant news?"

"Only little brother, you can pay for treasures or information of equal value. This Wan'e Poison Pearl is yours!"

How precious and rare are the treasures that increase lifespan?

The old man couldn't be sure that Bai Donglin really had such a thing, but he wanted to grasp it as long as there was hope, even if it was just related information.

Coincidentally, Bai Donglin really has it!
