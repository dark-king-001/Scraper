"Hey, what a pity, what a pity!"

The decision made by this kind of person is impossible for others to change, even if it is to smash his body and soul, it is irreversible to change his decision.

This kind of enchanting genius, but not attentive to the alchemy, is really a great loss to the alchemy world!

Shang Zhong restrained his disappointment, cheered up, and continued:

"Donglin, since you are determined, the old man will not persuade you too much, but the old man still hopes that you will not waste the elixir while you are cultivating. After all, the pill can also help you in your cultivation!"

Bai Donglin nodded slightly. This is not bad. The refined poison pill is very helpful for him to increase the energy. Compared with the use of resources to activate various spiritual weapons, the poison pill is obviously much more cost-effective.

"The deacon's words are extremely true, the juniors should keep it in mind!"

Shang Zhong nodded in satisfaction. As long as Bai Donglin doesn't completely give up alchemy, he will one day contribute to the alchemy world. Then he thought of the business and said with a serious expression:

"East, have you heard of the Dandao Conference held by Danyu?"

"Junior don't know."

The knowledge that Bai Donglin acquired in Shushan, except for a small part of the knowledge that was deliberately understood and retrieved, was randomly branded. Therefore, without a specific goal, he learned very mixed things and would be branded. What knowledge is entirely based on luck.

It is naturally impossible to be omniscient about many things. For example, at this alchemy conference, unless one day he branded the entire mountain of books into his mind, then it would not be much worse if he didn’t say omniscience.

It is not surprising to see that Bai Donglin didn't know the alchemy meeting Shangzhong. He knew that Bai Donglin came from a remote area, and it was normal for him not to understand a lot of things. He drank a sip of spiritual tea to moisturize his throat, and continued:

"This alchemy conference has a long history. Since the existence of the pill domain, it has not stopped. It can be said to be a feast for the pill domain, or even the entire alchemy world!"

"At that time, as low as alchemy apprentices and as high as alchemy saints, they will all participate in this grand gathering and compete with opponents of the same level to exchange alchemy techniques."

"Donglin, would you like to represent our ultimate saint sect to participate in this alchemy conference?"

Bai Donglin frowned slightly. He has always had his own goal in doing things. It is to avoid seeing rabbits and spreading eagles. If this alchemy conference is not good for him, he will just pretend to be a saint. He has no pretending system, but No such idle time!

"My lord deacon, the juniors are low-powered, so they don't dare to shoulder this important task, and the juniors have wasted a lot of time in the black prison. Now they just want to practice hard and improve their strength."

"Don't be too busy to refuse, you go to the conference and only have to face alchemists of the same realm. I believe that your alchemy strength is definitely one of the best in the supernatural power realm, so you don't need to consider your strength."

"As for you to cultivate, the most difficult thing in the supernatural power realm is to perceive and inscribe the laws, especially those rare laws."

"The rewards of the Alchemy Conference, there are many rare seeds of the law!"

Bai Donglin's expression moved slightly. You said that I won't be sleepy anymore, why didn't you say it earlier? !

The color of thought flashed in his eyes, it is impossible for him to worship Dandingfeng, he will not worship any major peak, can anyone in the whole Saint Sect guide his "Pregnant God Casting Demon Scripture"?

no! He can only rely on himself!

In this case, it is only for the purpose of opening the authority to worship the main peak, and the benefits obtained do not seem to be cost-effective compared with the obligations and restrictions that need to be undertaken.

He already had his own plan in his heart, but he didn't need to consider the matter of worshipping the main peak for the time being before it was implemented.

As for participating in this alchemy meeting, I really felt a little moved. It didn’t take much time to participate in a competition, and it wouldn’t delay his practice anyway. If he could get one or two rare seeds of the law, it would be a good deal. .

He wants to fight for every rare seed of the law as much as possible. Although he is different from Zhang Hai, he is immortal. There is no limit of lifespan, and he can spend a lot of time slowly, but if there is a shortcut, There is no reason not to leave!

Thinking of this, Bai Donglin already had a plan in his heart, and said to Shang Zhong:

"My lord deacon, please allow me to consider the matter of the alchemy meeting. If the younger generation can get away by then, it is not impossible to participate."

"Hahaha, well, the old man believes that as long as you participate, you will definitely earn face for the holy sect!"

Seeing that Bai Donglin was moved by himself, Shang Zhong smiled and laughed. Then the two discussed in detail the related matters of the Dandao Conference. Shang Zhong also established contact with Bai Donglin Bracelet, which is convenient for future contact. .

After a long time, the two people withdrew from the main hall and returned to the square. Bai Donglin estimated that the competition in other venues should be over soon.

Sitting cross-legged on the square, after waiting for more than half an hour, the bracelet on the left wrist began to flash red, a slight movement in my heart, the secret path finally came!

The light gates automatically opened in front of a hundred disciples, and all of them stood up and looked at each other, and they all stepped into the light gate with excitement.

Above the boundary sea.

A piece of black jade continent suspended in the air, the huge floating continent is a whole piece of crystal clear black jade, which is engraved with complicated inscriptions and various patterns, gorgeous and mysterious.

A series of light gates appeared out of thin air on the Black Jade Continent, and Bai Donglin stepped out of the light gate, his eyes flowed with fireflies, and the surrounding environment had a panoramic view.

There are a total of two thousand light gates in the void, and it is precisely the disciples who have obtained quotas through various competitions this time. Among them, one thousand disciples who have obtained quotas through combat stand together, and their aura is much stronger than the other thousand disciples.

There is still a strong fighting spirit in his body, and these disciples who are confident to participate in the fighting are not weak, and the disciples selected through repeated trials are the elites of the elite.

The two thousand disciples are all here, but there is still no movement on the Black Jade Continent, as if waiting for something. Although these disciples are anxious, they dare not show it. This is the Jiehai, one of the core places of the Holy Sect. It's not a place where they can be presumptuous, they can only wait patiently.

After another tea time, light gates began to appear in the void in front of the crowd, one after another, a full three thousand.

Bai Donglin, including many of his disciples, was taken aback, what's the situation? Isn’t there only two thousand places?

Without waiting for them to think, a group of figures crossed the light gate and descended on the Black Jade Continent. Everyone, including Bai Donglin, changed their expressions, and a terrifying killing intent rushed toward them. Except for a few disciples, the rest People back and forth again and again, their faces pale.

Who are these people?

Bai Donglin opened the eyes of the soul, and saw that in the void ahead, countless killing intents gathered, entangled and condensed into a ferocious beast with a height of ten thousand feet, opening its teeth and dancing claws, wanting to choose people and eat it!

What a strong killing intent, what a pure war intent, Bai Donglin's heart moved, is it possible that Shengzong summoned those people back?

In such a short period of time, it will inevitably cost a lot to recall the disciples from the many battlefields in the only real world. Since this is done, it seems that this broken world is very unusual, at least different from the past!

Three thousand horrifying disciples glanced at Bai Donglin and others and did not pay too much attention to them. They had tried their best to curb their killing intent, but the slightly spilled killing intent was still condensed into a killing vision, to others. It is not intentional to cause deterrence.

Bai Donglin squinted his eyes slightly, and there are countless lives on these people's hands!

Unlike the extremely evil criminals in the black prison, although the innocent lives on the hands of those criminals are no less than a million people, they are almost all **** slaughtering mortals!

As for these disciples in front of them, the lives that they killed were all powerful men with strong strength. These were two concepts. Bai Donglin could clearly sense the killing intent and fighting intent among them.

The two men and horses did not disturb each other. After waiting for another moment in this weird atmosphere, the colorful clouds above the Black Jade Continent began to roll, turning into a huge human face in a blink of an eye, covering the sky.

Yun's giant face had a pair of exaggerated eyes, staring at the five thousand saint sect disciples below, his voice was like thunder, and he slowly said:

"I am the lord of the sea, please listen carefully."

"The broken world captured by Shengzong this time is extremely special. When the ancestors deprived the core of its original law, he observed traces of the existence of the legendary ‘soldier’!"

"It is very likely that the inheritance of the'Military Family' has been left between the broken world~www.mtlnovel.com~ The broken world has a limited carrying capacity, so Pyle and other supernatural power realm disciples can only go to explore."

Yun's huge face paused for a while after speaking, his huge eyes moved slightly, staring at the powerful three thousand disciples, and continued:

"The people who inherited the'Military Family' and battled on the battlefield have the greatest affinity. This is also the reason why Shengzong Jianger and others have been recalled from many battlefields."

"Needless to say the importance of the battle formation of the'Military Family' to the battlefield of our human race, you and others are all outstanding among the disciples of the supernatural power realm, and you should be very clear about this aspect."

"Go, bring back the inheritance of the'Military Family'!"

The voice of the Lord of the Boundary Sea was like a thunder explosion, rolling in.

The disciples' scalp numb!
