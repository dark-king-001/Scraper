This is a technique that Bai Donglin has understood from the magical powers swallowing the sky and the earth, possessing the ability to shrink objects.

The skeletal palm also shrank at the same time, and Bai Donglin held a marble-sized ball in front of him, carefully observing the Earth Vein Yanling in it.

Sensing the terrifying energy contained in this trace of purple aura, he nodded in satisfaction, not wasting his patience for so long.

Although this Earth Vessel Yanling was trapped by him, it is still unstable. If it senses a mortal crisis, it will explode the energy core in the body to engulf purple energy.

Although he was not afraid of being blown to death, the Earth Vein Yanling blew himself up and his energy was poured out. He was in vain.

The "soul manipulator" took the most evil shot, but instantly controlled the spiritual sense of the earth vein Yanling!

Because the rich purple qi in its body still needs its spiritual consciousness to maintain, it can't be erased roughly, but can only be controlled.

As soon as his mind moved, the rune sphere was opened, and Bai Dong was facing the open mouth of the bones, and the black hole formed by the swallowing spirit aperture rotated slightly, and the two earth veins Yanling were swallowed into the black hole in an instant.

The spirit orifice where the black hole was located flickered and gradually stabilized. The energy contained in the flames of the earth veins was too terrifying, and the current swallowing sky and the earth had reached the limit of its load.

Swallowing the sky and the earth is a very high-level magical power, and the law of devouring is also a powerful law of peerless level. Without the seed of the law, Bai Donglin's perception progress is very slow. He wants to incorporate it into the light of the source and turn it into his own magical powers. Indefinitely.

Therefore, at present, this magical power can only be activated with blood elements, and its power is limited. The two earth veins are only temporarily stored in the black hole space after being swallowed.

You can only digest it a little bit slowly, and if you digest too much at one time, the energy gushing out is too large, and Bai Donglin can't absorb all of it, which will cause a huge waste. Now this is just right.

After doing all this, Bai Donglin held the rune ball on the palm rest of his bones. With a movement of his mind, pieces of materials were taken out, and he began to repair the damage of the formation.

The most important thing to capture Earth Mai Yan Ling is to lure it out, and to cut off the possibility of it escaping into the law.

Otherwise, with the agility and cunning of Dimai Yanling, as long as he escaped into the law, Bai Donglin would have no choice.
