The sixty-ninth floor of Tongtian Tower.

Bai Donglin looked at the four-winged angel floating in the void in front of him, with curiosity in his eyes.

This was the first time he saw this legendary creature, the bright and sacred angel, who was famous at the time of the Blue Star.

The angel in front of him was the same as in the legend, with white wings on his back, slightly glowing with white light, and a little bit of starlight falling from his feathers.

A pair of deep facial features, blue pupils, golden curly long hair, slightly raised breasts, a white armor covered with gorgeous lines, the whole body is covered with golden light, full of sacred breath.

Bai Donglin's eyes narrowed slightly. It is reasonable to say that such creatures as angels should belong to the bright camp, but he did not expect that they would also be placed in the Tongtian Tower. This shows that Qianyuanjie's camp is opposite to it.

"Hehe is interesting. It seems that the devil and the angel have joined forces. What kind of war is this that can actually bring two naturally opposed creatures together?"

He shook his head and condensed his thoughts, looked at the angel who condensed a sacred lightsaber, and continued:

"Sure enough, just like the legend, every angel is very beautiful. It is said that angels have no gender, and I don't know if it is true."

Let me dissect it and take a look!

Bai Donglin's eyes were cold and severe, and he was really curious about this kind of creature that he heard from when he was a child.

One step out, it instantly appeared in the void, with one hand sticking out, it rose up against the wind, and the huge palm was surrounded by countless laws and chains. Wherever it went, the space was crushed by the terrifying force.

The four-winged angel's eyes shone with white light, and the white wings flapped slightly, trying to avoid this terrifying blow.

As soon as the figure moved, a terrifying aura shrouded, and the four-winged angel was directly imprisoned in place, unable to move.

In the next moment, he was firmly grasped by the huge palm, and the law chain was tightly wound around it. All energy fluctuations in his body were frozen, and he could not even explode.

"Oh, don't move, let me see how your essence is made up."

As soon as the mind moved, the law of destruction turned into countless scarlet blades as thin as cicada wings, extremely sharp, and the faintly leaked blades cut through the space.

The scarlet blade revolves rapidly around the four-winged angel, looking from a distance, as if countless red rays of light envelop it.

The next moment, the white armor covering the whole body of the four-winged angel turned into shavings and dissipated, and the naked white body was exposed to the air.

Bai Donglin's eyes did not fluctuate, and he glanced up and down, the scarlet blade continued to rotate, and the skin, flesh and blood, energy operation system, bones, and layers were peeled off.

Bai Dong's divine mind pouring out like a deep sea like a sea, shrouded the broken angel after decomposition, the extremely powerful divine soul, and carefully observed the angel at the cell particle level.

After a long time, Divine Sense retreated, and Bai Donglin's eyes flashed with a bright light.

"Sure enough, there is no gender, or that this low-level angel has no gender, not only that, not even a soul."

"This four-winged angel is like a special creation, and its energy operation system can explain all this, and the strength is fixed from the moment it is created."

"In other words, this is just mass production of cannon fodder."

Slightly shook his head and felt a little disappointed. This is not a real angel, and it has no research value. When a thought moves, the red light bursts, and the decomposed four-winged angel instantly turns into nothingness and disappears.

The space was rippling slightly, and Bai Donglin was teleported to the seventieth floor and re-ranked to the top of the new star list. Shen Wuqian was surpassed again by him.

It's a pity that he can only get the chance to pick magical powers for free for the first time he has reached the top of the list for a month. He has already enjoyed this benefit.

On the 70th floor of the Tongtian Tower, the strength of the tower guards is almost the same as that of the regular monks in the supernatural power realm who incorporates 20 magical orifices.

According to estimates, the strength of the tower guards on each floor below will increase by the strength of five spirit orifices.

However, these tower guards are incomparable with the Saint Sect disciples of the same level. After all, it doesn't depend on whoever has the more spiritual apertures in the original source that the strength is stronger. The Saint Sect disciples are geniuses selected by a billion, and they are better than regular monks. too much.

And Bai Donglin is even more of a different kind, a peerless wonderful flower amongst wonderful flowers!

The more the monk reaches the later stage, the more complicated the judgment of his strength becomes, and the more factors need to be considered.

The five main reference factors are the physical body, the spiritual orifice, the law, the soul, and the use of the magical secret arts.

Whether the physical body is strong or not, excluding some special physiques, the main consideration is the development of the spiritual orifice, as well as the grade and type of the inscription of the spiritual orifice.

Bai Donglin now only included ten spirit orifices to enter the source of light, and the law level was quite high, and there were several peerless-level branch laws.

The laws of power, soul, destruction, and space are all important branches of the peerless laws. These laws have just begun to accumulate, and there is still a lot of room for growth!

For example, force now only involves physical power, and space only involves teleportation, and it is still far away for the destruction to grow into a peerless law of destruction.

In the future, we only need to continue to understand the laws of fusion of the same system, and it will naturally become a peerless law, or even an unparalleled law!

However, the step of law fusion will not start until the Divine Origin Realm, such as the Golden, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth. This seems to be a common law. When in the Divine Origin Realm, it can be merged into a powerful Five Elements Rule!

So when you are in the magical realm, you should inscribe different kinds of laws as much as possible. It is not only related to whether you can break through to the magical realm.

It is also related to whether there are more laws to be used as materials for fusion when you are in the source of the gods!

Many monks are thinking about the Divine Source Realm when they are in the Divine Passage Realm. For example, the poisonous cultivator is taking the Poison Avenue, so he has already begun to consciously collect poison-related laws.

When it comes to the Divine Origin Realm, the laws of the same system will be merged into one, and the strength will skyrocket!

These are all common sense of cultivation, and Bai Donglin knows it naturally, but the laws he has engraved now are still too few. It will take at least a hundred before the general direction will appear, and it will not be too late to start thinking about it.

From these we can know that the reason why Bai Donglin's current strength is strong has nothing to do with the law engraved in the Ling Aperture!

There are two powerful places in Bai Donglin. One is the Divine Soul. This is a Divine Soul comparable to the Seven Heavens of Divine Origin, which is piled up by massive strengthening energy, plus the magical "Trinity" method in the "Pregnancy God Casting Demon Scripture" , There are many armed forces of knowledge.

The second is the physical body. The power of the physical body can also be said to be the power of the spiritual orifice. After all, the essence of the physical body is the fusion and superposition of the spiritual orifice and the original light.

And Bai Donglin's hidden spiritual orifices have been opened to more than eight thousand, and all the spiritual orifices are still infinite space, coupled with the accumulation of countless cultivation resources, the endowment of physical power is already very terrifying!

The magical powers of the natal law are all motivated by the physical body, that is, driven by the origin of life, the stronger the physical body, the stronger the power of the magical powers.

Therefore, Bai Donglin's strength lies in his body and soul, and his magical powers, laws and various secret arts can only be regarded as auxiliary means.

The power of the law may have to wait until it reaches the Divine Source Realm before it can catch up with the status of the Divine Soul and the Body.

Of course, everything mentioned here is just conventional combat power, a normal method, without considering the many moves developed based on immortality.

For example, "Extreme Sublimation" and the "Original" sequence of the secret killer move. These are already extra-level things. If they are mixed up, it is even harder to estimate his strength.

Bai Donglin silently estimated his own strength in his heart. Without using Divine Soul and those extravagant moves, he should be on par with the three thousand elite disciples who had returned from the battlefield.

And this time, Li Wei didn't need to achieve that level. It was enough to expose the strength of those disciples who sneered.

A newly promoted disciple, or a disciple who hasn't worshipped the main peak, can achieve this level, enough to shock everyone.

Bai Donglin raised his eyes to look at the tower guard in front of him. He was a fat man with a green oil of two feet tall, covered with fist-sized bumps. He had seen many such strange creatures all the way, and there was no wave in his eyes.
