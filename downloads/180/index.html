Bai Donglin had been burned by the continuous attacks of the Balrog, leaving only a huge skeleton.

"Big man! Don't stop, I'm really dying, work harder, believe in yourself, you can do it!"

With his arms propped on his thighs, the flame demon stared at Bai Donglin with scarlet eyes, panting fiercely, and each breath of gas spit out, carrying a lot of sparks.

Damn it!

What kind of monster is this, why can't it be killed?

The flame demon is now very suspicious of the demon. He feels that his common sense has been subverted. It is just a human being. Why does such a weak race have such a terrifying recovery ability, even if it is physical training?

The tower is unpredictable, these tower guards are forcibly detained here, still maintaining all the memories and consciousness.

It's just that every time the challenge is over, everything will be reset.

Therefore, the basic common sense of the Balrog is still there, and there are not many human monks who have come into contact with it, and he can also see that the human being in front of him is revealing anomalies everywhere.

This is not a normal human!

Bai Donglin continued to sneer: "What's the matter? That's it? Isn't the flame demon all manic temperament, bloodthirsty crazy, I think you are like a good baby."

"Human! Don't underestimate this demon!"

The flame demon roared in a low voice, his crimson eyes were full of madness, and he stepped out and approached Bai Donglin in an instant. He opened his sturdy arms and hugged Bai Donglin, who had recovered a lot of flesh and blood.

"Morgos Bauglier is watching me!"

"Burning blood!"

The flame demon looked enthusiastic, and then the energy of his whole body gathered in the energy core of the body, a wave of fluctuations flashed, and a fierce explosion!

boom!

Terrifying explosions swept in all directions, the fragile small world space was fragmented, the endless magma was turned into nothingness, and the fierce shock wave engulfed the magma and shot into the distance.

A huge skeleton stood in the center of the explosion. The flesh and blood that had just been restored was burned into nothingness again. The large skeleton was faintly golden light, and hardly connected to the terrifying explosion without a trace of cracks.

After a while, Bai Donglin returned to its original state, the law of heaven was lifted, and his height returned to 1.85 meters. He smashed his mouth and said:

"These demons are really a muscle. Is there nothing else in your mind other than self-destruction?"

The Tower of Communicating Ideas did not continue to the 100th floor. The ninety-nine floor was enough to shock the world. It would be too much publicity if it continued. It is better to stop.

This Tongtian Tower reward is a one-off anyway, and it won’t be too late to come back later.

The surrounding space was rippling slightly, and Bai Dong's figure flashed, and he was teleported to the zero-floor hall by the Tongtian Pagoda.

Bai Donglin had just appeared in the hall, and everyone cast their gazes. The entire hall was silent for hundreds of thousands of disciples.

The eyes of the new disciples are full of awe, and the eyes of the elite disciples are also shocking.

In the past, a large group of people had gathered around to talk. Although Bai Donglin was the same as before, with a gentle face and a smile, there was no one around him this time.

People gather in groups and people are divided into groups. Monsters like Bai Donglin can no longer have the idea of ​​coming up to talk to them.

But now the facts before him, Bai Donglin's strength is no longer at the same level as the new disciple, and it is not a creature of the same dimension!

Shengqing and other acquaintances were not here either, but it seemed that he was a little alone. Bai Donglin shook his head slightly and didn't care. When he was about to open the light door and leave, a flashing figure appeared in front of him.

Miss Jiu, who was hesitant in the distance, was slightly taken aback when she saw this, and stopped the steps she had just wanted to take. She guessed that Bai Donglin was a powerful reincarnation, so she did not dare to step forward, hesitated for a moment but was taken first by others.

"Brother Bai, hello!"

"I wonder if you are brother?"

Bai Donglin's eyes showed doubts, the man in front of him looked soft and polite, and his body was faintly exuding the fragrance of pill.

The man did not explain, but took out a piece of jade pendant and showed it to Bai Donglin.

Bai Donglin saw the light flashing in Yu Pei's eyes, nodded after a moment of thought, and left the Tongtian Pagoda with the man.

The woman in the blue dress standing in the distance carefully looked at the person who was talking to Bai Donglin, and after a while recalled, said: "Miss Jiu, that person seems to be a disciple of Dan Dingfeng."

"Well, when did Bai Donglin get in touch with Dandingfeng?"

Miss Jiu's slender eyebrows frowned slightly, her expression inexplicably unsure of what she was thinking, she watched Bai Donglin have followed the people of Dandingfeng, the golden light flashed all over her body, and disappeared instantly.

...

The two of Bai Donglin crossed the light gate and came outside a majestic hall.

The man confessed apologetically and said, "Brother Bai, please forgive me. There were so many people just now, some things are hard to elaborate."

"Nothing, I don't know how the senior brother is called? What does it have to do with Deacon Shang Zhongzuo?"

Bai Donglin waved his hand and asked curiously, the jade pendant that this person took out just now was Shang Zhong's token.

He wondered in his heart, why not contact him directly if something happened, it would be so troublesome.

"Brother Bai is polite, in the next Shao Changxiu, Deacon Shang Zhongzuo is the teacher."

"No matter how strong you are, or how to refine alchemy, you are not as good as you, brother Bai, but you can't afford the word brother. We should still be worthy of our peers."

Shao Changxiu shook his head again and again. He was just one of Shang Zhong's many disciples, and his seniority was not high.

His master praised Bai Donglin very much. During this time, he heard calluses in his ears.

"Well, Brother Changxiu, I don't know what Deacon Shang Zhongzuo wants to do with me? Why did you come to this sect affairs department?" Bai Donglin went straight to the subject without talking nonsense.

The Zongmen Affairs Department is the place to deal with all kinds of chores inside and outside the Saint Zong. The collection and delivery of Zongmen tasks and the collection of task rewards can also be done here.

Jidao bracelets can also receive tasks, but they are all internal tasks of the sect. If you need to go out of the sect, you need to come to this sect affairs department to handle it.

"Brother Bai, I really don't lie to you, my master didn't tell me exactly what happened."

Shao Changxiu looked a little embarrassed, he was just a microphone, and then continued:

"Master said that after I reported my family, Brother Bai would naturally understand that it was something agreed between you, and asked Brother Bai to receive a mission to go out. Follow the instructions of this jade token, and you will find my teacher. Respect his old man."

Bai Donglin frowned. The thing they had agreed upon was to participate in the alchemy meeting, but is it necessary to make it so mysterious?

Sneaky like a thief, even his disciples are hiding it.

Bai Donglin thought for a moment, loosened his brows, reached out his hand to take the jade pendant, nodded and said, "I already know about this. I will go to Deacon Shang Zhongzuo, there is brother Lao Changxiu!"

"Don't dare, it's all a matter of course."

"Since the words have arrived, Shao will leave first."

"Brother Changxiu walk slowly."

The two confessed to each other to say goodbye, and Shao Changxiu cut through the light gate and disappeared, leaving Bai Donglin standing in place.

"It seems that the Dandao Conference is not so simple on the surface, otherwise it doesn't need to be so complicated."

"Can't even the Saint Sect cross-domain teleportation array be used?"

It seems that this bad old man still has a lot of things to hide from him!

But think about Shang Zhong's promise of his reward, he still wants to go to this alchemy meeting, and there is no reason to abandon the treasure that he can get.

"It happens to be that I have been cultivating in the Saint Sect for so long, and I haven't gone out to take a good look at the huge world of Qianyuan Realm!"

Bai Donglin's eyes were eager to try. He is now full of strength, coupled with the gift of immortality, as long as he doesn't get blind, he is not afraid of encountering danger.

Stepping into the main hall of the Zongmen Affairs Department, the huge and bright space is crowded with people, and the number of disciples is no less than the Tongtian Pagoda.

All the trivial things in the sect are handled here, and the handover of outgoing tasks is just one of them.

According to the guidance, when you come to a specific area, the light curtain suspended in the air, countless characters roll, each character is a task, refreshed at any time, the number of tasks is numerous and dense.

The mind penetrates into the light curtain. Here, like the mountain of books, it has the search function. This saves a lot of effort and allows each disciple to find the task he wants as quickly as possible.

With a move of mind, search for "going out", "no employer", "no time, strength, identity, and geographic restrictions".

The light curtain flashed, except for the tasks that met the conditions, all the other tasks were blocked.

Bai Donglin quickly swept his mind, his eyes lit up after a while, and he saw a satisfactory task.

Long-term task: demons and demons.

Task content: Search and eradicate the monsters and ghosts that are incompatible with the will of heaven and earth. Depending on the level of the eradicated target's strength, rewards will be given with varying contribution points.

Task conditions: None, can be received and submitted repeatedly.

Bai Donglin nodded in satisfaction, this task is good, not only fully meets his requirements, but also monsters and ghosts are a good thing.

Kill it and get the reward of the will of heaven and earth!

He killed some low-level ghosts before he started to practice officially~www.mtlnovel.com~ That clear spirit is a good thing, which can make people realize epiphany.

I didn't understand anything before, and my mind was empty, I could only use it to comprehend some common martial arts.

But now it's different. For him, it's not just a way to improve the perception of the law. The knowledge in his mind is like a sea. If he falls into an epiphany, the gain will be great!

After making up his mind, Bai Donglin took the task with his mind, and a ball of light fell out of the light curtain and fell into the Jidao bracelet.

This ball of light is the proof of entering and leaving the Saint Sect, and only with this thing can it evade the supreme guardian formation of the Sect.

Mind reminded Bai Xiaoxiao them through the bracelet, and then drew out the light gate to the far border of the sect.

Stepping out in one step is the boundless world.
