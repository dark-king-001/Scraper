The extremely large Saint Sect, which occupies an extremely large area, is an uninhabited restricted area for 100,000 miles outside the Supreme Guardian's Great Array.

Only one hundred thousand miles away will there be people and towns. One hundred thousand miles is an exaggerated distance for mortals, but it is nothing to monks.

The reason why this restricted area is divided is because the Saint Sect Supreme Guardian Array.

This array is running all the time, sucking the aura of heaven and earth, the aura of this 100,000-mile forbidden zone is extremely thin. This is one of them.

The second reason is because the power of the Great Array is too terrifying. Although all the powers are restrained, an invisible aura spreads uncontrollably around.

Under the oppression of this terrifying breath, let alone mortals, even monks would be panicked, and their hearts seemed to be oppressing the mountains, and this area was simply not suitable for long-term survival.

For high-ranking monks, crossing a mere 100,000 miles is as easy as crossing the "threshold" before a mortal goes out, so the Holy Sect does not care about the existence of this restricted area, anyway, it has no influence on the travel of the Holy Sect's people.

Bai Donglin used the vouchers in the bracelet to successfully get out of Shengzong.

Looking far away from the wind, it is an invisible virgin forest with towering trees and countless witless beasts living in it.

It is strange to say that these beasts with no wit can ignore the oppressive atmosphere of the Great Guardian Formation, and the beasts with wit will stay away from this restricted area.

Bai Donglin took out the jade pendant of Shang Zhong's token. After he came out of the clan, this jade pendant was activated, emitting a slight white light, and faintly connected to the distant place.

After concentrating on the position, Bai Donglin didn't stop too much. He stepped a thousand miles away in one step.

With his current cultivation base, he can now span thousands of miles with one step, and the speed can be regarded as extremely fast. After all, this description is very vague.

For example, the legendary Monkey King Monkey, a somersault of thousands of miles, for the cultivation of Monkey King, this speed must be considered very slow, because this description is too vague, distance divided by time Equal to speed, where is the truth of speed described by somersaults.

So everyone has overlooked a problem, that is frequency, how long does it take for a mortal to turn a somersault? Just one second.

Then Monkey King can't do more than 1,000 somersaults in one second, right?

Therefore, Monkey King's speed should be hundreds of millions of miles per second, which is reasonable, and it is worthy of the identity of Qitian Great Sage.

And Bai Donglin's stepping frequency is naturally not comparable to that of mortals. His steps are like phantoms, and he crossed the 100,000-mile restricted zone in the blink of an eye.

There is really no difference between this 100,000-mile restricted zone and the "threshold".

After crossing the Shengzong restricted area, Bai Donglin could already sense the abundance of people and smoke, and huge cities stood between the sky and the earth, crowded with smoke and breath.

According to Yu Pei's guidance, after tens of thousands of miles away, Bai Donglin's figure stopped in front of a huge city.

Youlan City, one of the four sub-cities of Taihao, the main city of the wasteland, the main city of 2008, covers an area of ​​a large area and has a population of over 100 million.

Shang Zhong's breath guidance was in this city, Bai Donglin thought for a moment, and took away the holy sect uniform outside of his body, replaced it with a black robe, and concealed the aura of the ultimate bracelet inside his body.

This is such a mysterious and mysterious Shang Zhong, it is better to hide his identity before he knows the reason.

Flicking his sleeves, the breath lingered all over his body, shielding the surrounding crowd and the perception of the city guards, stepping through the deep caves and entering Youlan City.

The prosperous and quaint streets are full of the breath of time, with people coming and going in the city, shoulder-to-shoulder, and full of voices.

In these years, except for Bai Donglin, who practiced to fight and kill, he hadn't experienced this kind of firework for a long time.

Unconsciously, he slowed down, carefully observing and experiencing everything around him.

Compared with the White City where he lived for many years before, this Orchid City had a large number of monks in addition to a larger area and a more prosperous area.

Its proportion even faintly exceeds the number of mortals, although most of them have just stepped into the body refining realm, only a little rough body refining secrets, and even the dantian has not been opened, so they are not true monks.

Perhaps these people are just for physical fitness, after all, cultivation also requires talent, and Bai Donglin walked into the city while thinking.

After walking through the outer city and entering the inner city, the flow of people will be much smaller, and the noise will be reduced a lot.

Mortals in this inner city are even more rare. Almost all monks have a cultivation base, and the things sold in the surrounding shops have become all kinds of materials needed for monks' cultivation.

From time to time, teams of black armored guards ran past. These soldiers had a strong aura, and they all had a strong cultivation base.

After all, all they had to face were monks. Fighting monks was forbidden in the city. They needed to maintain order and deter these unruly monks. No absolute force would do.

With a rough understanding of the composition and operation of this city, Bai Donglin didn't have the intention to continue shopping, and gradually accelerated his pace.

After a while, I came to a secluded house. As soon as I wanted to knock on the door, the door opened automatically, and it was Shang Zhong who opened the door.

"Hahaha, you can count as coming to the east, come, come, please come in quickly!"

When Shang Zhong sensed the approach of the treasure jade pendant, he quickly opened the door and saw that Bai Donglin's eyes lit up and he greeted Bai Donglin with enthusiasm on his face.

"Let Deacon Shang wait a long time." Bai Donglin said slightly, and then walked into the courtyard side by side with Shang Zhong. He was a little uncomfortable with the old man's enthusiasm.

"Hey, Donglin, don't be so dismissive. The name of a deacon is too unfamiliar. If you don't mind calling me a loyal uncle, it's fine!"

"Don't dare, the younger generation should call you still old."

Bai Donglin's fingers shook slightly. This old man was tens of thousands of years older. The two had only met for the second time. It was too strange to be called Uncle.

And the relationship is so close, it's hard to talk when it comes to benefits!

There was a trace of disappointment in Shang Zhong's eyes, and he couldn't persevere. The two went to the hall and sat down, and then another servant offered spiritual tea.

Bai Donglin saw that Shang Zhong did not speak, nor did he take the initiative to ask, he slowly tasted the spiritual tea in his hand, anyway, he was not in a hurry, he was calm, and the one who was anxious should be Shang Zhong.

"Ahem, Donglin, calling you out in this way is also due to a reason. Indeed, I didn't tell you clearly about the alchemy conference last time."

The real color flashed in Bai Donglin's eyes, put down the spiritual tea in his hand, and motioned to Shang Zhong to continue.

Shang Zhong's expression was complicated, and after pondering for a moment, he continued: "This participation in the Alchemy Conference is actually just my personal opinion, and it has nothing to do with Shengzong."

"Furthermore, we also need to hide our identities, and cannot participate in the alchemy meeting as a disciple of the Holy Sect."

Bai Donglin looked surprised. The Supreme Dao Sacred Sect is a noble sect, and it is a holy place for physical cultivation. Why does it need to be concealed when participating in an alchemy meeting?

"Old Shang, why is this?"

Shang Zhong took a sip of Lingcha, with a memory of memories in his eyes, put down his teacup and said:

"The last alchemy conference was my master, who is now the leader of the alpine peak."

"At the beginning, there was some unhappiness, my master had a bad temper, and finally made a big fight in the meeting..."

"So, hey!"

Shang Zhong sighed as he finished speaking, with five points of self-blame, three points of guilt, and two points of unwillingness.

Seeing Shang Zhong performing the fan-shaped picture, Bai Donglin's mind turned around, and he probably guessed the cause and effect.

It seems that Shengzong broke up with the Dan League that held the alchemy conference, whether it was the Dan League revoking the eligibility of the elixir to participate in the alchemy conference, or the Shengzong took the initiative to give up the qualification to participate in the face.

The result is the way it is now. If you want to participate in the Alchemy Conference, you have to hide your identity and hide it from the forces of the Alchemy League and the Holy Sect.

And the reason for this Shang Zhong to advocate privately is nothing more than unwillingness, or wanting to avenge his master to save face.

It seems that this Shang Zhongxin is obsessed with a lot of ~www.mtlnovel.com~ The conflict that broke out with Danmeng may also be related to him. No wonder he was willing to ask him to do something so precious.

Bai Donglin, who wants to understand everything, doesn't care. He doesn't care about these **** things. He just came to make extra money, waved his hand and said:

"Old Shang, please rest assured, I don't care whether you hide your identity or not. I don't care about the old past of Shengzong and the Dan League. I promised you that I will naturally go to the Dan Dao Conference."

Shang Zhong's eyes lit up when he heard the words, he was really afraid that Bai Donglin would be worried after hearing the truth, and would give up the deal with him.

After all, it is reasonable for an ordinary disciple to dare not get involved in the nastyness between the two top forces.

"Hahaha, good!"

"Donglin, the old man did not misunderstand you!"
