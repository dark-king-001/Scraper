After half a month.

Through several cross-domain transmissions, the three of Bai Donglin finally arrived at the Dan Region, the city where the Dan Dao Conference was held-the heart of the Nine Rings!

The pill domain and the wasteland are too far apart, the wasteland is located in the east of the Qianyuan realm, and the pill domain is located in the west, and the three of them have almost crossed most of the Qianyuan realm.

The cross-domain teleportation arrays that can be used by ordinary monks in the wasteland are all arranged in the main city of One Thousand and Eight.

Like other giant cities in the mainland that have a cross-domain teleportation array, the level of this formation is not as advanced as that in the Saint Sect, and the distance spanned is limited, so the three of them made multiple jumps.

The layout of the cross-domain teleportation array is complicated, the consumables are terrible, and the maintenance cost is high. It is already very good to be able to deploy this kind of teleportation array with a lower level, and only some super cities have this strength.

And every activation of the cross-domain teleportation array consumes terrifying energy, so every teleportation will only start after a certain number of people are gathered together, which is why the three of them took more than half a month.

Most of the time is waiting for the formation to start, after all, the transmission process is only a moment.

The Heart of Nine Rings is the largest city in the Pill Region, and can be ranked in the top five in the entire Qianyuan Realm!

At this time, the three of Bai Donglin were located in the sixth ring area and had just been transmitted by the cross-domain teleportation array.

Shang Zhong's lips moved slightly, and he spoke to Bai Donglin and Nian Nian's mind:

"The old man will not walk with you anymore. There are too many old acquaintances in the Dan League here, and walking too close to you may expose Donglin's identity."

"Donglin, everything is up to you! The old man will look at you in secret, pay attention to hide your identity, and when you receive the award, you will show the bracelets of the Jidao, let the old antiques look at it. We practice, we are Jidao. Saint Sect can also produce alchemy geniuses!"

Speaking of this, Shang Zhong's eyes rolled eagerly, as if he had seen Bai Donglin standing on the top of the heart, raising his arms, then turned his head to look at Nian Nian, and said gratefully:

"Yearly, thank you very much this time, this old man of love must be remembered in his heart!"

"Huh! It's all right, the older you get, the more wordy you are, let's go, Xiao Bai!"

Year after year, he waved his hand and stepped out of the teleportation array first. His body swayed, and his fiery figure was alluring just from the back.

"okay!"

Bai Donglin agreed, a smile flashed in his eyes, and turned to Shang Zhong slightly to confess his hand:

"Old Shang, don't worry, it's on me!"

He gave Shang Zhong a reassuring look, and then quickly walked to keep up with the year before the two slowly disappeared from the crowd.

Shang Zhong stood there for a while, looking around at the familiar and unfamiliar scene, his eyes were complicated.

‘The heart of the Nine Rings, ten thousand years, Master, let the disciples help you wash the humiliation you have suffered! ’

The Alchemy Conference is organized by the Alchemy Alliance, and the Alchemy Alliance is a huge organization that is led by the four great alchemy sacred sites of the Alchemy Domain and united with many powerful alchemy sects.

The organization's core mission is to "maintain the interests of alchemists and jointly explore and develop the alchemy", and its sphere of influence covers most of the only real world.

It can be said that as long as there are alchemists, there will be the shadow of the pill league!

The sects established with Dan Dao as the core are different from other forces established in the deep mountains and old forests. They need to trade pills and medicinal materials with external forces, so many huge cities have been born.

The heart of the Nine Rings is one of them. This huge city is not only the place where the previous alchemy conferences are held, but also the largest pill trading center in the alchemy domain.

"Sister Nian! So big!"

"The heart of the nine rings is really too big! This is the first time I have seen such a huge city!"

Bai Donglin followed Nian Nian, his eyes swept the distance, and he was surprised.

Forced by the fierce power of every year, Bai Donglin has changed his name to sister Nian during this period of time.

Nian Nian said, her age is only "a little bit" older than him.

Bai Donglin stared straight at Nian Nian, this kind of teasing him has been used to this time, and he doesn't need money, don't look at nothing!

Seeing that Bai Donglin was so shameless, he only had to converge every year, shaking his head slightly, really a brave kid, and said as he walked:

"The heart of the Nine Rings is the second most important thing. Qianyuan Realm is not without a larger city. The most powerful thing is the long history of this city."

"At the beginning of the establishment of the Dan League, the heart of the nine rings stood on this land..."

With the years of speaking, Bai Donglin also deeply realized the greatness of this city, which can be said to be one of the miracles of the human city.

The hearts of the Nine Rings are distributed in a ring-shaped belt, just like its name, divided into nine rings in total, plus the "heart" area at the deepest part of the Nine Rings.

The nine circles are distributed along the terrain, rising from the outside to the inside. There is no fixed number as to how large the scope is. As the population continues to converge, the outermost periphery is still slowly expanding.

This city is full of array restrictions, Bai Donglin’s supernatural powers can’t be displayed, and his spiritual thoughts are not daring to come out at will, so there is only a vague concept of the size of this city.

The population of the heart of the Nine Rings is even more impossible to count. This huge city is vomiting a huge population of hundreds of millions of people all the time.

The monks gathered here are not only people from the Qianyuan Realm, but also various forces from the depths of the universe. Most of these monks who come from afar are to purchase high-quality pill.

The heart of the nine rings has a very long history, and it has existed since the beginning of ancient times.

During these long years, it has been affected by wars many times. At the worst time, the Nine Rings were all turned into ruins, but the deepest "heart" remained.

But every time it is destroyed by war, the heart of the nine rings can be quickly re-established until now.

Between Bai Donglin and Nian Nian chatting, he crossed a few short-distance light gates to arrive at the Nine Ring Road area, and then went straight into a huge house.

The plaque of this house is engraved with the three characters "Nether Prison Gate". This is the stronghold of the Nian Nian Sect in the heart of the Nine Rings.

As long as there is a powerful force in Qianyuan Realm, they will establish a stronghold here, but they are not only used to trade pill. This kind of mixture of fish and dragons, where all forces converge, there are too many things that can be done, just collecting intelligence. , It is necessary to establish a stronghold.

"Gluck, come here, my good disciple, let the teacher see if you have lost weight."
