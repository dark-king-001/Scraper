Bai Donglin was silent for a moment.

In this world, with the huge power of the Dan League, it is also very reasonable to be able to obtain something like a "hypercube". With an idea, it is even easier to develop such a thing.

Bai Donglin's thoughts were put on the rules of the game again. The hypercube after the magic change did not have the original intent to kill at every step, everything was linked to alchemy.

There are six sides in the upper and lower quadrants, six passages, and a total of six roads. Participants need to choose a road according to their own strengths.

Bai Donglin estimated that he was at the center of this magically modified hypercube at this time. He needed to choose a channel and then break all the way until he walked out of here to complete the game.

At this moment, changes began to occur in the cube space, each silver wall began to shine, and a light ball slowly emerged.

Among the six light **** are suspended six kinds of alchemy materials, and a piece of white paper filled with text.

When the mind touched the ball of light, the direction of alchemy that each represented took the initiative to come to mind, healing, breaking, recovering, augmenting, erupting, and toxic!

After all, alchemists are still focusing on improving the cultivation of auxiliary cultivators. Almost all of the six paths are gaining effects. Among them, the most difficult one should be the "Broken Realm" category. Both low-level and high-level pill are rare levels.

And the most partial should belong to the Poison Pill. Bai Donglin pondered for a moment, but he chose "Very Poison". There is no need to challenge the more difficult ones. He just likes to refine the Poison Pill!

In this first game, you only need to get out of the hypercube and choose a path to go on. As for the difficulty level, it doesn’t matter, this is not the final.

Bai Donglin stretched his hand into the ball of light, twisting the white paper, and at the same time actively judged him to confirm the path.

The white paper in the remaining five light **** disappeared instantly, leaving only six different alchemy materials suspended in the air.

The rules of the game are very simple, but the difficulty of operation is not small. The white paper he chose was not written on a specific pill, but a description of the properties of poison pill.

He needs to use the 36 kinds of alchemy materials in this cube space according to the medicinal properties of this poison pill to refine the pill that meets the requirements of the white paper based on his own experience.

The degree of consistency of the drug properties cannot be less than 80%!

This level is mainly to test the alchemist's understanding of the medicinal properties of the endless alchemy materials, and the test of the alchemist's knowledge base is very high.

The difficulty is not the alchemy skills, but how to refine the pill that meets the requirements with the limited alchemy materials.

It is worthy of the Alchemy Conference, I am afraid that most of the contestants in this magic modified hypercube will have to be wiped out!

Thirty-six kinds of alchemy materials, although the medicinal properties of this first pill is not complicated, there are only two or three refining opportunities.

If it fails, it will be eliminated directly, but there will not be an unlimited supply of materials, so before refining, you must understand everything before refining, the characteristics of various materials, and the reactions between each other, and so on.

Huaxue Pill: Make the user's blood lose the ability to coagulate and scab, and there is no restriction on repairing.

This is Bai Donglin's first exam question. It is quite simple and has no restriction on cultivation level, which means that even if it can be used on mortals, it will be considered a challenge.

This kind of medicine was easy for Bai Donglin, but he didn't do it easily. Instead, he sat cross-legged and started thinking with his eyes closed.

According to the rules of the game, your choice in each cube will affect the changes in the space of the next cube, whether it is the selected materials or the medicinal properties of the refined pill.

And also consider the materials brushed out of the other five kinds of medicinal medicinal channels. These materials are all closely related to their corresponding characteristic medicinal medicinal pills at first, but they will change with your choice of materials later.

It is a very complicated brain-burning process to move the whole body by pulling a hair!

The most perfect way is to take all the changes in the medicinal materials in the cube space into consideration when refining the first pill.

At the same time, we must also consider the medicinal requirements on that piece of white paper, up to the last pill that was exported.

Whether anyone can achieve this level, Bai Donglin doesn't know, he can do it anyway!

Bai Dong, who was sitting cross-legged in the Divine Sea, moved his eyebrows slightly, and began to deduct a trace of energy.

In an instant, countless kinds of alchemy materials emerged in their minds, combining one by one, separating, and recombining each other, and many poison pills with different characteristics were deduced.

...

Outside, the Dandao Conference prefecture-level competition field.

In a huge void, there is no distinction between up, down, left, and right.

In this empty place, there are innumerable huge white jade platforms suspended on these platforms. These platforms are filled with crowds of crowds, all of which are team leaders from various contestants.

In the center surrounded by these white jade platforms, there is a huge cube suspended in silver, the whole body space is superimposed on layers, and the surface is divided into endless small cubes, which rotate slightly without regularity.

And above the silver hypercube, there is also a huge palace suspended in it, with people coming and going. It is the organizer of the Dandao Conference, the people of the Dan League, who are overseeing the entire prefecture-level stadium.

The silver cube is suspended in four directions with huge light curtains on all sides, in which the picture rotates continuously, reflecting the situation of the contestants in the cube.

However, there are so many contestants. Although the pictures are constantly circulating, it is impossible to satisfy all the audience. Seeing the contestants you want to see, the Dandao Conference naturally has other means.

Standing on the white jade platform, Nian Nian's mind moved, hand pinched and entered the contest numbers of Bai Donglin and Lianxin into it, and two small light curtains that reflected the two suddenly appeared out of thin air.

Seeing this, Elder Liu, who was standing on the side, also pinched the Fa Jue, and the scenes of the other eight disciples also appeared. Ten small light curtains lined up in a row, suspended in the air, shining slightly.

Looking at Bai Donglin, who was sitting cross-legged year after year, motionless, his pretty face suddenly turned black, his silver teeth creaked, and he said in a bad tone:

"What the **** is this stinky kid doing? Didn't you tell him to be restrained? Why didn't it work just at the beginning!"

Immediately, his gaze swept across the remaining light curtains, and seeing that Lianxin had broken into the fourth cube space, he nodded with satisfaction:

"It's still my good disciple who is obedient and capable, unlike some bragging braggarts who drop the chain at a critical moment!"

Elder Liu on the side swept across the light curtain, and said teasingly: "Elder, the kid you came in temporarily doesn't seem to be so good!"

"If he is at this level, if it reaches the top of the sect, I am afraid it will not be easy to explain!"

After hearing this, she glanced back at Elder Liu every year. Although she didn't know what Bai Donglin was doing, she didn't believe that the person whom Shang Zhong admired so highly would be an embroidered pillow.

She understands Shang Zhongxin's obsession, so she would never play tricks on her in this kind of thing, and she would not dare to forgive him!

She has never tried Bai Donglin from start to finish, but she believes in her instincts that this utterly nagging kid is definitely not as simple as the surface.

"Hmph! I don't need Elder Liu to worry about my affairs. If anyone in the senior sect is dissatisfied, just come to me!"

Elder Liu was so stunned that his expression was stagnant, and his awkward face twitched slightly. He was thinking too much. With this grandmother's background, no one at the gate of the underworld dared to move her.

"The elders should know what they are in their minds. They are talking too much."

After Elder Liu said, he remained silent and stood with his hands down, pretending to be a wooden figure.

The young snorted, ignoring Elder Liu, staring at Bai Donglin with charming eyes, and wondering what the kid is doing. This first level shouldn't embarrass him!

Bai Donglin's abnormal movement only attracted the attention of a few people, and did not stir up many waves.

At this time, the fastest among the silver cubes has rushed to half, but in the face of increasingly difficult medicinal requirements and increasingly complex materials, the speed has gradually slowed down.

In addition, most of the contestants had consumed the materials provided and did not refine the medicinal pill that met the requirements, and were directly eliminated ruthlessly.

Three days passed in a blink of an eye, and for alchemy, three days was nothing.

And there is no time limit for this game. Among the more than 20 million contestants in the hypercube, only the first 10,000 winners will be selected. The first 10,000 contestants out of the hypercube will advance, and the rest will be eliminated.

The reason why there are more than 20 million people in the prefecture-level stadium is because it is not only the Qianyuan Realm forces who participated in the alchemy meeting.

There are also various forces from the void of the universe. Although the influence of the Alchemy Conference is not as exaggerated as the "Pill God Sacrifice", the forces that are not too far from the Qianyuan Realm will still send people to participate every two or three sessions.

Whether you can achieve results or not, the most important thing is to show your face in front of the Danmeng, paving the way for future pill trading.

The number of participants is still the Dan League's Dan Dao forces accounted for the majority, and the total number of all forces is less than a half of the others.

After all, the Dandao Zongmen relied on this for their food, and the results of previous competitions were indeed the case. The championship was almost rounded up by the Dan League disciples~www.mtlnovel.com~ to put it bluntly, and the rest of the forces came to accompany the race.

Come to witness the power of the Dan League!

Doesn't it show off if you have the strength?

In the silver cube space, Bai Donglin, who had deduced for three days, slowly opened his eyes, with countless characters circulating in his eyes.

The expression was indifferent, and it was not difficult for him to crack this level. If it weren't for other training tasks that would affect the soul, it would take less than three days to perform it.

The fingers moved slightly, several medicinal materials were photographed, the hands were swelling with blue inflammation, they were refined and fused, and a simple pill was refined with the blink of an eye.

Throwing the pill to the silver wall, the light flashed and the pill melted.

Conformity of medicinal properties: 100%.

A vortex passage slowly emerged from the silver wall, and Bai Donglin stood up and flicked his sleeves, stepping into it.
