Bai Donglin stood on the huge blue-purple flower heart, feeling soft under his feet, and a scent filled the tip of his nose.

I took a sip and felt my mind clear, and I couldn't help but nod slightly. This vine plant is really extraordinary, and the small flowers that grow randomly have such a magical effect.

This can be regarded as adding a buff to everyone, which can effectively increase the success rate of alchemy.

At this time, another piece of information was shot into my mind and digested instantly. It was the rules of the game.

In this flower heart, many light **** floating in the air will be randomly refreshed, and alchemy materials will be randomly refreshed on the flower heart.

In each ball of light is an elixir. Participants can choose the elixir they want to refine according to their abilities and the materials on the ground.

Any refining method can be used, and it is not allowed to use its own alchemy materials, only the elixir that is refreshed in Huaxin.

The refined pill can be placed on top of the flower center, and the flower center will absorb the pill, and then depending on the quality of the pill, the small blue-violet flower will continue to grow.

The way to determine the winner is to sort in order according to the growth height of each participant's floret!

Limited time for one month!

After Bai Donglin read the rules of the game carefully, it seems that there is no such thing as the semi-final.

The rules of this game are very flexible, and the choice is left to each participant.

You can choose to win by quantity and refine some low-grade pill, which can also stimulate the growth of florets, or you can spend time holding big moves to get a high-level pill and fly directly into the sky.

In addition, it is a bit of a test of the players' luck. The alchemy herbs are randomly refreshed, which is a bit unfriendly to some players who are not very knowledgeable.

But the problem is not big, this is also to select the real alchemy genius, the winners only take the top ten, for these people, there is no partial discipline.

Ten thousand contestants stood quietly on top of Huaxin, calming their minds, adjusting their breath, and an invisible fighting spirit rose up.

Alchemy competition is also an alternative war!

And now, with Rosha's pre-war trash talk, he has successfully turned everyone's attention to Bai Donglin.

So many laws are simultaneously applied, and there are many types of laws contained therein, which will naturally resonate with nearby laws.

Body cultivation is to engrave the law into the body and spirit orifice, while Qi cultivation is to engrave the law into the body.

Physical training is to cultivate the small universe of the human body; Qi training is to cultivate the universe of heaven and earth, and is more good at comprehending the power of the outside world and driving the power of heaven and earth.

This is one of the strengths of Qi cultivation alchemy masters. All kinds of alchemy materials are born between heaven and earth. The inherent advantages of Qi cultivation make it easy for them to grasp the subtle changes of these materials.

The second advantage is that Qi cultivation is more powerful than physical cultivation spirits of the same level, which is also very important for alchemy.

And the biggest advantage is the law, which is also the root cause of the physical cultivation alchemist being crushed by Qi cultivation!

Bai Donglin's eyes swept away, and he gathered the people's Dharma form into his eyes. Three became a medicine furnace, three became a pill cauldron, and three became various flame gods, water, and thunders.

All are things that have a huge bonus to alchemy!

Although these people have similar types and appearances, they also have different powers because of the different laws engraved in them.

"Innate Jiulong Furnace!"

Rosha snorted coldly, and sacrificed the medicine that he was about to condense into substance.

As soon as the mind moved, the medicine furnace law phase automatically opened the furnace lid, a suction force appeared, and most of the alchemy material on the flower heart was sucked in, and then the medicine furnace automatically closed.

With the nine dragons entwining the furnace body, the light of the long eye was shining, and the sound of dragons groaned faintly.

The nine groups are of different colors, and the viscous energy groups with different laws and fluctuations are vomited into the medicine furnace by the dragon's head.

The many materials in the medicine furnace are divided into nine groups, lined up, and jumped into the law energy group one by one to refine and purify.

Bai Donglin slowly regained his gaze, and this Rosha had eliminated all his personality defects. He was indeed a powerful alchemist. He even refined nine different types of pills at the same time.

Moreover, the Dharma is also very extraordinary. There is a faint flow of Taoism on it. Compared with the medicine furnace method of other contestants, it is like a standout.

Damn, this grandson Rosha won't be the reincarnation of the Immortal Tool Spirit, right?
