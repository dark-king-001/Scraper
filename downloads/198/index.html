After a while, Bai Donglin gradually calmed down.

Putting the empty profound crystal ball into the bracelet, this thing does not completely belong to him now, and Luosha on the side is still staring at it.

According to the rules of the previous Dandao Conference, the awards of these competitions will not be made public, and only the winners and a small number of Danmen's internal high-level officials can know.

This is also a way to protect the contestants. After all, things like killing people and stealing treasures are not uncommon.

Bai Donglin glanced over the gloomy Luosha below, it was not surprising that he could know what the reward was as his father.

According to Shang Zhong, under normal circumstances, the first place in the prefecture level is to reward the seed of the peerless level, but this time the rewards of the competition are far beyond the past.

The rule of unparalleled level, and it is also the rule of dimensionality, it is no wonder that Rosha will bite into it.

The law of dimensionality can be roughly divided into three branches, dimensional space, dimensional matter, and dimensional time.

The three are the rules of unparalleled levels, and because of the particularity of the dimensions, there is no difference between them.

According to a secret anecdote in Shushan, these three dimensional laws merge into one, which is an extremely powerful dimensional rule!

The low-dimensional world carried in the golden spire can be created by the strong who mastered the dimensional rules.

Bai Donglin secretly guessed that the dimensional law he obtained should be dimensional space, and the seeds of the Wushuang-level law would not fluctuate and overflow, which is why he didn't know what the pitch black crystal ball was at first.

He judged it based on the color of the seed of the law, and the "heart of dimension" of the vine plant.

With the preciousness of the Wushuang Law, even the rich and powerful Dan League cannot be easily taken out. Since it is taken out, it shows that it has a stable output channel.

The "Heart of Dimensional" at the foot, which is good at manipulating space, just meets this point, but the speed of gestation must be very slow. It is possible to bred a seed in millions of years, or even tens of millions of years!

Ignoring Luosha's cannibal gaze below, Bai Donglin was already thinking about how to escape the Nine Rings Heart smoothly.

All the laws of Wushuang level have very high requirements for the carrying spirit orifices. Bai Donglin secretly judged that he has only included a dozen or so spirit orifices so far. Even if the potential of the infinite spirit orifice is huge, he wants to carry the Wushuang law. It's also a bit reluctant.

This can't be done forcibly. Without certainty, Bai Donglin will not easily absorb this seed. If he fails, the seed of the law may be in danger of disintegration and escape.

With Shenhun's full deliberation and deduction, he already had a vague plan in his heart. If he wants to maximize the benefits of this baby, the opportunity lies with Xiao Zi.

This seed is of great use to him, it is absolutely impossible to let go, his eyes gradually become cold, and whoever wants to grab it, he cut the opponent's claw!

After thinking about it, Bai Donglin had already made up his mind. At this time, all the ten winners had received their rewards. Except for Luosha, they all looked happy.

At this time, new changes appeared in the upper sky, the space was rippling, the rays of the sky were shining, and a few stalwart figures stepped out of it.

The dozens of members of the Bronze Hall, together with the elder Dangan, all descended on the top of the heart at the same time.

However, a dozen people are located in different dimensions on the third floor. A dozen of them can see each other beside each other, but Bai Donglin can only see a few people in the dimension of the prefecture-level arena.

Luo Dian and several others who had a prejudice against physical cultivation alchemy masters were all led by Dangan into the dimension of the heaven-level arena, and the prefecture-level arena was handed over to Liu Dongchuan.

Liu Dongchuan looked at the peaceful Bai Donglin, nodded slightly, and there was a trace of appreciation in his eyes. It is a good seed to obtain such a treasure, but still can hold his mind.

And being able to cultivate the alchemy to this level as a body cultivator can be described as a true alchemy genius.

If this little guy can be recruited into the Dan League, perhaps it can bring a bit of anger to the decayed Dan League.

"Congratulations, the winners of this competition."

"As a young generation, you who have the most talent for alchemy, you can also choose a reward, and that is to join the alchemy league."

The Dan League is a loosely structured alliance organization. The founders at the beginning all came together with the love of Dan Dao, and created the only one of the greatest forces in the real world.

Joining the Dan League does not need to be separated from the original sect force, which is equivalent to a kind of affiliation, with an additional identity.

And of the ten winners this time, eight of them are already sect disciples of the Dan League coalition forces. Joining the Dan League is a matter of course, and they will get a lot of status rights, and the Dan League's various resources will also be inclined to them.

In addition to Bai Donglin, another female cultivator from the non-dan alliance forces immediately expressed their stance and expressed their willingness to join the Dan alliance.

If you miss this opportunity, you have to start from the bottom if you want to join in the future. Anyway, there are only advantages and no disadvantages, why not join.

Then everyone looked at Bai Donglin, the biggest dark horse who rose against the trend and won the first place with physical cultivation.

Bai Donglin was silent for a moment, and said slightly to Liu Dongchuan's great powers, neither humble nor overbearing:

"Thank you for your kind intentions, I have no plans to join the Dan League for the time being."

Bai Donglin's decisive refusal to show everyone's surprise, Liu Dongchuan's face showed regret, and in his capacity, naturally, he would not make a second invitation.

As for Luosha, he was happy that Bai Donglin didn't join the Dan League, he didn't need to worry too much when he started to do it later.

"My sirs, there is actually one more thing that the younger generation concealed from you. It happens to be in front of everyone and I will tell you all!"

After Bai Donglin finished speaking, he stepped into the void, a light flashed on his body, a black robe turned into a black robe, and his aura suddenly became Ling Ran.

At this time, everyone looked at Bai Donglin curiously, and the endless audience of the Heart of the Nine Rings below, including those from other dimensions, wanted to see what this magical little guy wanted to say.

Below Shang Zhong's hands trembled, and every year he clenched his hands tightly, showing a little nervousness. The two who were the initiators naturally knew what Bai Donglin would say next.

Bai Donglin felt vaguely, a lot of unpredictable gazes, looking at him across dimensions.

Slowly raising his left arm, with a thought, the blood red Jidao bracelet hidden in the body appeared on the wrist.

With Bai Donglin's urging, the bracelet shone red, and the small word "polar" on it suddenly glowed with golden light, and the golden light shot into the void, reflecting a huge golden word "polar"!

The breath is mysterious, majestic and mighty!

"I, Bai Donglin, is a disciple of the Supreme Dao Sage Sect!"

"This time I am on the top of the heart, I just want to tell everyone that I am waiting for physical training, and I am not weaker than others on the path of the elixir!"

Bai Donglin's expression was solemn, his tone was awe-inspiring, and his mighty voice spread throughout the heart of the Nine Rings through countless light curtains. After speaking, his heart suddenly relaxed.

Okay! After finishing work and successfully completing the task, you can go home and plant the law of gravity!

When Bai Donglin showed the Jidao bracelet, Liu Dongchuan understood what was going on, but he didn't stop it. He was different from the people in Luo Dian, but he smiled.

Luosha looked shocked, and her body couldn't stop taking two steps back. How could the little lamb that he thought he could swallow at any time turned into a hedgehog covered in thorns and hard to start!

It can be said that there is a world of difference between the Saint Sect of the Extreme Dao and the Gate of the Underworld. The strength on the bright side is already a behemoth no weaker than the Dan League.

Moreover, the Saint Sect of Extreme Dao is extremely short-sighted. If the disciple of the sect died at the hands of his peers, it would be fine, but if an old fellow did not follow the rules and killed his disciple across the border, the anger of the Saint Sect would surely be met.

Not only Luosha, Luo Dian and many other members of the general public all had their faces sinking and became extremely ugly.

Luo Dian is because of Bai Donglin's identity, which has a little effect on the retrieving of the Dimensional Seed. Others feel that they have been severely beaten because of the events of the previous conference.

Being embarrassed, these nine days high, comparable to the power of the gods, naturally can't bear it, some people snorted and wanted to make a secret move.

"enough!"

Dan yelled and interrupted everyone's movements.

"Do you not exist as an old man? The matter ends here! Who dares to take action, don't blame the old man for being polite!"

Compared with the fierce reaction of the crowd on the top of the heart, the audience below the heart of the nine rings is much calmer, just a little surprised.

Even most people didn't know Bai Donglin's fake identity from the beginning. At this time, they would only feel that they were originally disciples of the Supreme Dao Sect. It's no wonder that he was so powerful and could win the first place with his physical cultivation status.

Ten thousand years ago, the nasty between the two forces, the people who knew about it were only a few after all, and only a few people changed their faces slightly, thinking of this old past.

Shang Zhong was so excited that he was in tears, and he would be calmer year after year, while his pity was dumbfounded, looking at his master pitifully.

Where is my younger brother? So a big junior, who was there just now, why did he disappear in a blink of an eye? !

The prestige of Ji Dao Shengzong is not just for fun!
