　　 The moment the female ghost died.

　　Bai Donglin feels his brain is clear and bright. This is the same feeling as after killing the wolf head monster last time.

　　 immediately sat cross-legged, running the Qishang Fist, stimulating the strong blood in the body, oscillating slightly within the seven major orifices.

　　Bai Donglin feels so fit with the world at this moment, as if he has entered the legendary unity of nature and man!

　　 vaguely felt the pulsation of the spiritual energy of heaven and earth.

　　 Tanzhong acupoint frequency coincides successfully!

　　 The three major orifices of Shuidao, Tianshu, and Zhongwan also coincided successfully.

　　 Just as Bai Donglin was trying to get all the seven acupoints in place in one step, the clear spirits in his mind was exhausted, and he fell out of that peculiar state in an instant!

　　 felt stunned in his heart, that state was too strange, his brain was like a firework, and the aura was continuous like a tide.

　　 It's a pity, only Qimen point, Yanglingquan point, Shenshu point three big orifices can be practiced into "Qishangquan".

　　 But this time the epiphany has greatly reduced the difficulty of the Qishang Boxing Sheet, and the seven-digit code has been directly changed to three-digit!

　　 He now has the confidence to learn "Qi Shou Quan" within seven days.

　　 calmed the blood, closed the power and opened his eyes.

　　 I looked around and found myself sitting cross-legged on a large rock. Where is there any luxurious mansion?

　　 This is a barren land, surrounded by weeds and corpses and bones, like mass graves.

　　Bai Donglin frowned and thought, and it seemed that he had fallen into the illusion. This illusion was neither strong nor weak, but it happened to be stuck with his own bug!

　　 If the illusion is very powerful, it will cause substantial damage to the spiritual will and even the soul. In this way, it will trigger the "harm reversal", which will be solved naturally.

　　 If the illusion is too weak to shake one's mental will, it will naturally have no effect.

　　 Actually, although he didn't see through the illusion last night, he had already noticed something was wrong, but he had no fear, and he would just accompany her to the scene.

　　 This kind of consequence is also because he does not have the power of the cultivation world. Although his body has been strengthened very strong, he is not a monk after all. Facing the monk, he is a little stretched.

　　 illusion is also a point of restraint if one's ability is not big or small.

　　 It seems that the tempering of spiritual will will be intensified in the future.

　　 And what is the pure spirit that inexplicably appeared in my mind after killing the monsters and monsters?

　　 Is it the monsters and monsters that come with them? Just like killing monsters and explosive equipment in previous games?

　　If this is the case, you should pay more attention to the traces of demons and ghosts in the future. This clear spirit is so easy to use, it is definitely a great benefit for people with low understandings like yourself!

　　 Bai Donglin cleared his mind, got up and walked down the mountain.

　　Walking and thinking, this female ghost doesn't know if it is a fish slipping through the net of a sect disciple. If it weren't for this world, it would be too dangerous. You can meet ghosts when you go out!

　　 went down the mountain and returned to the official road, and continued to walk in the direction of last night.

　　 Not long after he left, he met a caravan and hurried forward to ask for directions. Looking at the other guard's vigilant face, Bai Donglin stopped ten meters away and no longer approached, offering his hand from a distance:

　　 "Don't worry, fellow heroes, there is no malice in Xia Xia. Xia Xia is just lost. I want to ask how Baicheng can get there?"

　　 The caravan's guard saw that it was just a boy with no hair, and he was very vigilant in his heart.

　　 At this time, the fat man who saw the caravan owner came out of the carriage, smiled at Bai Donglin:

　　 "Little brother, our caravan is going to Baicheng, you are going in the opposite direction!"

　　 Let's go, what quantum mechanics is really unreliable!

　　...

　　 After an hour, he returned to the White House.

　　 First, I went to my father and told him about the soldiers of Inu Rong. Although Bai Donglin estimated that the country of Dog Rong only infiltrated such a small army.

　　 After all, the border is tightly guarded, and it is impossible for a large number of enemy troops to infiltrate.

　　 He didn't bother to worry about these things, so he could just tell Bai Li. He had fought Inu Rongguo for so many years and knew each other deeply, and he would handle it better than himself.

　　The idle Bai Donglin wandered around and arrived at the Wu Pavilion, ready to read a book on the first floor of the Wu Pavilion.

　　 This can be regarded as a low-profile version of the Alien Library!

　　 Books in this world are very precious things, and only rich and powerful people have time and money to play with knowledge at will.

　　With the strength of the Bai family, there are only tens of thousands of books! This is already quite remarkable. The production level is not high enough to compare with the previous life.

　　 But this is just the mundane world, what the monk's world is like, wait for him to find out later.

　　 The old men in Wuge Nursing Home remained the same. After saying hello, Bai Donglin entered the first floor of Wuge.

　　 just picked up a book and started to read it, regardless of the type or knowledge. In Bai Donglin's view, this is an accumulation.

　　This kind of accumulation is very beneficial to the future road of spiritual practice!

　　The growth of the tree of the road requires nutrients, and knowledge is the nutrient.

　　 The reason why Bai Donglin made this decision is also due to his own body. If he changes to a previous life, even if he has that ambition, how much knowledge can he remember? How many books can I finish reading? How much time will it take?

　　 was doomed to fail before it even started, because in the previous life, he did not have the hardware conditions!

　　 But now it’s different. His soul, physical fitness, his brain, memory, observation, understanding, logical thinking, etc., I don’t know how many times stronger than the previous life!

　　 I don’t say that I am not forgetting, but I can write it down after reading it two or three times. If it is not complicated and advanced knowledge, I can understand it while memorizing it!

　　 Even if you can't understand it for a while, you can remember it alive, and save it later when you need it, or when you can understand it, you can instantly recall it from your mind.

　　 The human body is a very mysterious thing, and the brain is even more important!

　　In the past life, the brain was called God's forbidden zone!

　　 In this life, the brain is where the soul of the Lingtai is.

　　 The potential of the brain can be said to be endless, so it is reasonable that the brain strengthened by Bai Donglin has such excellent learning ability.

　　 Fragile brain development is very dangerous. Bai Donglin is also a trick. It relies entirely on almighty strengthening energy. He treats the brain the same way. It is easy to say that strengthening is strengthening.

　　Bai Donglin read one book after another, only to hear the sound of flipping through books.

　　 The books on the stone table in front of him grew higher and higher, and until dark, he stretched and stood up.

　　 He didn't even eat lunch, he watched it all day, and he finished reading a hundred books! And they are all deeply in my mind, and I will never forget them.

　　 is more reliable than a computer hard drive!

　　 It took only a hundred days to finish reading the entire Wu Pavilion book.

　　 Moreover, as it continues to grow stronger, the speed of reading will increase in the future.

　　 walked out of Wu Pavilion leisurely, and returned to Qingyou Xiaozhu to sleep after dinner.

　　 Combination of work and rest has always been the lifestyle that Bai Donglin followed. Sleeping in his eyes is a kind of enjoyment. After working in the previous life, he didn't sleep well, so he won't be wronged in this life!

　　 Bai Donglin returned to a calm and regular day.

　　 Get up in the morning and practice "Seven Swamp Boxing" and "Jianhong Glimpse". After lunch, I will go to Wuge to read a book. When night falls, I will go to the aunt for dinner and feed the birds by the way.

　　 After eating, I went back to Qingyou Xiaozhu to sleep.

　　 Before going to bed, you must drink a cup of warm milk, and then do a tea-time soft exercise. When you go to bed, you will fall asleep immediately, and you will sleep till dawn, and never bring fatigue and stress to the next day.

　　 Live a peaceful and fulfilling life.
