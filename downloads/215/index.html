Rumble!

The silver pillar plows the land, and the mountain collapses and the ground cracks.

Countless huge and deep black holes are all over the earth. Looking down through the mouth of the dark hole, huge, criss-crossing yellow roots are faintly visible in the deepest place.

The countless silver beams of light pouring down from the top of the heart are so powerful that they have not completely shattered the earth and penetrated into the depths of the mantle, eliciting hot magma.

Relying on the deep underground of this area, the dense roots of the Heart of Dimensional intertwined with a large net, with its terrifying defensive power, living against the bombardment of the silver beam of light.

In the place where the Heart of Dimensionality originated, the figure in the golden robe sat on the ground, with his hands deeply inserted into the ground, and the blue veins on his arms burst out, faintly lingering silver light.

The golden brown expression was gloomy, black air surging in his eyes, the whole body was so full of resentment that it turned into a magical shadow behind him, and he roared up to the sky.

Why do these **** alchemists oppose him everywhere?

Isn't it good to stay where you are?

The change of the situation has completely lost control. It is not that he does not want to use the power of the Dimensional Heart to slaughter the alchemist from the beginning, but he can't do it.

A moment ago, all the power of the Dimensional Heart that he could mobilize had been used to stabilize and twist the black hole. If the Dimensional Heart's power was rashly removed, the black hole would be in danger of collapsing.

Just when the twisted black hole was completely stabilized and he was just about to free his hands, these alchemists immediately attacked and escaped.

As if everything was just right, he could see through all his methods.

"No matter who you are and what method you use, you must still be there. It is impossible to break through the blockade of endless dimensional space!"

Golden Brown drew out his hands fiercely, now that it is useless to think about it, it is the business to find a way to find these nasty guys.

The endless dimensional space blockade was arranged by him. It is a huge spherical space that covers the entire "heart" of the nine rings. The sky and the underground are all under blockade.

The only way out is the twisted black hole above the sky, and it is naturally impossible for them to escape through the twisted black hole.

His perception was integrated into the endless dimensional space blockade, and he did not sense any abnormal fluctuations.

"There are still two days left, even if it is to smash everything in this space within two days, I will find you all!"

He is now not only trying to kill these monks to vent his anger, but also to please the evil god, hoping to get help from the evil god, and completely master the heart of dimension!

As soon as his mind moved, Golden Brown's will instantly attached to the distorted black hole, the pitch-black passage that had stagnated for a while revolved fiercely, and endless black mist suddenly poured down.

"Come! A twisted existence born in a filthy and dark place..."

Deep in the canyon.

Wuwei Shenhun sat cross-legged with Liu Dongchuan, the golden-red spire suspended between them, the golden-red light flickered slightly, and Bai Donglin stepped out from it.

Wuwei Shenhun's eyes flickered slightly, turning into a stream of light and returning to the sea of ​​God.

When Wuwei opened the low-dimensional world, Bai Donglin, who had already deployed ninety-nine original bombs, also took the opportunity to enter the low-dimensional world through the split body of the obelisk.

The obelisk here comes across a long distance, which is also a primary application of the low-dimensional world, but this kind of function is very important in Bai Donglin's view.

Even surpassing many other functions, this is also the place where Bai Donglin values ​​the horn stele the most. When he enters the boundless universe and stars in the future, this inconspicuous function will be calculated in trillions of light years. The terrifying distance will shine!

"To the east..."

Liu Dongchuan looked extremely grateful when he looked at Bai Donglin, and then he stopped talking, as if there was something unspeakable and didn't know how to speak.

"Senior Liu, you don't have to say, you know this is impossible."

Bai Donglin shook his head slightly. Before Liu Dongchuan could speak, he refused decisively. He naturally knew what Liu Dongchuan wanted to say. It was nothing more than asking him to take the many powerful men above the sky into the low-dimensional world. .

This matter is too risky, it is impossible for him to agree, not everyone is willing to, like Liu Dongchuan, in order to save many monks, unscrupulously initiate two big oaths.

The stronger the stronger, the less likely it is to initiate an oath. For them, this is a very big fatal loophole. For example, they swear not to hurt Bai Donglin at all.

But what if Bai Donglin becomes an enemy to them in the future?

Is it slaughtered by the neck?

In other words, in the future, where there is Bai Donglin will be avoided for hundreds of thousands of miles?

This is absolutely impossible for a powerful and powerful person who is as high as a **** to accept it.

Moreover, the situation they are now facing is not a desperate situation. Instead of leaving such a fatal weakness, it is better to be cruel and immediately blew on the spot, activate the back hand, and resurrect at a huge price.

Not everyone is as loving as Liu Dongchuan, which is why Bai Donglin respects him.

And it is impossible for Bai Donglin to let these powerful people directly enter the low-dimensional world. Now in the low-dimensional world, the dozens of powerful incarnations sleeping in the jade card have already made Bai Donglin jealous.

This is also a situation he hadn't anticipated before, but it was just an incarnation. He still had a hint of certainty to kick it out of the low-dimensional world, but if it was a great power that retained its full strength, it would enter it.

He can't guarantee whether the low-dimensional world is likely to change ownership by many powerful means.

Hearing Bai Donglin's direct refusal, Liu Dongchuan's eyes dimmed slightly, and he knew that his thoughts were a bit too much. Bai Donglin had done enough, and he could only give up.

People's hearts are unpredictable, and how can he guarantee that other people, like him, will face the supreme treasure of the low-dimensional world without being distracted.

"Senior Liu, you enter the low-dimensional world first, and you still need to come forward for the follow-up."

"it is good!"

Liu Dongchuan nodded lightly, then raised his sword fingers and lightly pressed the center of his brows, and a faint wave of information spread out, instantly transmitted to the sky.

Bai Donglin's eyes flickered, Liu Dongchuan didn't avoid him when he did this, he probably guessed what he was delivering as soon as he thought about it.

‘I’ll leave the rest to Liu Dongchuan. You little guys in the one-two polar realm, you should explode as soon as possible. Don’t let the strong opponent look for aura and kill you completely! ’

Suddenly above the gorge there was a bright light, silver light shining, and a thick silver beam of light blasted from it.

A smile appeared in Bai Donglin's eyes, and it seemed that the incarnation of Dimension Heart's resentment was really anxious, and he had begun to bomb the area that was shielded from perception by the mighty power.

The entire "heart" of the Nine Rings is under the perception of Golden Brown, and the powerfully shielded areas like Liudongchuan are pitch black, so abrupt on the entire map.

Before, he could still ignore it, but now that things have developed to this point, he will naturally attract his attack.

Bai Donglin and Liu Dongchuan ignored the attack on the silver beam, and at the same time stepped into the low-dimensional world.

The golden red horn stele trembled slightly, and disappeared into the low-dimensional world.

Rumble!

The huge silver beam of light swept across in an instant, and the entire gorge suddenly turned into nothingness, leaving only a dark and deep hole in place.
