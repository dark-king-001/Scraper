"Divine Court Inspection Division, decree!"

Hum! A golden light shot out from the bow of the ship like lightning, facing the storm. It instantly turned into tens of thousands of feet in size, and plunged into the ground along with the roar of thunder.

Bai Dong was surrounded by white glowing fireflies in his eyes, staring at the decree made of gold glittering crystals, with a line on both sides of it.

Divine power is like prison, inspect the world.

The edict trembles slightly, and the rays of light converge and condense at the top of the edict, but a huge eyeball is outlined in a moment.

The eyeballs converged by the golden red light turned slightly, the golden pupils contracted and changed, staring at all the monks below.

Being swept by the eyeballs, everyone felt a chill, as if everything about them had been seen through.

Bai Donglin's eyes were cold, and the three divine souls in the divine sea instantly merged into one, with a vague aura, returning to the original and returning to the truth and re-transforming into an ordinary divine soul.

"Sister Nian, have the people of Shenting always been so unscrupulous?"

With frowning, Bai Donglin asked with an unhappy expression. According to the unspoken rules of the practice world, he would spy on others at will, and it would be fine if he was not discovered. If he was discovered, it could be regarded as a life or death provocation.

Year after year he shook his head slightly, and stretched out his hand to hold Bai Donglin's sleeves, as if he was afraid that he would be the first bird on impulse, and said in a cautious tone:

"I have never had any contact with the Shenting Inspection Division, and I don't know if the other party has always been like this, or because the matter was too serious this time, so the action was so direct."

"However, we just need to cooperate. It is still very troublesome to provoke these guys..."

It is a pity that not everyone is arrogant in the face of the Shenting Inspection Department, there are ghosts in their hearts, and there are many monks who do evil things.

"Run away!"

When the eyes of the golden pupils were about to be swept over, many monks struggled fiercely. Someone couldn't suppress their panic and shouted.

Suddenly, tens of thousands of streamers shot out from the crowd, fleeing in all directions, extremely fast, obviously desperate, only hating their parents for not giving them more legs.

Jingle bells!

There was a sound of metal collision, and countless dense golden chains shot out from the huge battleship, piercing the space, and each golden chain had a sharp barbed tip.

Pouch! All monks who flee from flying away, no matter the distance, are pierced by the golden chain at the same time, and all of them are imprisoned.

Shattered, the chain pulled tens of thousands of monks, and instantly retracted into the battleship.

Liu Dongchuan frowned in the void, remembering what the ancestor said when he left, and resisted the urge to shoot.

He was not a bad person, he knew in his heart that none of the monks who had fled frantically in the face of the Auditor General of the Gods Court had no good people, and he deserved it if he was arrested.

The drastic change only took a moment, and the huge battleship had just completely escaped from the space vortex, suspended in the void above everyone's heads.

The five figures standing on the bow of the ship flickered slightly, and instantly appeared on the huge decree.

The clothes of the five people are similar, but when you look closely, it is not only the color of the characters on the eyebrows, but also the patterns on the robes.

The leader put his arms around his chest, stepped forward a few steps, with a smile on his face, and squinted his eyes directly into a crescent moon.

"Everyone please stay calm, I'm Shenting Auditor, the sixth deputy director, He Du."

He Du's voice paused, his eyes looked around, and the golden red giant eyes floating above his head also moved slightly with his sight.

"I am waiting for the sudden arrival, just because of the drastic change in the heart of the Nine Rings, the Dan League is so easily broken through, it is impossible for the other party to do it without the inner response."

"These inner ghosts are our purpose. Please cooperate with our inspection department. If anyone makes trouble..."

He Du stopped talking, smiled, the huge eyes above his head burst into light, and everyone below trembled.

This grandson is a smiling tiger who cannibalize people without spitting out bones!

Bai Donglin's eyes were deep, and the strength of this Kadu was very strong, not an ordinary powerful person.

He didn't feel it, he didn't have this ability yet, but he could see it from the expressions and attitudes of Liu Dongchuan and others.

"Old man Dangan, the Dan League's rotating guard commander this term has met Deputy Director He."

Dan Gan stepped forward, came to the decree, and slightly offered his hand.

Dan League guards are on duty, changing every 10,000 years, which coincides with the time of the Alchemy Conference. They were about to change guards, but when they encountered this, Dan Gan was also upset.

"It turns out that it is Canglong Pill Sage, so it's so polite."

The atmosphere between the two of them was harmonious, and there was no gap between them because of the direct involvement of the Shenting Auditor in the matter. They both performed their own duties.

"Deputy Director He, although this drastic change seems to be serious, but fortunately it did not cause irreversible consequences. According to the old man, you should not pay too much attention to this matter, and the speed is coming so fast... "

Dangan's eyebrows were full of doubts. Although the Dan League's upheaval was fierce, only a hundred thousand cultivators died.

To put it bluntly, the inspector took away tens of thousands of people as soon as he arrived. This casualty shouldn't have alarmed the court.

He Du shook his head slightly, as if thinking of something bad, the smile that had been on his face was two points weaker.

"Canglong Pill Saint, you have been trapped here for the past few days, and you don't know anything about the outside world."

"Your Dan League is not the only one who has been attacked this time. The Qi League's stronghold in the Qianyuan Realm has also been attacked."

"They are not as lucky as you, their stronghold was completely destroyed, and it affected many refining sects around."

"The entire device area, 30% of the area has been completely fallen, and there are countless casualties..."

Dan Gan's expression changed wildly, and the conversation between the two was not specifically blocked.

After the uproar, everyone was honest. The more serious the situation, the greater the determination of the gods. If they don't cooperate well, they will definitely suffer.

Even if it is backed by a powerful sect, it is not good, after all, the opponent is a god!

Human forces are the only real world in the vast expanse, and all forces can be roughly divided into four types.

Aristocratic families have the strongest cohesion with their blood surnames. Although the number is small, as long as they survive for a long time, they must have thunder means. Almost the length of their existence can be used to distinguish their strengths and weaknesses.

As long as people still marry and reproduce, the power of the aristocratic family will never be extinct, and these children of the aristocratic family can also join the other three types of powers. They have a good blood and talent for cultivation. With the support of the family behind, they can almost all be mixed. Upper level.

Whether it is an ordinary dynasty or the cultivation world, the power of aristocratic family is the most powerful force that cannot be ignored.

The sect force, with the cultivation and inheritance as the bond to maintain, respecting the teacher, respecting the path, and ranking the seniority, is the most extensive and huge force in the cultivation world.

Organize alliances and use the same interests or a certain profession as the bond to maintain. The cohesion is not high, but the binding power is weak. It is loved by some cultivators who love freedom. The alchemist is the core of the alchemist. The core instrument league is the representative of this force.

The official forces are forces whose purpose is to fight against foreign aggression, preserve the human fire, and coordinate the entire cultivation world.

It has the characteristics of authority, justice, and jurisdiction. Of course, behind all this is the support of terrorist power.

The "Shen Ting" and the "Ghost Mansion", which were born on the basis of Qianyuan Realm, are one of the highest manifestations of official power in the cultivation world.

Dry is heavenly sun, Kun is earthy yin.

Yuan is the beginning and the end is the end.

The opposite of the Qianyuan Realm, the End of Kun Realm is the birth point of the "Ghost Mansion".

Within the God Court, it protects and controls the cultivation world of the human race, and has the function of supervision and jurisdiction.

In addition to the Ghost Palace Lord, its main function is to invade the alien world, assassinating, investigating, undercovering, destroying, the only meaning of existence is to bring death and destruction to the alien.

Bai Donglin's eyes flickered, his thoughts rolled, and the news he had learned from Shushan passed through his heart one by one.

The four types of forces are intricate, and there are monks superimposed on each other.****** For example, I may come from a certain family, and then I joined a sect, joined the Dan League organization, and still served in the gods. .

One person can intersperse the four types of forces.

The complicated relationship among them is a headache just to think about.

Such a huge and mutually overwhelming composition of forces can get along without hindrance. In the final analysis, the word "person" controls everything.

The supreme majesty of the Shenting, coupled with the tragic deaths and injuries of the instrument domain, everyone naturally did not want to be touched by the inspector of the Shenting at this time, and they all waited quietly for review.

He Du watched the restless crowd below quiet down, smiled satisfied, and said a few words with Dan Gan, Dan Gan nodded and Liu Dongchuan and the others turned into streamers and escaped into the upper battleship.

"Thank you for your support to the Audit Department."

"Cleaning and killing foreign races is an important matter to protect our ethnic group. Your cooperation is also a kind of contribution!"

He Du's eyes flickered, his gaze swept across the back of Dan Gan and the others who had left.

The little guys below can find at most some small fishes and shrimps, these powerful monks, especially those who explode.
