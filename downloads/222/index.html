Look at what a profession is!

When these little fishes and shrimps were caught, they didn't even raise their eyes, and they were still thinking about how to deal with the powerful abilities of the Dan League.

"Tsk, it's really a chore!"

Standing outside the light gate, Yundi looked at the Mu family's sister and brother who calmly entered the light gate, with a sorrowful smile on Hui Bai's face.

The full of longing when entering the world of cultivation, the fierce fight for blood, the loneliness of being far surpassed by the disciples of the bloodline family, the ecstasy of breaking through the gods, the perseverance and swelling ambition of being introduced to the dark forces...

Scenes flashed through his mind instantly, Yundi's eyes dimmed, and he stepped into the light gate one step at a time.

"Hehe, what a miserable life..."

The golden light spots lingering around Yundi's body seemed to sense something, a sudden stagnation, and instantly turned into a scarlet color. With twists and turns, the hard body cultivated by God's Origin Body instantly turned into nothingness.

Only the weak soul left behind was also completely imprisoned, a strange wave flashed by, and was directly transmitted into a special space.

What is waiting for him is to completely destroy the true spirit level after reading the memory thoroughly, and there is absolutely no possibility of reincarnation.

The huge inspection decree, the back of the deep light gate, and the monks who passed through the light gate hurriedly left the place, not wanting to stay for a while.

Mu Xiu, Mu Miaoling and others stood by and did not leave immediately.

"Sister, why hasn't Yundi come out yet?"

After a long time, Mu Xiu raised his head to look at the cold-looking old sister, and asked in confusion, he didn't even notice it, and his tone trembled slightly.

Yun Di followed the light gate behind them, but after so long, many cultivators had already come out, but Yun Di was still not seen.

Then naturally there is only one reason.

"build."

"Let's go, come home with me, I think it is necessary for the clan to test you again."

"This time I will personally be your examiner!"

Mu Miaoling's tone was cold, with purple awns lingering in his eyes, and a majestic momentum made Mu Xiu lower his head, his mouth moved slightly, but he couldn't say a word.

"Humph!"

Mu Miaoling snorted coldly, flicked his sleeves, turned into a stream of light, and went away in an instant. Seeing this, the guards of the old woman on the side led Mu Xiu to keep up.

Three days later, dawn.

Now it was finally Bai Donglin's turn and the others. Looking at the light gate in front of him, Bai Donglin looked calm. Although he had many secrets, he was upright and not afraid of this thing.
