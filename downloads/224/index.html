In the area of ​​the Sixth Ring Road, Bai Donglin and Shang Zhong walked side by side.

"Donglin, we are not in a hurry. You can stay with the girl for a while."

Shang Zhong smiled, Bai Donglin put away the two sachets in his hand when he heard the words, and said casually while thinking:

"I've said all that should be said, it's not a farewell to life and death. I wait for the monk, and I have a long life, and there will always be a goodbye day."

Shang Zhong shook his head slightly, this kid, he could see so clearly at a young age, and his Dao heart might be so hard.

Is there such a coincidence in the world?

Bai Donglin thought about it in his heart, and then rejected it. Although the seed of the law is rare, there are also many types. Why did he give him these two kinds of pity?

The law of tides is one of the sequence branches of the law of gravitation. If the seed of this law is planted, his perception of the law of gravitation will be greatly improved.

The seed of the law that Lianxin gave him just now is even more weird, it is actually the law of magnetic force!

He wants to fuse the law of electromagnetic force, the law of electricity already exists, and the law of magnetism is missing. Although it is not very precious, it is only a rare level, but it is not easy to find it specially.

And this pity, as if knowing his intentions, directly sent him to the door.

It is estimated that this involves the profound and profound way of destiny, and he and Lianxin’s luck may have some inexplicable intersection!

When did it start?

‘Hey, yeah, the master has found a handsome junior for you, are you happy? Was it unexpected? ’

Bai Donglin's mind flashed with laughter every year, and his old face suddenly became dark. Okay, every year, it turns out to be you, no wonder.

As time goes by, the longer the two get along, the more intense the exchange of air and luck, so this time the magnetic law that he needs more will be delivered!

Needless to say, this pity is his blessing!

But he is still very cautious about this thing. It is impossible for a pie to fall in the sky, it will cause this kind of result, and it will definitely consume certain things, such as luck.

Others might try to keep pity by their side, but in his heart he is very disgusted with this kind of thing beyond his control.

The gift given by destiny has already been secretly priced.

If you ask for it endlessly, there will be disasters. Although he is not afraid of death, he may not be able to bear his pity.

"Here, Dong Lin, if you are fine, we will return to the Holy Sect directly."

Shang Zhong's words interrupted Bai Donglin's cranky thinking, and he looked up and saw the busy cross-domain teleportation formation in front of him.

The shocking and dramatic changes in the "heart" of the Nine Rings did not affect this peripheral area, and it was still prosperous and crowded.

In the end, outside of the endless dimensional space defense at that time, surrounded by monks who came to support, those monsters naturally did not dare to actively jump over the defense and attack the outside world.

"Shang Lao, I'm naturally fine, we..."

Bai Donglin hadn't finished speaking yet, his expression was taken aback, what happened today?

One after another weird coincidence!

With a movement of mind, he took out a brass waist plate from the bracelet space, a plain waist plate, with complicated inscriptions on the back, and the word "absolute" on the front.

The brass waist plate trembled slightly, and the scarlet light of the word "Absolute" on the front shone fiercely, and a message came into Bai Donglin's mind.

Bai Donglin frowned, retracted the brass waistband, and said apologetically to Shang Zhong:

"Old Shang, I'm afraid I won't be able to accompany you back to the sect. You should go back first. I have something personal to deal with."

"I will return to the sect by myself afterwards."

Shang Zhong nodded slightly, did not care, thought for a moment and said to Bai Donglin:

"Donglin, don't forget the time, your new disciple's second entrance examination will begin in less than two years."

"With your strength, you can pass it easily, but the rules of the holy sect are strict and cannot be changed easily. If you miss this time, you have to wait another five years!"

Bai Donglin's eyes were smiling. He didn't care about this assessment at all. He had a plan for his own path, but naturally there was no need to talk to others about this kind of thing. He nodded and said:

"I can save it!"

The two bid farewell to each other and watched as Shang Zhong's figure disappeared into the cross-domain teleportation formation, Bai Dong moved into another cross-domain teleportation formation.

The closest boundary to the Moa Yanyan Desert is naturally Lei Zeyu. It is located in the east of Qianyuan Realm to the north, close to the Eastern Sea of ​​Ming, and only dozens of realms away from the wasteland where the Saint Sect of Extreme Dao is located. .

Therefore, Bai Donglin had to cross a small half of Qianyuan Continent from the western Pill Region, and the distance was extremely far, requiring dozens of trans-regional teleportation arrays.

However, according to the message from the brass waist plate, Shahai Absolute Palace will not be opened until March, and there is still plenty of time.

With a movement of his mind, he communicated with the center of the cross-domain teleportation formation, determined the route, threw a handful of spirit stones on the teleportation formation, and the spirit stones were swallowed up.

After waiting for another half a day, when the number of people reached a certain level, the formation was automatically activated, a fierce light flashed, and everyone instantly crossed the remote territory and disappeared.

...

After half a month.

Lei Zeyu, Zhaolan Giant City, Bai Donglin stepped out of the cross-domain teleportation array.

Rumble!

The black clouds pressed by the black sky were endless, and Bai Donglin couldn't see the eyes, and the violent thunder burst in the black clouds, like rolling thunder dragons.

The heavy rain poured down, and the rain curtain was continuous. This kind of rainfall made Bai Donglin smack secretly.

I looked around and found that the crowds around me turned a blind eye to this torrential rain, as if they had become accustomed to it.

Everyone walking on the street is surrounded by a thin mask, shielding the terrifying rain from the outside.

This mask is just a small thing, not even a magic weapon, even mortals can use it. Although this thing is ordinary, it is the most indispensable thing for mortals living in Lei Zeyu.

Lei Zeyu, three hundred and sixty-five days a year, three hundred and sixty days are rainstorms, and the other five days are also cloudy.

The reason why there is such an abnormal climate is all due to Lei Yin's awe-inspiring great mountain range, which spans more than a dozen boundaries.

Intercepting and converging the ocean currents from the East Polar Sea resulted in the formation of the Moanyan Desert on one side of the mountain range and the never-ending rainstorm in Lei Zeyu on the other side. Two completely different extreme climates.

With the monks' ability to move mountains and seas, it is not difficult to change this extreme climate, just split Lei Yin's great mountain range!

However, this area is full of sects who are good at thunder and water. They love this kind of climate, and naturally there is no reason to go against it.

"Didn't it mean that the Moan Flame Desert was formed by the gods weeping blood, the gods fell to the ground, and the fire burned?"

"Is the legend wrong? Or is there another hidden story?"

Bai Donglin shook his head slightly. This is a big world. The years are endless, and there are countless legends and secrets. Although his knowledge is like a sea, he is not omniscient and omnipotent.

Looking up at the thunder dragon tumbling fiercely among the dark clouds, Bai Donglin showed a slight movement in his eyes, stepping out, and he was still domineering at the end of a long time, and his figure appeared in the thunder sea in an instant.

As soon as his mind moved, the law of gold floated slightly, Bai Donglin's skin and clothes instantly turned into metal, and his long hair also turned into metal, towering high.

The law, the heavens and the earth, are displayed at the same time. The larger the volume, the more thunder can naturally be attracted. The huge body of more than 300 feet stretches its limbs in the clouds.

Humanoid lightning rod achievement reached!

"Mom, look, there is a giant of light in the sky!"
