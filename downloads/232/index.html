A tragic clearing, but in a flash.

Regarding Fang Fang Miaomiao's outrageous action, except for a few people who were compassionate, everyone else was cold-eyed. Even if Fang Fang Miaomiao didn't take action, they could not say they would do it.

How can the ants wait to pick up the bargain when the beasts fight each other?

There are always people who don’t know the heights of the sky and the earth, who have read too many biographies, thinking that they can be a fisherman, but they don’t know that clearing the ground before the war has become an unwritten rule.

Those who have the strength should stay, those who have no strength should be killed, and those who should be gone.

Thousands of monks were shocked by their breath when the Absolute Palace was born.

Fang Fang Miaomiao shot a part to kill, and another part was taken away. There are only a hundred monks left.

They are all amazing, talented, and enchanting people with trump cards.

Turning out the Qianlong Young Phoenix Ranking, you can find the names of all these hundred people on it.

Except for the alien of Bai Donglin.

Shoo! The figure of the woman in Tsing Yi flickered, standing in the void, with awe-inspiring killing intent in her eyes, staring at Fang Miao Miao firmly.

"What do you look at? Are you envious of this girl's body better than you?"

Fang Fang Miaomiao got up and stood with his hands on his hips. The waves were suddenly rough and beautiful.

Hiss~Guru!

Bai Donglin trembles his fingers and looks around, who is it? My cultivator, doesn't even have this concentration?

Such a shameful voice!

It's really insulting to gentleness, and shame the men's faces!

"You..."

The woman in Tsing Yi raised her eyebrows and her body was trembling slightly with anger. She was stunning, beautiful, and had a superb temperament, and she was unparalleled in beauty. She had never paid special attention to such meaningless things as her figure.

So compared with Fang Fang Miaomiao, her figure is indeed a bit ordinary.

Although a monk can easily reshape his body, the proud girl of heaven will not deceive himself.

"What are you?"

"Qing Yuqing, I will teach you a little lesson this time, and take care of your own mouth in the future!"

‘Hmph, stinky woman, dare to laugh at this girl’s height, don’t let me take the chance, otherwise I will chop off your legs, and I will give you some material, so that you will never recover your body! ’

Cang Dang!

A loud noise ran through the world, shattering countless clouds, hundreds of enchanting geniuses with different thoughts, their hearts trembled, and they couldn't help but cast their eyes on the dark palace.

This loud noise seemed to explode on the heart, making their blood boil.

Click! The huge and extremely dark metal door opened slowly, extremely slowly, as if the weight of this door surpassed a world!

The height of a door is tens of thousands of miles. Through the opening of the door, it is like seeing another world.

The expressions of everyone were shocked, and Bai Donglin's pupils shrank slightly. Although this pitch-black palace was very similar to the palace he saw when he first entered the Shahai Palace.

But the power of terror displayed now, no matter from which aspect, are two completely different concepts.

Moreover, the gate of the palace was not opened at the beginning, they just penetrated a layer of light film, and now I think they did not enter the palace at all at that time, just wandering outside.

Clang!

There was another loud noise, the dark door was completely opened, and the movement stopped.

Behind the giant gate, there is a whirlpool passage that rotates slightly, colorful, deep and blue, and I don't know where it leads to.

Huh!

The golden-winged roc bird, which has been hovering at a very high point in the sky, screamed, and just watched everything that happened below.

The golden pupils of the Golden Winged Roc Bird are full of royal pride, which makes it disdain to have too much contact with the monks below.

Moreover, the relationship between the demon race and the human race is not as good as that.

In particular, there are monks who serve monster beasts below, making it even more displeased.

Huh!

With a long wingspan, the glittering golden wings, with a slight movement, instantly turned into golden light, and flew away into the whirlpool.

The golden-winged roc bird is well-known for its extreme speed. Many monks have not even captured its flight path, and the opponent has already arrived in front of the whirlpool channel.

Hum!

Amidst the humming sound, a strange wave swept across, and the golden-winged Dapeng bird and the woman on its back showed a brass waist plate with red glow on each.

The waist card collapsed into a bunch of light spots, wrapped around his body, the vortex channel was slightly rippling, and the golden-winged roc bird entered smoothly.

Someone made the first step, and hundreds of monks didn't dare to stay longer. The inheritance test inside didn't know what it was.

Hundreds of streamers crossed this not short distance in an instant, and the bizarre fluctuations shone fiercely, and the brass waist plates of the people instantly collapsed into light spots, encumbering the people through the vortex passage.

Bang!

The colorful giant python under Fang Miaomiao bumped its head against the illusory whirlpool passage and made a loud noise.

Hissing ~ The colorful giant python's pupils were cold, and stood upright, vomiting colorful venom, and fiercely hit the vortex passage, banging.

Fang Fang Miaomiao's expression changed, and he shouted in a low voice, "Aji, stop!"

The colorful python was extremely obedient, and stopped when he heard the words, the cold vertical pupils still stared at the vortex passage.

Fang Miaomiao's slender eyebrows wrinkled slightly, she naturally had more than one brass waist badge, she also prepared one for her Aji.

It's a pity that things are not as simple as she thought. If you want to pass the whirlpool channel, having a waist card is only one of the conditions, and there is also a restriction, that is, the age cannot exceed one thousand.

This news is not recorded in the secret book of the clan.

Her Aji, who had just passed his one hundred thousand birthday last month, naturally did not meet this requirement.

Fang Miaomiao bent over and gently stroked the huge scales of the colorful giant python, the blue light in his eyes kept flowing, and he whispered:

"Aji, you are waiting for me outside, I will go in alone."

"If I didn't come out, you would kill all the people who came out of the dark palace! Do you understand?"

Hiss~

The colorful giant python roared up to the sky, and among the huge vertical pupils, the awe-inspiring killing intent was the essence.

Fang Miaomiao's figure moved, turned into a ray of blue light, and instantly disappeared into the whirlpool passage. The colorful giant python's eyes condensed, and it disappeared into the depths of the earth to hide.

But while breathing, the figures outside the pitch-black palace almost disappeared, leaving only two figures standing in the void.

Bai Donglin's expression moved slightly, and slightly surrendered to the purple-robed Taoist not far away, and asked loudly, "Lei Yuanzi, don't you go in?"

Lei Yuanzi was taken aback for a moment, looked at Bai Donglin who was not in a hurry, and shook his head and said: "I am old, I am old, and the poor age does not meet the requirements. If you want to go in, you can't go in."

"It's the little brother you~www.mtlnovel.com~ They all rushed into the palace, why are you so calm? Are you not afraid of being picked up first?"

Bai Donglin stretched out his hand to take off his hood, and looked at the slightly rotating Qingyou whirlpool passage, with white light flowing in his eyes, slowly speaking:

"If it's destined to be mine, naturally you won't be afraid that others will take the lead; if it's not mine, you can grab it."

After all, Bai Donglin stepped forward, and the golden light spot lingered around him, instantly crossing the vortex passage.

Lei Yuanzi stagnated.

That Tianzun Wuliang, his grandma's, this kid seems to have a bit of truth to say!

However, it is a bit too domineering.

The aura of Absolute Palace changed drastically, Lei Yuanzi's expression changed slightly, and he quietly retreated.
