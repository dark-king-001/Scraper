Yin Tong's expression was solemn, and the silver light gleamed all over his body.

Huh, it's just a tool, why do you pull it?

But the problem is not big, he is immortal and afraid of a hammer, and the most depressed and unhappy in his heart must be the monks around him.

Except for a few people who seemed to have expected it, the expressions of other people were a bit ugly. The chance of passing the assessment was too low, and the death rate was too high. If you add up a little in your mind, it is clear that this trip is really a big loss!
