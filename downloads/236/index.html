Then he glanced at the Fang Fang Miaomiao in the distance, and his eyebrows were slightly frowned. This person's luck was even better than her.

The sound of dragon yin and phoenix screamed faintly in his body, his eyes condensed, Mei Hongying quickly suppressed the unrest in his heart, calmed his breath, and the scream of dragon yin and phoenix slowly disappeared.

‘The conflict between the souls of dragon and phoenix is ​​getting stronger and stronger. Now even the fierce ups and downs of emotions can cause restlessness. This time, you must obtain the inheritance of the Wandering Emperor’s Gas Dual Cultivation! ’

‘Otherwise, let’s not talk about further progress in the cultivation base, now it’s even more difficult to maintain the balance in the body. ’

Dragon and Phoenix are one, fellow practitioners of gas!

‘My Mei Hongying will never stop here, not only the inheritance of the wandering emperor, but also the secret storage of the void ancestor dragon and the nether ancient phoenix, everything is mine! ’

‘Whoever wants to stop me, I’ll kill someone! ’

With awe-inspiring eyes, Mei Hongying stepped out, his gaze swept across the area where Bai Donglin was, looking at Bai Donglin, who had been completely submerged by the pitch-black beetle.

She was extremely impressed with Bai Donglin. She had died in front of her but suddenly resurrected, and she had also lifted the blood curse she had cast.

This strange guy not only won the brass medal, but also made such a big improvement in a short period of time. It seems that like her, he has also won the bad luck!

She can remember very clearly that this person was just a mortal who hadn't stepped into cultivation at first, but his body was extremely powerful.

In less than ten years, he has grown to the point where he competes with the enchanting genius. Without the chance, it would never be possible.

Maybe you can take a look at this person's chance, Mei Hongying's eyes flickered, and she, who has tasted the sweetness, is naturally extremely interested in such things as chance.

I hope you don't die in the mouth of these bugs, otherwise it would be a pity.

Withdrawing his gaze, Mei Hongying stepped into the square area with flickering light.

Pouch!

Feeling itchy nostrils, Bai Donglin sneezed and sprayed countless black beetles.

"What's the matter? Which beauty is thinking that I can't make it?"

Naturally, he was not narcissistic and random thinking. Just now, his intuition fluctuated and he sensed that someone was malicious in his heart. The two spiritual orifices engraved with the law of prophecy and foresight also flickered slightly.

Under the pairwise comparison, it is natural to understand that the change just now is not an illusion.

"Hehe, interesting, I don't want to cheat people, you should burn incense and worship the Buddha, you dare to pay attention to me!"

Bai Donglin's deep black eyeballs turned slightly, and the pitch-black beetle crawling around couldn't block his sight, and swept the crowd quietly.

Although no abnormalities were found, he was not anxious in his heart. He would naturally make a move if he wanted to make it.

Time has passed for a long time, except for Bai Donglin, no one has stayed in the first square area anymore. With fast speed or luck, they have already taken four or five steps and entered the fifth area.

Bai Donglin didn't look impatient or impatient. This silver boy didn't have a time limit. As long as he could live on the throne of the sun, he was not anxious at all. He needed all the energy for assessment, cultivation, and energy!

Don't be greedy, how can you become stronger!

Rustle!

A dense torrent of beetles flooded into Bai Donglin's body one after another. When he swallowed hundreds of millions of beetles, the cave on the void ground slowly closed, as if the beetles had been exhausted.

After a while, the beetle that had broken its source was quickly swallowed, and Baidong’s battered flesh breath healed as before, the red glow flashed, and the tattered black robe returned to its original state.

"Yes, not bad. These little things are a little smaller, but they are also a bit fleshy. The spirits and spirits have improved a little."

If you don't accumulate steps, you can't reach thousands of miles, so your strength is accumulated little by little.

Nodded in satisfaction, Bai Donglin got up and stepped out. The same square area was instantly cut out under his feet, and the colors flashed alternately.

Bai Donglin looked back, and the dark area before him was not far behind him. He had used it just now, and it seemed that he could only walk past it step by step.

Although I have seen this phenomenon in other people, I still have to try it myself before reconciling it.

Looking at the shining Sun Throne in the distant galaxy, the distance between them is not short!

The colors stopped alternately, a scarlet light shone in the square area, and the entire void ground was bloody.

‘Extremely fierce, karma burns! ’

Six characters lingering in scarlet flames slowly floated into the void, dazzling the eyes.

Bai Donglin flicked his fingers, his face darkened, he had observed carefully just now, besides him, there was no second person who had been "serious" twice in a row!

And this "extremely fierce" is obviously a higher level of torture.

He wasn't scared, he just felt a little strange. Is his luck so bad?

This wandering emperor's assessment, he had already experienced in the initial peripheral trials, and there were places to test his luck everywhere.

The assessment here is also the same. Those with luck against the sky may go all the way to the throne of the Sun.

He glanced over the silver boy who was located at a very high altitude, and then shook his head slightly. This silver boy should not have the right to intervene in the assessment, otherwise he wouldn't need a boring assessment for 190 million years.

Then the problem lies with him.

Frowning thinking, there was a glimmer of enlightenment in his eyes, and the only strange thing that happened to him recently was the gift of pity.

Sure enough, he didn't think so badly. There will be no pie in the sky. No, the price will come!

That is, he, if he is someone else, he has to peel off his skin if he doesn't die!

Bai Donglin's expression moved and he sensed the strange changes in his body, converged his thoughts and calmed down, sitting cross-legged.

He had only heard of something like Karma Huo, but had never seen it before.

There is a saying in Buddhism that karma fire refers to evil karma that harms the body like fire, and it also refers to the fire that burns sinners in hell.

Bai Donglin was silent for a moment, and slightly estimated that the number of people who died directly or indirectly at his hands in the past ten years is estimated to be no more than ten million.

No, Bai Donglin's mind flashed through the fifty-nine floors of the black prison that had been swallowed by the great sun's sacred fire.

If you want to get to the bottom of it, the sun fell because of him, then those people would be considered to have died indirectly in his hands.

In this way, he didn't count how many people died because of him.

Although they are all damned people, he kills with peace of mind, but the Buddhist teachings are old-fashioned, but without this saying, killing is killing, and there is no distinction between good and evil.

Presumably, this karmic fire will come very fierce!

Bear!

Before the thoughts fell, the black and red flames that he did not know were surging out from the inside out, and instantly enveloped Bai Donglin.

The raging fire of karma rushed several feet high, and the terrifying high temperature instantly turned his hair and clothes into nothingness.

Flesh and skin, bones and internal organs, Bai Donglin felt that every cell particle in his body was burned by the fiery flame.

Not only that, in the sea of ​​gods, the three souls sitting cross-legged, from the inside to the outside, are also blazing karma.

What made Bai Donglin a little surprised was that the pitch-black stone stele under him showed no response at all, and this karma is really not easy.

Bai Donglin frowned, and as the karma burned, a pain that surpassed the past instantly drowned him.

It is indeed a flame specially used to punish sinners. The additional pain is really strong enough, and the pain principle in the body shines with unprecedented intensity, and the pain principle within it seems to have eaten hormones and grows wildly.

According to the rumors, the deeper the sin of the person burned by the karma fire, the darker the color of the karma fire, and the more severe the pain.

And in his body, fueled by endless guilt and resentment, the burning karmic fire is already red and black!

‘Sure enough, this uncle is a wicked person! ’

As soon as his mind moved, the law of fire within his body flickered slightly, and the fire-controlling magical power was used. He forcibly caught a trace of the black and red karma and stuffed it into the spirit orifice.

Puff, Bai Donglin seemed to hear the abnormal sound of the burning and penetration of the Ling Aperture.

His law of fire spirit is still too weak, this law of fire has never been the focus of his cultivation, and this karma fire is also very special, not a single law of fire, it also contains other things.

Bai Donglin frowned and thought, sensing the body and soul that were constantly being burned into nothingness, and the damage to the soul by this karma far exceeded the damage to the body.

Burning the flesh seems to be only incidental damage, if it weren't for his extremely powerful spirit, I'm afraid it would be a bit unbearable.

It's a shame to throw away such a powerful and good thing.

"Never mind, Babuzhi's "Imperial" is about to get started, this time I will do an experiment in advance at that time, it's just a waste of a little spiritual energy."

"Just accumulate experience in advance, there is only one seed for the law of dimensional space!"

With the movement of the mind, the three divine souls merged into one in an instant, and the blue and purple clear spirit ball was drawn out from the depths of the divine sea.

The mind-changing knife gently cut some Qingyou debris, and before the fragrance diffused, Shenhun swallowed it in one bite.

A few fragments were also condensed from a trace of clear spirit, and the effect was still against the sky. Bai Donglin instantly fell into an epiphany, and the endless pain of karma was forgotten by him.

The Babu "Imperial", one of the eight cores of the Heaven-defying Battle Array, masters the way of dominance, and can dominate all the laws of energy in the human body.

It is extremely mysterious and powerful, and the difficulty of its cultivation has surpassed the "Truth of Pregnancy God Casting Demon" without assistance.

After all, the golden-red horn stele is not like the black stele, it has the magical function of helping others to practice.

The power of the Eighth Heaven's Soul erupted with all strength, and the enhanced energy and karma were wrapped in entanglement and blending.

After the "Imperial" was used, a strange wave contained both. Under the cover of the strange wave, the Fire Spirit Aperture gleamed slightly, and subtle changes had taken place on it.

All these manipulations are extremely complicated, and he can only do it when he swallows the clear spirit.

With the participation of enhanced energy and "imperial", the devoured karma fire did not burn through the spirit orifice, but was fused into the purple-gold flame within the spirit orifice.

This method is indeed feasible!

I was happy in my heart. This was not a whim. Now the joy is not because of swallowing the fire of karma, but because of the feasibility of his plan.

Converge your thoughts and devour the fire of karma with all your concentration.

With the beginning, it will follow naturally, and the karmic fire will form a whirlpool, and all will be poured into the fire orifice.
