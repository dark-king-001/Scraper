The temple of the town demon, the thorny road leading to the star of the galaxy throne, the square areas of different colors, shining dazzlingly.

A large group of red and black karma was burning. Bai Donglin sat cross-legged in the karma fire, pinching his hands, and his expression was calm.

This fierce flame, paid for by sin and resentment, seemed to be endless, vowing to never stop without burning Bai Donglin's sins of killing.

Both the body and the soul suffered continuous terrifying damage at the same time, and the enhanced energy was unprecedentedly fierce.

Bai Donglin usually because the spirit is too powerful, and the attack that can cause damage to the spirit is also very rare, so he uses the energy to increase the energy to do it by hurting the body.

At this time, the two are going on at the same time, and the damage caused by the reversal of the Eighth Heaven Divine Soul is extremely huge, and the strengthening energy is surging and extremely rich.

Multitasking, a part of the strengthening energy is used to swallow the karmic fire, most of the energy is absorbed by the soul, the law of pain shining through the spirit orifice, and the law of pain is engraved very smoothly under the extreme pain.

After a long time, the turbulent red and black karma fire gradually became thinner, swaying slightly, and then like a candle in the wind until it extinguished.

Bai Donglin's body and soul, which had been burned into nothingness, recovered in an instant. He slowly opened his eyes and spread his palms. The fire spirit inside his body flickered slightly, and a red and purple flame spewed out.

Under the control of the Vulcan Power, the red-violet flame splits into red-black, purple, gold, and three-color flames. When the mind moves, the flame instantly condenses into a lotus flower. Spread out.

"Buddha's anger lotus? Haha, shouldn't you have to pay copyright fees?"

A hint of smile appeared in Bai Donglin's eyes, and he shook his palms, the fire lotus collapsed, and traces of flame were sucked into his body again.

This kind of attack method formed by mixed heterogeneous flames can be easily done with his powerful spirit, not to mention three kinds of flames, even ten thousand kinds of flames are easy.

After all, it is not a complete integration. It only needs to maintain a delicate balance. It is also a product of the law of fire. Compared with his "primordial", the difficulty is not known how many times lower.

He also doesn't need this kind of attack method. The ultimate move of the "primitive" sequence is more powerful and has greater potential.

The reason why he spent a lot of effort to devour the fire of karma was just because of its restraint on the spirits.

A faint color flashed in Bai Donglin's eyes, and he felt that his body and spirit seemed to be lighter, as if some invisible substance was consumed forever.

"Could it be that the statement of sin and resentment is true? And the burning of karma just now has completely eliminated these hidden dangers in my body?"

Bai Donglin pondered slightly for a moment, and he affirmed this possibility. After all, he could clearly sense the inexplicable relaxation in his body.

‘It’s no wonder that the pitch-black stele that suppressed Shenhai didn’t respond to the Karma Fire attack. It’s not that it failed, but that it’s good for me. ’

The step of devouring the karmic fire was the right step. Bai Donglin secretly decided that after killing and setting fire in the future, he would cause the karmic fire to burn him inside and out again, so as to prevent sins and grievances from entering the body and leaving unknown hidden dangers.

I got up and stood, looked around, and found that I was far behind.

The other people have taken seven or eight steps at the slowest pace, and they are all going all out in order to pass the assessment.

He was the only one who slowed down behind him, vowing not to stop without eating all the benefits.

Without thinking about it, Bai Donglin stepped forward and stepped into the third square area, ready to continue being attacked in his heart.

The light of different colors alternated rapidly, slowly stopped, and finally stopped in the color of white jade.

‘No, it’s very peaceful! ’

The four large characters lingering with jade light slowly floated into the void, causing Bai Donglin to be taken aback for a moment. This thing doesn't play the card according to the routine!

It doesn't matter to him whether it is good or bad, anyway, whether it is reward or punishment, he will be forcibly turned into a growth resource, but the latter will cause his progress in the assessment to be slower.

"No, it seems that I have been transferred, or that the impact of pity on me has ended."

The two seeds of the law are indeed very important to him, and the two dangerous criminal laws just now are not simple. If the principle of equivalent exchange is followed, the two will be offset.

Fujitailai's four lingering large characters of jade light merged into one and turned into a ball of light, and a pitch-black ball the size of a head fell out of the ball of light.

Bai Donglin quickly stretched out his hand to take it, his arm sank slightly, his face changed, and a hint of surprise flashed in his eyes.

So heavy!

"This thing is..."

Bai Donglin's eyes fell on the pitch-black sphere, and he observed carefully. On the pitch-black surface, there were faint fine golden lines winding and spreading. Various kinds of information flashed through his mind, and a name appeared.

Black meteor gold!

An extremely precious material, one of the main materials of the black knife death omen is this thing. This is the heart of the refiner. When refining the tool, only a small piece needs to be added to greatly increase the hardness and toughness of the magic weapon.

Not only that, in addition to refining artifacts, body cultivation contains "Essential Artifacts" when in the Divine Origin Realm. If you add Black Meteorite Gold, you can also improve its quality.

He can still remember that before entering the military world, Duo Baofeng's Nine Miss was asking for this thing at a high price.

Bai Donglin slapped the black meteorite gold in his hand. It is indeed a treasure that everyone is eager for. His head is so big that it weighs more than one million tons.

Don't talk about using this thing to refine it into a magic weapon, just use it to smash people, and its power is not weak.

Nodded with satisfaction, and stuffed the Black Meteorite Gold into the storage space. This thing can be used for personal use or traded.

This vagrant emperor is really fat!

It is very valuable to give out the same reward casually. Bai Donglin's eyes flickered slightly, and he became more and more interested in this inheritance.

As soon as the figure moved, it continued to step into the fourth square area.

‘Little fierce, five thunders are on top! ’

Bai Donglin was not surprised. Although his luck had returned to normal, it was a test, and naturally it was more fortunate than fortune.

Through his observations, the number of auspicious appearances will decrease as far back as possible, and the monks in the front are all refreshingly fierce.

Rumble!

The dark clouds gathered, and the purple thunder was shining. Before the thunder fell, Bai Dong came and shot into the clouds without retreating.

Swallowing the sky and the earth, with a move of mind, the purple thunder converged and the clouds were all swallowed into the abdomen.

Bai Donglin discovered that this level of assessment could threaten him more than the big one, and allow him to spend time to squeeze its value, but the big and small ones can only tickle him, and they can't use much energy.

Too lazy to waste time, directly use drastic means to get rid of them one by one.

The fifth square area is fierce and poisonous!

The sixth square area, Xiao Ji...

The seventh square area, fierce...

"Woo~hoo~"

Fang Fang Miaomiao panted slightly, sweat faintly appeared on her white forehead, and a few strands of long hair wet with sweat stuck to her cheeks.

Looking back, there were a hundred strips of different colors, and the shining light strips spread to the end of the line of sight.

The light bands are uneven, with different lengths, some light bands are still slowly extending, and some light bands have completely stopped moving.

Fang Fang Miaomiao knew in his heart that these things that hadn't moved for a long time meant that the examiner on it had died.

Retracting her gaze, this is an assessment of the wandering emperor's inheritance. The dead man couldn't be more normal. Even in the next square area, she would die on the spot, and Fang Fang would not have a hint of surprise in her heart.

Her luck is already very good, she has met more auspicious than fierce, and has been leading the way.

It's a pity that it gets harder as you get to the back, especially...

Fang Fang Miaomiao raised his head and looked up, like a galaxy close at hand, the shining sun thrones, radiating light and heat all the time.

With Fang Miaomiao's cultivation base, he has already repaired his body without leakage. There is no pore on the crystal-like jade skin, and sweat was forced to evaporate. One can imagine how hot the temperature here is.

Under the throne of the Great Sun, success is within easy reach, but in addition to facing all kinds of weird horrors in the square area, he also has to face the oppression of the Great Sun over his head.

Fang Miaomiao's gaze condensed, and his hand pinched Fa Jue, a crystal blue orb was sacrificed, suspended on her head, slowly rotating, and constantly spilling blue light chips.

These guys are not simple, they are the biggest obstacle to her inheritance!
