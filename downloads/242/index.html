In a huge metal base, the towering walls are full of various fort weapons.

A team of neatly organized humanoid machines patrolled back and forth, and several spaceships full of technological sense were suspended in the air.

If it weren't for the harsh air, the layers of black beetle corpses piled up outside the wall, and the desolate surface in the distance, Bai Donglin thought he had come to the world of future technology.

In the spacious and bright hall, surrounded by silver-white metal walls, Bai Donglin was sitting on a metal table and chairs, and on the metal table in front of him was a cup of green unknown liquid, still slightly hot.

The living room was extremely simple, and Bai Donglin didn't care. He already understood that on this planet, there were only two civilizations with unity consciousness at the core.

And they are hostile to each other.

Naturally, there is no possibility of guests coming to visit. These tables and chairs are slightly hot, obviously they have just been cut out.

With the skill to make these useless things, the core consciousness of the civilization of the machine side is definitely willing to use these metals to make robots.

"Hello, sir, I am the heart of the machine, the highest will of the machine civilization on this planet. I don't know if you land on the red star, where can I need my help?"

The robot sitting on the opposite side of the table still asked in a soft voice, both inside and out, exactly the same as the robot he had crushed just now.

This is easy to understand. In a civilization with a unified consciousness, the will to dominate can come to every individual. The robots who greeted him, the robots patrolling outside, and even every mechanical item in this base are clones of the dominating will.

It can be controlled by will at any time, or you can use the programmed smart ai to let it run automatically.

Bai Donglin, who has memories of past lives, still knows a little bit about these things.

Bai Donglin frowned slightly, and did not respond to the opponent, but was thinking about the significance of this assessment.

Imprisoning his cultivation is obviously not a test of his strength, and these two collective consciousness civilizations have been thoroughly studied by the wandering emperor, so he is not required to do research.

Domination, war, Bai Donglin's eyes flashed with a gleam of enlightenment, and he knew the meaning of the assessment.

Is this to test his war leadership ability?

In this way, among the wandering emperor's Jiufen heritage, one is related to the battlefield, perhaps the battlefield or something.

If you want to distinguish which type of inheritance many examiners are suitable for, without knowing their personality, talents, etc., you naturally need to use different types of evaluation content to test.

Through continuous assessment, many routes continue to converge to the final Jiufen inheritance. While selecting outstanding inheritors, it can also determine which inheritance is suitable for them to make the best use of the material.

And above the red star, these two civilizations have been studied by the wandering emperor for a long time. The strength of the two sides can be said to be evenly matched, and it is indeed very suitable to test the war leadership ability of the examiner.

Moreover, this assessment is not without danger. For other assessors, the cultivation level is completely imprisoned. If they are not handled properly and do not take this assessment seriously, there is still a great chance of death.

The two civilizations still have some means. The mortal body can't hold it. The battlefield is changing rapidly, and all kinds of things can happen.

Bai Donglin wanted to understand the meaning of the assessment, and his expression suddenly fell into a trough.

He is not interested in fighting at all, he is dedicated to the ultimate strength of the individual, even if he has obtained one of the eight battlefields against the sky, "Yu" is only used to improve his combat power.

I have never looked for the other seven cores in the past to form the idea of ​​a battle against the sky.

This assessment didn't go to his heart, so naturally there was no need to force himself. This was also the purpose of the wandering emperor's selection and inheritance, just follow his heart.

But this assessment must be completed before the next step of assessment can be continued.

If so, just choose one. Bai Donglin's eyes fell on the opposite mechanical body. This mechanical civilization is clean and polite to him, but it makes him very fond of it.

In contrast, the slimy bugs hiding in the ground are obviously much worse.

After all, Bai Donglin is still a normal person, and his aesthetics are also very normal.

After making the decision, Bai Donglin, who just wanted to settle everything as soon as possible, entered the next level of assessment, and said directly:

"I have come to Red Star for only one purpose, to help you solve the bugs in the ground!"

When the robot heard the words, the characters flashed across the dark crystal light curtain, as if performing a fierce calculation. After a while, he said:

"Dear powerhouse, thank you very much for your help, but I have a request, that is, when you make a move, please don't completely destroy this planet!"

"I am sealed on this planet by a powerful energy. I cannot enter the void of the universe. If the red star is destroyed, my civilization will also die."

There was a hint of excitement in the robot's tone, and then it turned to worry. After speaking, he looked at Bai Donglin with a little nervousness, with a little expectation, as if waiting for Bai Donglin to do it.

Bai Donglin looked at him, a little embarrassed. Obviously, the master consciousness of this mechanical civilization did not know that his cultivation level had been imprisoned. Maybe when he came, he had been observed by what means, so he had some doubts about his original strength. A little understanding.

If the cultivation base is not imprisoned, he can indeed easily destroy this star, he only needs to detonate a "primordial" bomb in the center of the earth.

It doesn't need to be too strong. With the size of this planet twenty-nine times the size of a blue star, it only needs the fusion of the four laws to completely destroy the center of the earth.

Having said that, the degree of evolution of this dominance consciousness is very high, and it has already possessed such obvious emotional changes.

"Cough cough cough, mechanical ruler, you don't have to worry, because for some special reasons, I can't use my power to help you. To completely solve those bugs, I still need your power."

"So, you don't need to worry that I will destroy the Red Star."

Bai Donglin's plan was simple. He went to the center of the earth by himself and directly carried out the beheading operation. He didn't have the time to lead these mechanical soldiers and insects.

Bai Donglin discussed with the master consciousness for a long time, and finally took six fist-sized silver-gray metal **** and quickly left the base in a spaceship.

On the spaceship, Bai Dong stood beside him with a thick pitch-black mace, playing with a metal ball in his hand.

Don't underestimate this gadget, this thing is the highest scientific and technological achievement of this mechanical civilization, the most powerful weapon, called anti-matter extinguishing bomb.

Bai Donglin only needs to send this thing into the Earth Core Worm Nest, and only two pieces are needed to completely send the other's mother to the sky.

Prepare six, just in case.

According to the Domination Consciousness, this thing is very difficult to make, and there are only ten in total.

By directly taking out six, he already believes in Bai Donglin very much. Of course, it is more of a fear of his terrifying power than to believe it.

call out!

The spacecraft was extremely fast, and it didn't take long before it flew over a huge cave.

Bai Donglin carefully put the metal ball into the special metal belt around his waist, and raised the mace next to him with one hand.

Rumble!

Numerous bone spurs, plasma, and venom spewed out, enveloping the spacecraft in an instant.

The dark blue energy shield shone fiercely, but it was completely shattered after holding on to it in an instant. Under countless attacks, the spacecraft was torn into pieces, and in the fierce explosion, it turned into a ball of fire.

Huh! A figure broke through the flames and landed quickly. The arms torn by the explosion grew out in an instant, the burned skin quickly recovered, the bone spurs deep in the body were squeezed out by the muscles, and the wound healed instantly.

Shoo! Countless intensive attacks came oncoming, Bai Donglin was holding a thick mace in his backhand and blocking him in front of him. The characteristics, weight and hardness, were specially created for him by Bai Donglin and let the master.

Clang clanging, the sound of metal clashing was endless, and the exposed body was constantly torn apart, but the mace was still intact.

Blessed by the weight of the mace, Bai Donglin fell extremely fast, skimming over the cliffs of the dark cave, and in the dense caves on the surrounding rock walls, countless insects fired frantically, but they were all useless.

boom!

A huge roar sounded at the bottom of the cave, and Bai Donglin, who had fallen into a puddle of mud, was resurrected with blood in an instant, carrying a mace on his shoulder, step by step out of the huge pit filled with smoke.

A pair of cold eyes looked around, and the horror will spread madly.

Under the pressure of will, the ferocious insects did not dare to step forward for a while.
