Without special means, entering this huge maze can only become a headless fly, not to mention finding a hiding place for the mother's body.

It's a pity that all this is in vain. An immortal Qianyuan Realm's top ordinary martial artist is enough to crush these conventional combat power on this weak gravity planet.

The status of martial artist is second, and the most important thing is the extremely high nature of Qianyuan Realm creature.

With abundant spiritual energy and terrifying gravity blessing, the creatures of Qianyuan Realm were already a step ahead when they were still sperm and egg cells.
