The assessment content of this node flashed in Bai Donglin's mind. He had been busy chasing and killing Jialou Jie before, and had not had the time to pay attention to the so-called turbid and clear air.

But when he searched for the traces of Mei Hongying just now, he had already pierced through this small world, but found two strange places.

The whole body rippled slightly in the void, striding across the space, and instantly came to the sky above the sky.

At the junction with the void of the universe, that is, under the barrier of the virtual world, there is a bunch of strange lights shining.

Bai Donglin glanced vigilantly at the imaginary barrier not far away, and a faint sense of crisis haunted his mind. This is the place where the wandering emperor has placed a ban. He has tried it a long time ago, and he will die if he touches it!

Yintong was restricted from interfering in the assessment process, and this ban should have played a big role.

After the assessment is initiated by Yintong, it runs automatically from beginning to end.

As one of the seven cores of the Temple of Demon Suppression, Yin Tong also obeyed the rules in this way.

Withdrawing his gaze, he didn't care about these things, but it was related to whether his immortal ability was exposed to Yin Tong. At present, it should be beneficial to him.

A dark and turbid deep pool, suspended in the hot magma of the earth's core, lingered in bursts of black mist, completely isolating the surrounding heat.

He has his own Scripture against Heaven, and at the beginning he didn't think about changing the inheritance of the wandering emperor. He just wanted to get some benefits as much as possible.

"Hehe, these assessments are really amazing. I have unknowingly figured out my talent, the extremely powerful soul, and the ‘imperial’ who has already started to master, it’s really enchanting in the control of his own power."

He is not bragging, he is clear about his own strength, and he doesn't need self-deception and humility.

It is still a bit difficult to blend this turbid and clear air, Bai Donglin estimated that it would take two or three days.

A strong inheritance usually takes a lot of time, move faster and should be able to catch up with Mei Hongying.

"Hey, I am the king of the way of the soul, and this way of energy fusion must be the most powerful way of doing nothing!"

"Amitabha, goodness, goodness."

Wuwei's expression remained cold and silent, and since the most evil was separated, his temper became closer to the road.

He ignored Bai Dong's schizophrenic and self-talking jokes from time to time.

The yin and yang Tai Chi picture shoots out from the center of the eyebrows, and grows in the wind.

With a movement of the mind, the turbid and clear air flew into the Taiji diagram under the envelop of the power of the soul.

The turbid gas falls into the cathode, and the clean gas falls into the anode.

With the movement of "Royal", a strange force began to push the Tai Chi diagram.

The Tai Chi diagram rotates fast, getting faster and faster, and the two black and white fish are connected end to end, slowly blending, and gradually turning into a gray area.

Two days later.

The Yin-Yang Tai Chi diagram shrank fiercely, and then shot into the Shenhai, returning to the depths of Wuwei's eyes, leaving only a cloud of gray energy suspended in the air.

Bai Donglin spread out his palm, pressing the silver dice mark toward the gray energy group, and the mark flashed lightly, swallowing it instantly.

The mind flicked, and the silver dice was rolled one last time.

‘Result of the roll: Fifth Legacy! ’

The golden beam of light rose into the sky, and Bai Donglin turned into countless spots of light, and followed the beam of light to the depths of the void.

The dazzling beam of light swiftly across the void, away from the galaxy where the stars are shining, and into the darkness.

A ray of light suddenly appeared in the front, very eye-catching in the dark starry sky, and the beam of light fell straight to the bright place.

The beam of light fell on the dark ground, igniting circles of light shavings, and a human-shaped light and shadow instantly turned from virtual to real, stepping out of the diffuse light spot.

A faint of shock flashed through Bai Dong's eyes with the white light streamers. This was a dark continent with invisible edges, and there was no color on the bare surface.

Under the observation of the sky and the earth, a huge pyramid formed by the condensation of rich golden light stands in the center of the dark continent.

In front of the Pyramid of Light, there is still a stone monument inscribed "Fifth".

"This pyramid is the fifth place of inheritance."

Bai Donglin's eyes condensed slightly, and he stepped out a few steps, and instantly came to the bottom of the Pyramid of Light.

The pyramid is more than tens of thousands of feet tall, and the fierce shining light is the only light source in this dark void.

The little light he saw in the void of the universe just now radiated from this pyramid.

"This is…"

Bai Donglin stepped forward and stroked the pyramid, which was formed by the condensation of light spots, as if the transparent wall of the tower was smooth and hard.

With just a slight induction, it can be easily discerned that the defense of this pyramid far exceeds the restrictions placed by the wandering emperor on the periphery.

Looking across the transparent outer wall of the pyramid, the scene inside is clearly visible.

A round of light, with countless runes circulating on the surface, suspended in the center of the pyramid, and a large number of light groups flying in it, faintly visible there are various treasures in the light group.

A red figure sitting cross-legged under the big day of runes, surrounded by dragon and phoenix shadows, countless runes flowed from top to bottom, slowly flowing into the center of Mei Hongying's eyebrows.

At this time, Mei Hongying's red light lingering on his face was scattered, revealing a charming and charming white jade face, with a solemn expression.

The dragon yin and the phoenix cried, the whole body faintly exudes the noble and majestic dragon and phoenix blood, the dragon chases the phoenix, the golden light and the flames linger, at a glance, the Meng Hongying, who is covered in ling feathers, is like a nine-day goddess.

This Mei Hongying is really not easy!
