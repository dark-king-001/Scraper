Bang bang bang!

Bai Donglin clenched his fists and slammed it against the transparent tower wall, making ears-sounding roar, but he couldn't make any ripples.

"Hey! Is there anyone in there? Open the door and let me go in and learn about the wandering emperor's unrequited inheritance!"

Bai Donglin's expression was teasing, the inheritance had already begun, so naturally he couldn't go in, unless the inheritance of Mei Hongying failed, or only after she left, could he go in and pick up some leftovers.

Mei Hongying frowned slightly, and the movement of the outside world could not hide her from her. She slowly opened her eyes, staring at Bai Donglin and said:

"Bai Donglin, you are a step late, the inheritance of the wandering emperor is already mine."

With the ability of Mei Hongying, it is not difficult to separate a trace of thoughts in the outside world without affecting the acceptance of inheritance.

Bai Donglin also understood in his heart that he did not use this to influence his thoughts on accepting inheritance.

"Hahaha, it's not too late, you haven't left yet? This wandering emperor's inheritance looks very extraordinary. If you want to completely accept it, I am afraid it is not something that can be done in a short time."

"Huh! You don't need to worry about it, you should worry about yourself!"

Mei Hongying Liu raised his eyebrows, a sneer flashed in his eyes, and continued:

"Hehe, I really underestimated you. I didn't expect that even Jialoujie would die in your hands."

"You should think about it carefully now. After you get out of the Temple of Demon Suppression, how should you be chased by the Golden Winged Dapeng clan and the Wing Shrine to save your life."

Bai Donglin raised his brow, raised his hand and made a stop gesture, shouting loudly in his mouth:

"Stop, stop! Mei Hongying, don't spit people, I have never seen any golden-winged roc bird!"

As Bai Donglin said, his body was raised up at the same time, and it instantly soared to more than 300 feet.

With a movement of his mind, the corpse of the Roc Bird in the Origin World of Omen of Death was taken out, and the huge corpse fell on the ground with a roar.

The huge palm turned over, and the law chain shot out, entwining the corpse of the Dapeng bird tightly, and the strange fluctuations swept over the body, and the corpse began to shrink rapidly.

From the wing span to 2,000 feet, it was compressed to two thousand feet before it slowly stopped. This was already the limit that Bai Donglin could do.

This method of shrinking the physical body was a small technique that he retrospectively deduced from the magical powers of "Dharma Heaven and Earth."

This golden winged roc is worthy of a god-level bloodline, and its essence is extremely high, and it can only be reduced to this extent.

"Mei Hongying, I found the carcass of a strange bird before. It looks delicious. I just have nothing to do. I'm going to have a barbecue all the time..."

Muttering in his mouth, his hands kept moving, and the horrible force gushing, the hard golden feathers of the Golden-winged Roc bird were shaken off a little bit. These feathers are also very precious materials, and they were collected by Bai Donglin one by one. stand up.

After fading, the huge corpse suddenly became smaller again, and the golden light on the skin, lingering in the sun, like the best topaz, exuding bursts of fragrance.

This golden-winged roc bird is a treasure medicine as a whole.

Summoning a sign of death of more than two thousand feet, he swallowed the scarlet sword light, and pierced the Golden Winged Dapeng with a single knife.

"Good weight!"

Bai Donglin looked in amazement, a drop of blood could smash a big hole on the ground, one can imagine how terrifying the weight of this Jialoujie corpse.

When he stepped on his feet, the ground rolled, and two mountain peaks made of condensed metal from the ground stood up. Bai Donglin carried the omen of death on his shoulder, and put the Dapeng bird on the metal mountain peak.

A simple barbecue grill is completed in this way.

Bai Donglin took a fierce breath, and a **** flame spewed out. The karma fire and the purple-gold flame were extremely hot, and it was enough for grilling the Dapeng bird.

"Huh! It's not easy to have a warm bite! Mei Hongying, I think you worked so hard inside. If you are tired, you can come out and have a rest and have some barbecue!"

Looking at Bai Donglin with a warm smile on his face, Mei Hongying took a deep breath.

This guy, grilled the Golden Wing Roc in front of her, and said that he had never seen Jialou Jie.

Picked it up? It is so easy to pick up the body of the Dapeng bird, tell her where to pick it up, and she wants to pick it up too.

Seeing Bai Donglin's shamelessness, Mei Hongying didn't want to talk nonsense anymore, closing his eyes and fully accepting the inheritance.

In her opinion, Bai Donglin killed Jialou Jie, and if she didn't want her to take action, he was already dead.

Seeing Mei Hongying who ignored him, Bai Donglin didn't care, shook his head and sat cross-legged. He didn't need his control for the sign of death, and he rotated slowly. He only needed to add a ball of flame from time to time.

The surface of Bai Donglin was extremely quiet, but his body was moving fiercely at this time.

"The tenth one..."

Bai Donglin whispered in his heart, a "primordial" bomb fused with five laws and a hundred spiritual orifices, concealing all breath, flying out of the body quietly, and gently sticking to the surface of the pyramid of light.

There are already ten of the same terrorist bombs, and the number is slowly increasing over time.

Today's "original" bomb is a little better than the one that was detonated in the Heart of Dimension.

At the core of each bomb, there is a point of extreme shining light, which is condensed by a massive amount of soul power.

This is the inspiration he got when he blew himself up for many times when he killed Jialou Jie.

With the addition of Divine Soul, the power of "Original" should increase a lot.

From the moment his murderous intent started in his heart, he was already moving quietly. The Pyramid of Light was arranged by the wandering emperor, and its defenses can be imagined.

It is impossible to wait outside for Mei Hongying to accept the inheritance and then come out. After the inheritance is completed, the inheritor can directly teleport and leave the Temple of Demon Suppression.

It is impossible to know where it will be teleported to. When the time comes, there will be a huge crowd, and it will be difficult to meet Mei Hongying again.

Therefore, if you want to kill Mei Hongying in one fell swoop, you can only play a big one, together with the pyramid of light, and destroy everything in it!

Whether it is because of the grievances between him and Mei Hongying, or because of Jialoujie's matter, this Mei Hongying must die.

"Fuck, this is too fragrant!"

Bai Donglin's nose moved frantically. At this time, the golden-winged Dapeng bird had been roasted slightly greasy, and the strange aroma filled hundreds of miles away.

He didn't grill the Golden Wing Roc to cover up his actions in the dark, but to integrate the "primordial" in his body. It was not necessary to be extremely concealed, nor was he to mock and provoke Mei Hongying.

He simply wanted to try the taste of roasted Dapeng bird!

Anyway, idle is idle.

Bai Donglin glanced across the Rune Day inside the Pyramid of Light, slightly speculating that it would take at least a year and a half for Mei Hongying to fully accept the inheritance.

This is normal, the amount of information passed down against the sky is so exaggerated.

A monk with a weak soul and spirit has only this level of ability to accept it.

‘In one year, how many original bombs have to be condensed, hehe, the arrangement of the vagrant emperor is not impossible to destroy...’

Guru!

The temperature of the karma fire is extremely powerful. Although it is far less terrifying than the endless sins and resentments that burned on him at the beginning, this golden-winged roc is just a corpse, without mind control, and the defense has long been weakened to the bottom.

But half a day's work, has been fully familiarized.

Without adding any seasonings, the aroma is refreshing, and the golden grilled meat exudes the light of Shenxi.

"Luminous cuisine!"

Bai Donglin couldn't wait to step forward, and severely tore off a thigh, even if the Fa Tianxiang used his full force, this thigh was much larger than his whole person.

Ahhh! Take a big bite and swallow it whole, Mom, it's so delicious!

The wind and the clouds, Bai Donglin's body is not invaded by water and fire, and he is not afraid of being burnt, mouth after mouth, he can't stop at all.

The swallowing black hole in the stomach spins rapidly, the rich energy-rich Dapeng barbecue is swallowed and digested, and a stream of pure energy gushes out, which is instantly absorbed by the hungry spirit orifice.

The energy of this Dapeng barbecue is extremely pure, there is no trace of residue, and it is very digestible.

Bai Donglin just tasted the taste, UU reading www.uukanshu.com digestion is completely left to Devour Lingqiao, so there is no need to worry about not being able to eat it.

One day later.

The huge golden-winged roc bird was eaten cleanly, leaving only a huge skeleton in place.

Bai Donglin lay slanting on the metal mountain peak, his expression still unfinished, the soul of food in his body seemed to have awakened. After practicing, his taste felt extremely strengthened, and he was not interested in ordinary food at all.

But these pure-blooded beasts are really different, and it is true that high-end ingredients often only require simple cooking!

Waving his hand to put away the golden skeletons, this is a good material for refining tools.

Looking across the Pyramid of Light, Bai Donglin sat cross-legged, plunged into cultivation.

The original bombs were manufactured in batches one by one.
