"Luo Qing'er, are you crazy? I'll give you a chance to take back what I just said. I think this has never happened before!"

On the main seat of the hall, the stalwart man sitting still had gloomy eyes, spit out every word, his voice vibrated, and the surrounding lights shrank fiercely.

The hall suddenly dimmed, and the atmosphere became more and more depressing.

Luo Qing'er raised her eyebrows, looked at the man without fear, and said decisively:

"No, I have decided, and now my mind is all on the road, I have no time to take care of the love of my children, let alone your son..."

"Clan Chief Xiao, you are also a cultivator. Naturally, you understand that there is a huge gap in life expectancy between cultivators. If I marry Xiao Ran, I won't have to live a lifetime of widowhood in the future!"

Snapped! Luo Qing'er's unrelenting and straightforward words aroused public anger in an instant. The table and chair did not know how much it was broken. If it hadn't been pulled by a calm person, it would have been a big battle.

"Presumptuous! How dare you curse my young master!"

"Bold shrew!"

Patriarch Xiao’s gloomy expression became more and more ugly, and he tried to suppress the anger in his heart. He is Xiao Ran’s father and the elder of the family. He couldn’t get angry and asked again in a difficult voice:

"Luo Qing'er, is this your decision, or your father, your Luo family's decision?"

"This is naturally my decision, but I have persuaded my father, and his old man has also agreed!"

The angry people in the hall suddenly looked stagnant. Since Luo Qing'er came with the will of the Luo family, it seemed that there was no room for recovery.

"Cough cough cough!"

A weak cough came from outside the door, and a man with star-eyed sword eyebrows came slowly with the help of a maid.

The temperament is extraordinary, the appearance is dignified, but it is a pity that he is weak and weak.

In this world where the strong are respected, no matter how handsome and unrestrained he looks, he is not worthy if he has no strength.

"Xiao Ran has met his father, elders!"

"Also, Miss Luo!"

Luo Qing'er glanced at Xiao Ran, then turned her head, as if she didn't seem to take another look.

"Of course, you, why are you here?"

Patriarch Xiao's eyes flashed with distress, and he quickly got up and sat down with Xiao Ran by his side.

His only precious son used to be so talented, but now, alas, he can't even stand still.

"My father, if I don't come, wouldn't it be looked down upon?"

"If you want me to be a tortoise, you will be humiliated for me, my child can't do it!"

Xiao Ran was frail, but his eyes were extremely bright, and an awe-inspiring will was contained in it.

"Miss Luo, if you want to divorce, you can naturally, but this is a shameful shame, Xiao Ran will bear it alone, and it has nothing to do with the Xiao family!"

"Five years! Five years later, Xiao Ran will come to visit me, to wash away the humiliation of today's divorce!"

Xiao Ran's tone was deep, and every word fell in the hearts of everyone, and a high-spirited will made everyone's heart tremble, as if they had seen the heroic Xiao family genius before.

Unfortunately, after all, it was just an illusion, Xiao Ran was still that Xiao Ran, weak.

"Good! Five years later, waiting for you to fight!"

Luo Qing'er narrowed his eyes slightly, and didn't take Xiao Ran's words to heart, only that he was pretentious and wanted to forcefully save a trace of face.

After that, Luo Qing'er dropped a volume of books, turned around and walked out of the hall without hesitation.

"Thirty years in Hedong, 30 years in Hexi, don't bully the young and poor!"

The voice coming from the hall seemed to have some magical power, Luo Qing'er's figure was slightly stagnant, and then turned into a escape light and shot into the void and disappeared.

Luo Qing'er returned to Luo's house, as if she had just done a trivial thing, and didn't care about it at all.

Back in the bedroom, Luo Qing'er offered a white jade bead floating in his palm and turned slightly, his eyes flickering, and his inner emotions fluctuated fiercely.

"Haha, with this treasure, Luo Qing'er is destined to embark on the road of the strong!"

"This is just a small city of insignificance. Neither Xiao Ran nor the family can hinder my pursuit of the great road!"

All this is just beginning, with Bai Yuzhu's assistance, her future is bright!

Luo Qing'er pressed down his passionate thoughts and sat cross-legged on the bed, with the white jade beads floating between his hands, quickly immersed in the practice.

This white jade bead was one of the hundreds of splits he had previously sold at a desert oasis stall. He didn't expect that in just two years, he had already begun to change ownership.

This weak Luo Qing'er, but Yuantai Realm, was naturally not one of the original buyers.

The secrets of the heavens are spinning endlessly, and as expected, the mystery is abnormal.

"Interesting, interesting, Luo Qing'er and that Xiao Ran are very extraordinary, with a lot of luck in them."

With a movement of thought, a trace of information suddenly passed through Bai Yuzhu and shot into Luo Qing'er's mind.

‘Tianluo Space is activated, and the main mission is updated:

1. The rise of the fallen genius again: Explore the hidden secrets of Xiao Ran, deliver them to Tianluo Space, reward 20,000 points, one exercise secret book, and one middle-grade artifact.

2. The choice of destiny: to find people with great luck and contact with them...’

From Bai Donglin's point of view, this low-dimensional world, as a hiding place for escape, can still accomplish a lot if the brain is big enough.

The hundreds of split bodies that have been emitted are just a preliminary experiment, and the subsequent numbers will continue to increase.

During this period of time, as soon as Wuwei Divine Soul was free, it was creating a split body that carried most of his Divine Soul's power. Nowadays, there are a lot of accumulations in the low-dimensional world.
