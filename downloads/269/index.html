Time, time! You slipped so fast!

In a blink of an eye, eight years have passed.

The Jidao bracelet trembles fiercely, and a red light gleams and spreads, wrapping Bai Donglin and the two small ones who are immersed in cultivation, rippling in the void, and being kicked out of the sea of ​​the law of origin in an instant.

"Well, who is the first to realize the big dream, there is no time for cultivation!"

Bai Donglin's misty eyes instantly awake, these eight years of deep-level cultivation is like having a big dream, but the skyrocketing strength is real.

Raising his hand to grab the two small animals that were still in a daze, he opened the light gate, stepped out and returned to Zizhuju.

The mind sinks into the body and begins to adapt to the power in the body.

The fifty-two laws of the spirit orifice have all been incorporated into the source of light, and many laws have been strengthened a lot.

More than 10,000 spiritual orifices are also in the non-stop nurturing, which has been greatly improved.

As long as the cultivation facility of the Origin Law Sea increases the speed of the law induction and inscription of the monk, there is not much aura, and there is no advantage to the cultivation of the spirit orifice.

The reason why Bai Donglin's spirit orifice has improved so much is mainly due to the accumulation of resources. After all, he has too many spirit orifices and has unlimited potential. Simply relying on absorbing the aura of heaven and earth is simply a drop in the bucket.

But eight years ago, ninety-nine percent of the resources were fed to the immortal realm. Where did his cultivation resources come from?

Naturally it is a low-dimensional world!

Although he is in a retreat in the sea of ​​law of origin, the spirit of Wuwei has never been idle, constantly making obelisk splits, and then communicating with the outside world through the low-dimensional world.

"Tianluo Space" will issue tasks to many talented hosts, sell split bodies, and converge training resources on a large scale!

So in the past eight years, there has been no shortage of resources.

Bai Donglin's thoughts flowed, and he immediately estimated his current strength.

As long as the power of physical cultivation comes from the blessing of the spirit and the laws.

The stronger the spiritual orifice is cultivated, the more rare and powerful the engraved law, the higher the degree of completeness, the more proficient in mastering, and the stronger the blessing of the physical body.

He now incorporates the law of the origin of the spirit orifice, each of which can bless him with the power of thirty dragons, and the most special eternal and immortal spirit orifice is even more exaggerated, giving him a blessing of five hundred dragons.

The law spirit aperture is more powerful than other spirit apertures, plus the law, so it can increase so much power.

Compared with other physical cultivation, this is already very exaggerated. Take Tu Ya as an example. Only by taking Tuya's magic orifice can he bless him with 5-7 dragon powers.

This is because Tu Ya was originally a genius of evildoers, and he could only achieve this level when he cultivated with the Heavenly Overlord Body. Ordinary monks were at the level of 1 to 2 dragon power.

Fifty-two magic orifices incorporated into the source have blessed Bai Donglin's 2030 dragon power in total.

As for the remaining more than 10,000 spirit orifices, especially the weaker hidden spirit orifice, although a single blessing is not much, it is better than a large number, giving him a total of 5,000 dragon powers!

Bai Donglin is divided according to realm, but he is the second heaven of supernatural power realm, but his true strength has reached 7030 dragon power.

And one of the most common and trash cultivators of perfection in the supernatural power realm is nothing but the power of 365 dragons.

This is the gap in the background!

In the later stage, Bai Donglin estimated that his strength would be even more exaggerated as the other spiritual apertures grow up.

After all, his hidden spirit orifices have been developed too much, and there is no growth limit. The only restriction on him is the cultivation resources.

The power of a dragon is 100 million catties and 50,000 tons.

Bai Donglin is now relying solely on the power of his physical body, without performing any magical secret techniques, with one arm swayed, 351.5 million tons!

I can’t help but feel stunned by my current combat power. If you use magical powers of heaven and earth, and then use the secret technique to sublimate, the power will exponentially increase, and the use will explode this power, and the destructive power will be even more terrifying. .

"The combat power of this world is really exaggerated!"

Not only is he lamenting himself, even a cultivator of the supernatural power realm, if he reaches the blue star in the previous life, he can be the king and hegemony.

Unless a nuclear weapon explodes at close range, it is almost impossible for a nuclear weapon to hit a monk with the monk's perception of danger and the speed of movement at several times the speed of sound.

What's more, there are all kinds of magical magical secret techniques, which are beyond the imagination of mortals.

"It is reasonable for the ordinary people in this world to call the monks of the supernatural power realm "land gods". The power of one person can destroy cities and countries. In the eyes of mortals, they are naturally comparable to gods and demons!"

Suddenly soaring so much strength, it takes a lot of time to adapt to being someone else.

Bai Donglin had completely mastered it in just a moment, and the soul in his divine sea was more brilliant and powerful than his physical body.

However, the growth of Shenyuan Eighth Heaven is really too slow now, and it is only a small realm when it has swallowed up the energy for eight consecutive years.

This is also no way. The physical body and the soul are too far apart, and the strengthening energy is the energy that is reversed based on the damage of the physical body. Now it is used to strengthen the soul, and it feels a little like a small horse-drawn cart.

Pressing his thoughts, Bai Donglin finally put his gaze on the blinking bracelet, his thoughts penetrated, and a large number of prompt messages suddenly disappeared into his mind.

The moment I scanned the information, my hand couldn't help but shook, and his eyes glared.

"I fuck!"

One, ten, one hundred, one thousand, ten thousand, three and five hundred thousand points are just negative!

"I owe more than 3.5 million points?!"

Bai Donglin is stunned, still bring this? Can you still owe fees for using training facilities? Wasn't it kicked out after all the deductions?

Isn't this also one of the hidden benefits of starting the extreme trial?

But in an instant, Bai Donglin was relieved immediately. It turned out that this was the real welfare. If he died during the trial, he would naturally not have to pay it back.

But if he succeeds in passing the trial, a genius with a promising future will naturally have the ability to pay back his debts.

"Forget it, there is no interest anyway, the strength is strong, earning points is not easy to catch."

After skipping many spam messages, Bai Donglin's expression gradually became solemn, and his gaze fell on a golden decree.

‘Extreme Dao Trial·Nine Stars:

Task 1: How can a true genius be willing to be inferior to others? Sweep the heroes and reach the top of the Qianlong list!
