　　 The moonlight was shining on the surface of the river, and the breeze was blowing, sparkling.

　　 The dark blue ship drove fast on the surface of the river, and the fluorescence flowing between the runes became more eye-catching in the night.

　　 At this time, among the many messy cargoes in the bottom cargo hold, a bronze box full of patina was engraved with weird patterns, and the patterns were entangled with each other, vaguely forming a grimace.

　　 This patterned grimace has its mouth wide open, as if there is a strange magic power that will swallow the soul of the person staring at it.

　　 The bronze box shook slightly.

　　 Snap, the box fell from the pile of goods and tumbled a few times and stood on the ground.

　　 A puff of gray smoke floated out of the open mouth of the grimace.

　　 A suffocating gloomy laughter sounded in the silent cargo hold.

　　 "Jie Jie Jie..."

　　 "Soul...time...node...sacrifice, open..."

　　Unknown language, vague words, as if crossing the world with difficulty, came out of the bronze box.

　　 Gray smoke hovered, like invisible and innocuous, passing through the hull, the magic circle barrier, and constantly approaching the area where the passengers were.

　　 Gray smoke burrowed into a guest room. The monk inside was unaware, still sitting on the ground carrying the true yuan to absorb the aura of heaven and earth. Hovering around the monk, suddenly slammed into the monk's mind.

　　 This cultivator is only the True Primal Realm Great Perfection, and he is two full realms away from the spiritual platform that involves soul cultivation. Although his soul is much stronger than ordinary people, there is no essential difference.

　　 is still fragile and weak, especially in the face of the gray smoke attacks against the soul, and there is no resistance.

　　 The gray smoke easily swallowed the monk's soul. The monk looked unscathed, but only a body remained, completely dead.

　　 Grey smoke came out of my mind, circled around, and then went to the next room.

　　Whether it is a monk, a mortal, a passenger or a crew member, the gray smoke will not refuse to come. As long as you meet it, it will devour its soul, until it has swallowed more than a hundred people.

　　 Faces are constantly emerging on the gray smoke that keeps tumbling. They are the people whose souls have been swallowed just now, with expressions of hideous horror, anger, pain, unwillingness, fear...

　　 is full of evil negative atmosphere.

　　 The gray smoke returned to the cargo hold and got into the bronze box. The bronze box gave out a gleam of light, and some of the patterns on it seemed to come alive, slightly twisted.

　　 "Jie Jie Jie..."

　　 There was another gruesome laughter from the silent cargo hold.

　　 The next morning.

　　 A shrill scream broke the old silence of the Azure.

　　 Then the door was knocked and a staff member came in.

"What happened?"

　　 "Routine inspection!" After speaking, he hurriedly left, knocking on the next door.

　　Bai Donglin, who just finished the morning exercise, frowned slightly. They have been sailing on the Nu River for five days, and there was no accident in the five days.

　　 He just wants to spend the eighteen days safely and reach the Bian Erqi domain as soon as possible, without unexpected twists and turns.

　　 It's a pity, it seems that things have gone contrary to expectations, but something has happened. Open the door, call Liu Dafu and Zi Xiaoling, and walk to the noisy deck.

　　 At this time, the deck was already full of people, and there were staff in uniforms maintaining order. Bai Donglin saw Captain Wang Lufei also among the crowd.

　　 It seems that something serious happened, and even the captain was alarmed.

　　 When the three approached, they heard the captain being questioning.

　　 "Are these corpses found in Area C? Who was the first to find the corpses?"

"Captain, ninety-three corpses were found in Area C, and the remaining 24 corpses were found in Area B, which is next to Area C. The first to find the corpses was Zhang Ergou from the back kitchen. His name is The roommate in the same bedroom got up to prepare breakfast, only to find that his roommate's body was cold."

　　 "After receiving the report, we have checked the entire ship and found a total of 117 dead bodies. The cause of death is unknown!"

　　 "What was heard last night?"

　　 "I have inquired about the tenants around the deceased, and they all said no. Even those in the same dormitory did not hear any movement, as if these people died naturally."

　　 The captain snorted when he heard the words. There were only more than 3,000 people on the ship. Can more than 100 people die naturally at the same time? The possibility of food poisoning was also ruled out, and the concentration of the dead was obviously man-made.

　　 As a monk of the Great Perfection in the Divine Bridge Realm, he naturally has a lot of means.

　　Running the mind, reciting the Dharma tactics silently.

　　 "Broken Void Eye! Open!"

　　 With a soft drink, the captain's pupils turned into golden colors, and an inch of golden light appeared. He carefully observed a corpse from top to bottom, and finally fixed his eyes on the corpse's forehead.

　　 The captain stopped the tactics, his eyes returned to normal, but his face became more and more serious.

　　 The soul was swallowed completely. After death, the soul dissipated between the heaven and the earth, and the true spirit returned to the mother river.

　　 Even so, there will still be some soul aura in the corpse, even soul fragments.

　　 And these corpses are empty inside, this is definitely not a natural dissipation, it is attacked by means against the soul.

　　 is a master above Lingtai Realm? Or the monsters and monsters at work? Or is it the evil practice of cultivating weird and secret methods?

　　 No matter what the situation is, he is not optimistic. He can kill one hundred and seventeen people silently overnight, his strength is definitely above him.

　　 The captain paced back and forth, thinking about what to do. The Canglan still had 13 days to reach the Silver Snake Wharf, and the distance was too far to count on the support of the wharf.

　　 I only have to do a few more preparations, not careless, there are many monks on the boat with a background, if there is an accident, I am afraid that I will not survive!

　　 Wang Lufei immediately ordered left and right: "Keep all the corpses properly and put them in the ice store. Disperse the crowd and tell the passengers to stay in their rooms."

　　 "Order the crew to return to their posts and not discuss the matter."

"Yes!"

　　 Left and right deputies led the order and stepped back.

　　 The captain took out two more ten thousand li sound transmission notes, and passed in a message for help. The talisman burned spontaneously, turning into two white lights, one north and one south, and disappeared instantly.

　　 After finishing this, he called several captains into the captain’s room to discuss the night patrol carefully.

The three of Bai Donglin saw everything in their eyes not far away. Liu Dafu looked solemn, Bai Donglin expressionless, and Zi Xiaoling was still eating sweet-scented osmanthus cake carelessly, her mouth bulging. , Like a squirrel.

　　 Bai Donglin's expression moved, as if thinking of something.

　　 "You mean..."

　　 "Yes, this is very similar to the style of monsters and ghosts, and it may still be the most troublesome type."

　　 Liu Dafu narrowed his eyes slightly, as if thinking of some unpleasant experience.

　　 Although Bai Donglin has not come into contact with many demons and ghosts, he knows a truth, everything is a cause and effect, but demons and ghosts are not included here. Their purpose is to kill and destroy.

　　 This kind of indiscriminate killing, and the way it is so weird, is indeed the style of monsters and ghosts.
