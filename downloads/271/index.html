It seems that this extremely enthusiastic Youvi is really just a passer-by.

Bai Donglin's eyes sank slightly, and the powerful spirit power swept back and forth in his body, and he slowly retracted it after a long time without gain.

A powerful monk, is it really just to eat and drink?
