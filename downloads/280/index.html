After one month.

In the palace of Touzi, Liu Dongchuan sat cross-legged, his eyes seemingly closed.

He did not look into the situation in the Bagua Furnace, because he was afraid that entering his mind would affect the judgment of Bai Donglin's trial mission, but he could still slightly perceive the extremely vigorous life of Bai Donglin in the Bagua Furnace through the crimson token. breath.

Not only did Bai Donglin not die, but he was still alive. For physical cultivation, the strength of the breath of life can explain his state.

His worries from the beginning have now become calm, although he doesn't know how Bai Donglin did it.

He didn't seem to be so uncomfortable about this. After all, Bai Donglin had already created a miracle in front of him. Compared to his performance in the "heart" of the Nine Rings, this time the samaya true fire refining was nothing.

"Um?"

Liu Dongchuan's eyebrows trembled slightly, and a message sent across the spatial dimension was received, and Yingning was urging him.

"These people move very fast. It seems that some people can't help it anymore."

Slowly got up, a light and shadow clone was still on the ground, urging the crimson token in his hand to open the vortex passage.

There is still more than half a month before Bai Donglin comes out, he can settle the matter first, and then come back to pick up Bai Donglin.

Now that Bai Donglin's situation is safe and sound, there is no need for him to stare here, leaving a light and shadow clone.

Stepping into the vortex passage, Liu Dongchuan instantly appeared in a void, and in the distance was a majestic palace filled with clouds and mist.

The void was rippling slightly, Ying Ning's figure emerged out of thin air, looking at Liu Dongchuan and asking:

"Brother Dao, is the pill refining smoothly?"

"Well, it's almost there, with my clone and Dong Lin watching, it's not a big problem."

Ying Ning nodded slightly, not paying attention to these trivial things, his eyes fell on the palace in the distance, and slowly said:

"People from all forces have arrived, and the messenger of the ghost mansion will also come. I don't know who it is this time..."

"Hehe, I won't know in a moment, and who is the difference? Anyway, the results are not bad."

Liu Dongchuan's eyes flickered, and with his hands on his back, he walked step by step towards the palace suspended in the clouds.

"Hey, brother Dao, I'm not afraid of encountering difficult people. After all, you also know that the people in the Ghost Mansion have very strange tempers."

Yingning shook his head and laughed, and walked to the palace together with Liu Dongchuan.

"If you are like them, in the endless years, you are always thinking about how to annihilate the race and destroy the world, and every action is accompanied by the fall of endless life, your temper will become weird!"

"Blessed Heaven, Pang Dao is not a bloodthirsty person, but he can't bear this important task!"

"By the way, Yingning, has your sister come out of the center of the earth?"

"nature!"

"Uh…"

Liu Dongchuan's expression was stagnant, and there was no more indifferent expression on his face.

During the chat, the two of them stepped out a few steps and came to the outside of the palace. The gate of a palace like a fairy palace was a huge light gate, and behind the gate filled with golden light, there was a faint sound of fairy.

Crossing the light gate, the two appeared on a huge bronze platform, an invisible force of terror added to their bodies, and their bodies suddenly sank slightly.

And above the bronze platform is a bright cosmic void. When you look at it, there are big suns and stars, and the breath is mighty. This is a real starry sky.

The core of this corner of the starry sky is the huge bronze platform under their feet. Numerous suns, moons and stars revolve around it. One can imagine how terrifying the quality of this bronze platform.

It's no wonder that they will feel their bodies sink slightly when they come here as powerful people. If they are any non-powerful monk, they will be crushed into a pancake in an instant!

"Yingning, your Dao Sect is really deep, no matter how many times you watch it, this'Zhenwu Hall' is so shocking."

Yingning shrugged and was noncommittal about this, no way, he didn't know how many times he had heard these words, he was already immune.

Click!

The sound of metal friction sounded, and two huge bronze thrones grew out of the bronze table. The thrones covered with mysterious patterns filled with the breath of ancient years.

Liu Dongchuan looked at each other and jumped onto the bronze throne at the same time. When they sat down cross-legged, the throne burst out with a glimmer of light and disappeared instantly.

The bronze throne carrying two people, instantly appeared in the central core of the bronze platform. There were densely packed thrones here, and a figure sat cross-legged on top of each throne, exuding an aura of horror.

These people are all powerful monks!

Sweeping at a glance, there are thousands of them, all of them come from Qianyuan Realm's powerful Qi cultivation forces.

With countless powerful auras permeating, the tough space here trembles slightly. This is the will of the strong, unconsciously affecting the surrounding reality. Under the touch of different wills, the turbulent aura disrupts the space.

As the saying goes, one mountain cannot tolerate two tigers, let alone these powerful monks, although they are unintentional, but the powerful will not be able to be moved by people. If they gather together, this situation will inevitably occur.

This is also the reason why they chose the venue here. If in the outside world, with so many powers gathered together, the will will be tit-for-tat, I am afraid the sky will collapse!

Everyone was silent, and the atmosphere seemed extremely depressing.

Da da da!

A metal pedaling sound broke the silence here, and everyone moved slightly and cast their eyes into the distance.

A tall and tall figure walked slowly, wrapped in a full set of heavy armor and shoes made of unknown metal, stepping on the bronze ground, making a crisp sound.

The speed of the arrival is extremely fast, the last moment is still very far away, a few steps down, has appeared in the center of many mighty powers.

clatter! When the armored man stepped on his feet, a creak sounded, and a wide bronze round table slowly emerged. The armored man was standing on the bronze round table, and his body gradually raised.

When the bronze round table stood still, the armored man sat cross-legged on the table casually, his face wearing a metal mask looked around, his pale eyes ghostly.

"Ahem, the old man is the War Department of the Ghost Mansion, the third director, codenamed "Hai", this meeting will be chaired by me, ahem..."

The armored man codenamed "Hai" has a very old and shrive voice, as if a piece of rotten wood is rubbing against it. "Hat" seems to be very weak. He coughs several times every once in a while, making people mistakenly think that this is just a mortal who is about to die. Old man.

However, many mighty minds did not dare to look down upon them a little, they all looked solemnly. In their sight, with every word of "horror" falling, a strong and extremely dead air filled madly, in the distant void. The sun's rays are all dimmed.

Of course they would not look at the general death aura, but the death aura that permeated the "horrified" body was extremely distorted and mad, eternal and immortal. This is the death aura that can be condensed by slaughtering many great-level cultivators!

Sitting on the bronze throne, Yingning's expression changed slightly. He was surprised at the identity of this "horror." The way he played and manipulated the bronze table at will, this undoubtedly explained one thing.

The masked person code-named "Hack" comes from their Taoist door!

This is absolutely not wrong, if it is not a Taoist person, even if it is a strong person in the ghost mansion, it is impossible to manipulate the "Zhenwu Hall".
