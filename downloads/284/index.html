Miao Kong grabbed Bai Donglin's sleeves, his eyes were reddish, and he stopped talking.

"Go to Miaokong, remember what I said, and work hard!"

Bai Donglin reached out and patted Miao Kong's shoulder.

"Yeah! Uncle, I won't let you down!"

Miao Kong looked serious, his eyes were full of firmness, put down his tight sleeves, and ran to Yingning in two steps.

Yingning raised her brows and said in a pointed tone:

"Miao Kong, if you join the Dao Sect in the future, you can't just call Master Uncle when you recognize whoever you want, otherwise, wouldn't you mess up your seniority?"

"As for you..."

Ying Ning's gaze fell on Bai Donglin's body, and when he pointed it out, a small crane was condensed with the crimson light, shaking its wings, and hovering in front of Bai Donglin.

"The monks of the outer sect who came to participate in the Daoist Conference will have a while before they leave the Daomen in unison. This light crane will take you to their gathering place."

I instantly understood what Ying Ning meant. This was to ask him to leave with the large army, how to leave, and Bai Donglin slightly offered his hand.
