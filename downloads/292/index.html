The old man caressed his beard and smiled unpredictably. Before starting, he wanted to test Bai Donglin's cognition of cultivation.

Bai Donglin frowned. For the monk, what was important was not those foreign things, such as physical fitness, talent, comprehension, and magical secrets cultivation resources, but what the old man said was definitely not so superficial.

"Senior, from the perspective of juniors, the early cultivation of a monk is nothing more than the accumulation of time. It takes less time to have good talents and resources, otherwise it takes more time."

Bai Donglin looked at him for a moment and continued:

"In the later stages, the role of these factors of natural resources will become weaker and weaker, but one thing is eternal. The unswerving belief in one's own way and the eternal will is the most important!"

"After all, I think and therefore I am. Only the will is immortal, and everything we wait for the monks to work hard for has the meaning of existence!"

Happiness!

The old man gently moved his palms, his eyes filled with admiration.

"Yes, you are right. You can see through the illusion and point to the essence. You are really good."

The old man nodded lightly, the smile on his face grew richer, and his gaze at Bai Donglin became more and more satisfied.

In his opinion, Bai Donglin can start the nine-star trial, which is absolutely the top talent in terms of talent, and this genius monk enjoys the benefits of talent, and is generally blinded by it, convinced that talent is supreme.

But when the monk arrives in the later stage, which powerhouse who can achieve great power is not the one with amazing talents?

And there is also a huge gap between the powerful and powerful, which naturally has a reason, this is the gap that talent cannot fill.

Bai Donglin was young, but he was able to get rid of the blindness of his own talent and understand the essence, which was really not easy.

"What the old man wants to teach you is how to master your own will. If you can achieve something, you will go further along the way of cultivation."

"Thank you senior!"

Bai Donglin got up and bowed slightly to salute. No matter what the agreement between this mysterious old man and the Extreme Sage Sect, but can point him to the benefits, this is a kind of kindness.

"Well, first of all you have to understand a truth, will is everywhere, whether it is our monks, or the world, all things have their own will."

"A stone, a weed, a magic weapon, a wisp of law, even a world, there is will."

"The will is illusory, an abstract concept. Unlike consciousness, the will is passive, but it has strengths and weaknesses. The first step is to master your own will, and then touch it with your own will. The will of all things in the world!"

Bai Donglin nodded slightly, his eyes showed a thoughtful color, only to feel a brand new world slowly unfolding in front of him, and then asked suspiciously:

"Senior, what does this will have to do with arowana fishing? Why do you set this task?"

The old man smiled without saying a word, stretched out his hand to pick up the fishing rod on the ground, and shook it lightly, and a looming thread emerged. Bai Donglin saw it more clearly this time. Even close at hand, he still did not sense energy fluctuations. , The law fluctuates.
