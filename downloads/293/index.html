"You have now ignited the fire of will, and it is a matter of course to completely master your own will. Now you can strike while the iron is hot, and there is a big opportunity in front of you!"

You must know that he has never stopped perceiving the spirit orifices in his body. The powerful spirits of the Eighth Heaven in Divine Origin Realm have played a great role in opening up hidden spirit orifices, but the most important thing is the "Pregnant God Casting Demon Scripture". Special.

He estimated that the technique had reached a bottleneck, and he needed a deeper understanding to increase the speed of opening up the hidden spiritual orifice again.

But this truth is so difficult, even with the aid of a dark stone stele, he still struggles to practice.

"Senior, please help me protect the law, this junior will give it a try!"

"Don't worry, the old man is safe here."

The old man shook his head and laughed. He was also very curious in his heart, wondering how many hidden spirits this monster-like kid could open up.

At least five hundred, right?

Bai Donglin nodded lightly, sat cross-legged, his mind instantly calmed down, his will spread to the depths of his body.

The darkness, deep in the depths of the flesh, resembled the infinite void of the universe, the will is like fire, passing by the dazzling eyes, like the spirit orifice of the great sun, these are all the spirit orifices of successful opening.

At the same time, a huge mind and soul also followed, and before that, he had another pair of eyes called "will".

It's really different!

Will, everything has its own unique will, and the same is true for hiding the spirit orifice.

Five hundred, one thousand, three thousand, until five thousand hidden spiritual orifices were marked, it slowly stopped. This was the limit that his will could sense at present.

But in a moment, he completed the amount he had worked so hard in the previous few years. This newly awakened will is indeed a great opportunity, and I think it will not have such a good effect for the first time in the future.
