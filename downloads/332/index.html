The place where Sanctuary Wings, the great sky, the flying feathers, and the three territories meet, is one of the three sacred forces of the Monster Race, the headquarters of the Wing Shrine.

Tens of thousands of miles away, on the top of a towering mountain, Bai Dong was sitting cross-legged, looking forward, as if he had seen countless birds and sacred birds flying freely in the Wing Shrine.

The sacred place of Wing Shrine is very special. Within its territory spreading millions of miles, the rules have been reversed, and gravity seems to have lost its effect.

The entire territory is suspended above the sky, in addition to the huge floating continent that enters two million miles, it is surrounded by countless small lands.

A huge shadow is cast on the Qianyuan Continent, and beneath its territory is a bottomless pit that stretches for millions of miles.

The base camp of the entire Wing Shrine seemed to be peeled off the ground.

Ho Ho Ho!

The roar of the beast sounded from time to time in the vast primitive dense forest, Bai Donglin retracted his eyes and looked around.

In the realm dominated by the three holy land forces of the Demon Race, almost all have this appearance, the boundless vast forest, few traces of the human race, there are only endless monsters of the demon race.

Bai Donglin set his gaze on the dark and secluded lucky ring, and a cold light flashed faintly in his eyes.

"After waiting for so long, it's time to withdraw a little interest. Hehe, I have been put on a bounty list. Don't you know that I am always low-key? It's really hateful!"

As early as two or three years ago, the pure version of the Golden Red Obelisk Split Invasion Plan was completed, and the number rose from one thousand to ten thousand.

The so-called pure body is a split body that does not contain a trace of Bai Donglin's power. This split body can only be used as the opening coordinates of the low-dimensional world, and does not have the function of "Tianluo Space".

The concealment is extremely strong, and with its extremely high nature, the possibility of being discovered is very slim.

These split bodies were either transformed into spirit stones or various treasured minerals, and they were smoothly brought into the base camp by the children of the Wing Jingong through trading, accidentally obtaining them, and so on.

Everything is going well. The reason why Bai Donglin hasn't done anything is because of the lucky ring. His previous lucky value was absolutely negative, and he still has no idea how much he lost.

In addition, the Wing Shrine is different from those low-level forces, but there are many strong ancestors, which has to make him extremely cautious.

If it was unlucky enough to attract the ancestors of the Wing Shrine, it would be no fun.

If you want revenge, you must be fully prepared before you do it!

During this period of time, the knights went around fighting for righteousness and solved the last goal of the black mine cave not long ago. The number of criminals slaughtered has reached millions.

At this time, he took a shot at the Wing Shrine, and he felt a little more at ease.

"Ten thousand overlords are spread all over the base camp of the Wing Shrine, and each split body projected two hundred original bombs, which is two million..."

Bai Donglin's eyes were drooping, and his expression was extremely indifferent. Today's original bombs are much more powerful than before.

After practicing the way of will, the fusion of the law and the spirit orifice is even more handy, and the maximum limit is 300 pieces of ten different laws and orifices that can be integrated!

Although because of efficiency, the original bomb he had stored was only five or two hundred, but the power was also not to be underestimated, far more than the one in the Temple of Demon Suppression.

Moreover, the disciples of the Winged God Palace who have obtained the split body are all younger generations. Are these cub demon races living together?

Their strength is not strong, it is impossible to withstand so many original bombs.

"Humph! Even if you can't let the bloodline of the Wing Divine Palace appear to be broken this time, it will have to be painful, or the pain will go deep into the bone marrow, otherwise I really think I'm a bully!"

Bai Donglin was also a little depressed, but just roasted a golden-winged roc bird, and even gave him a reward list.

The reward list of this Winged Shrine is not low!

The ruler of the Wing Shrine is not the Golden Winged Dapeng clan. Logically speaking, it is just a younger generation who has died, so he shouldn't be staring at him like this.

Bai Donglin was thinking about it, suddenly his heart moved.

"Could it be because of the little girl's skin of Mei Hongying? The vision she had when she accepted the inheritance, and the weird spirit, seemed very difficult..."

"In that case, the bubble world shouldn't be the heritage treasure of the wandering emperor, but the equipment exploded from Xiao Niang's skin?"

"It is even possible that the goal of Wing Shrine is not me at all, but the bubble world!"

Bai Donglin was sure in his heart, and affirmed a bit of his guess, only in this way could the reason why Wing Shrine was so aggressively fighting for the two dead monsters.

A Dapeng bird is less expensive than that.

"Haha, okay, death can cause me trouble, Mei Hongying deserves to be you, I look down on you!"

Pressing down on the rolling thoughts in his heart, the most urgent thing is to make him vent his anger!

With a dazzling gaze, he erected a red-lighted palm knife and chopped off his left arm at the elbow. The body began to self-destruct, suppressing the indestructible recovery ability, keeping the arm in a broken arm state.

Picking up the broken arm and looking at the lucky ring on his finger, a smile appeared in Bai Donglin's eyes.

This is not an ordinary broken arm, its wounds are lingering with light, not only contains a complete body and soul, but even the soul is also cut into it.

This is also the reason why the lucky ring can still be worn on the finger of the broken arm.

There is no instruction manual for the lucky ring. He explored everything on his own. After so long, he almost figured it out.

There is a limit to the distance of the lucky ring to absorb the dead, not only the hand of the dead, but also the distance.

For example, when he was in Biyou Palace, the monsters he killed with the original bomb, because they were separated by two worlds, he didn't absorb a trace of luck.

It was the same when destroying the Blood River Sect. Thousands of miles away, the lucky value absorbed was very small. It seemed that it was in the process of transferring and had been dissipated between the heaven and the earth.

This time he is going to pit a Winged Shrine fiercely. As one of the top powers, the lucky value contained in his children is not comparable to the **** he slaughtered!

"Such a great benefit is in front of you, it's impossible not to avoid it, but I never make a trade that suffers, hehe, meaningless killing, always almost meaningless!"

Bai Donglin couldn't help but laugh, and gave a thumbs up for his wit. His body naturally couldn't get close to the Wing Shrine. It was too dangerous, so he could only find a way to send the lucky ring in to absorb the luck!

He has done experiments, this method is feasible, after the broken arm is destroyed, the non-drop feature of the lucky ring will automatically activate.

After a quarter of an hour, in a very strange way, he returned to his hands silently.

A quarter of an hour is enough to absorb luck.

"The time has come, the split bodies are almost all gathered together..."

The base camp of the Wing Shrine is extremely large, and the divisions are also very scattered. The lucky ring has a distance limit to absorb luck, and it cannot take into account everything, which will cause waste.

Through the low-dimensional world, Bai Donglin can vaguely sense the distance between the split bodies. During this period of time, he has also worked out the pattern. These children will gather together from time to time, perhaps listening to predecessors, or What other important gatherings.

He doesn't care what they are doing, he just knows that it's good for him!

"It has nothing to do with right or wrong, with nothing to do with good and evil. Since I have become hostile, then I will not be compassionate!"

"Life and death are fateful, go all the way!"

The red light gathered in the depths of the eyes, killing intent boiling frantically.

"The low-dimensional world..."

"open!"

The new generation children of the Wings of the Shrine gathered together, they were all awe-inspiring and mighty, they were arguing in a hustle and bustle of what they were arguing about. Without their noticing, the split bodies stored everywhere cracked a scarlet gap silently.

Two million faint light spots spouted out, with a broken arm lingering in the light, the **** was inexplicably raised, and the black light of the dark ring worn on the curved index finger appeared.

——Original·The Light of Ten Thousand Wheels Breaking the Realm! ——

With a movement of Bai Donglin's mind, all the original bombs were detonated, and he immediately opened the entrance to the low-dimensional world and stepped into it without hesitation.

Huh!唳——

At the moment the Scarlet Channel was closed, a shrill horror swept past.

A bird-shaped phantom with a wingspan of millions of miles~www.mtlnovel.com~ appeared out of nowhere above the Wing Shrine. The extremely large wings shook slightly, and the wildly expanding splendid day was instantly annihilated.

The bird's head is drooping, like the huge eyes of the blazing sun, the golden light is shining, sweeping back and forth across the world.

唳——

"The ancestor has lowered the decree!"

"Take a thorough investigation! Check it out!"

"at all costs!!"

唳唳——

Countless huge birds and animals, covering the sky and obscuring the sun, their wings shattered into the void, blasting away in all directions.

The boiling anger illuminates the sky red.
