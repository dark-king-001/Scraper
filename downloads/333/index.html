Taking the low-dimensional world as a transit, Bai Donglin instantly crossed tens of thousands of realms, completely away from the area where the Wing Shrine was located.

"Seventeen, sixteen, fifteen..."

Bai Donglin sat cross-legged on the thick tree trunk, with a calm expression, his eyes fell on the index finger of his left arm that had regrown, and he meditated in his heart.

A quarter of an hour passed quickly, as precise as expected, a dark ring appeared abruptly on the empty finger, without any trace of fluctuation, as if it had been on the finger forever.

"Hey! This time it's a big game!"

A little surprise flashed in Bai Donglin's eyes. The lucky ring, which used to be ordinary and unremarkable, changed drastically at this time, with traces of extremely small silver chains circling and winding on the ring body.

In the past, when absorbing luck, this ring flashed a black light at most. Now that such a big change has taken place, there is only one explanation.

There is so much luck this time!

Fortunately, this is just Bai Donglin's personal preference. The monks in this world uniformly call this kind of luck, and there is essentially no difference.

In addition to the luck that comes with one's own life, a monk has only a few ways to improve his luck. The most common method is to join a powerful force.

Top forces like the Wing Shrine have great luck in the body, and have powerful treasures to suppress Qi luck, and the disciples who join these forces will have their Qi luck tied to it.

Excellent disciples will enhance the luck of the forces they belong to, and similarly, the luck of these forces themselves will also be shared among the disciples.

If the disciple lives and dies unexpectedly, the role of the treasure that suppresses the Qi Luck will be manifested, and he can forcibly recover the Qi Luck that has been distributed.

And this lucky ring is against the sky, directly forcibly shutting off the air luck that should be recovered, and even plundering the deceased's own air luck.

This is absolutely unbearable for one force. This is also the reason why the Wing Jingong is so angry. The disciple can be cultivated after death. This robbed of luck is really gone. It is extremely difficult. Recovery, loss of luck, often represents the decline of a party's power.

"Broken other people's foundation, this time I completely offended the Wing Shrine to death, even if there is no previous thing, there is no possibility of relaxation, but then again..."

Bai Donglin shook his head slightly, pressing down on the meaningless thoughts in his heart. Now he also wants to understand that the grievances between him and the Wing Shrine have long been inevitable.

As early as in the Shahai Palace, I had a grudge with Mei Hongying for the first time. The fate of the two had been entangled, but he had never thought that Mei Hongying had such a special status in the Wing Shrine. I thought this woman was just Jialoujie's concubine.

"This time it was really thrilling. Fortunately, I was cautious enough!"

There was a glimmer of fear in Bai Donglin's eyes, and there was indeed an ancestor guarding the base camp of this winged divine palace, although it was only a light and shadow clone.

But in the "heart" of the Nine Rings, he was fortunate to have seen the ancestor named "Absurd" make a move. It is also a clone, which has already made him understand the perversion of the world's top powerhouse!

Although the low-dimensional world is magical, if you dare to use it under the eyes of the ancestors, the possibility of hiding it from the other party is less than 10%.

"Although it is indeed a little risky, but this gain is enough to make up for it, haha, no loss or loss, if I have a chance, I will dare next time!"

Seeing the extraordinary lucky ring on his finger, Bai Donglin couldn't help but laugh, and the slight worries in his heart also disappeared.

He dares to act in this way, the biggest reliance is not the low-dimensional world, but the fact that he has no real spirit in his soul, is not in the operation of this world, and is not afraid of any deduction.

As long as he is not captured on the spot, who can accuse him of doing it?

'Humph! I, Bai Donglin, is nothing more than a good person passing by. How could it be that I did such a tragic thing? Don't spit people! ’

"Tianluo!"

"Master, what's your order?"

"Give me a random low-dimensional channel in Qianyuan Continent. I want to try how lucky I am now!"

"OK!"

Bai Donglin was calm, not longing for something in his heart, but let his own luck run, and he also wanted to enjoy the treatment of the Son of Destiny!

A ray of red light flashed, and Bai Donglin's figure disappeared instantly.

…

Qianyuan Realm, the northernmost region, is located in the northernmost part of the mainland. It is a large area covered by ice and snow all year round. Although it is a large area, its area is not less than that of an ordinary sanctuary.

The wind and snow roared, wrapped in silver.

The dense forests are home to the most hardy plants in this world. They are not much different from plants in other regions except for the hard and vigorous branches and the dense leaves like needles.

簌簌!

There were large creatures rushing in the dense forest, shaking fiercely under the collision, and the heavy snow on the quaint trees fell rustlingly.

Ho Ho Ho!

"Master sister! There are too many spirit-eating beasts, we have been surrounded, these beasts are waiting for us to exhaust..."

As the white dress woman shouted, her expression suddenly changed. The blue thin sword in her hand turned around and chopped horizontally. The light of the light sword that was several feet long flashed away, and a snow-white alien beast that flew in the air was slashed back.

clang!

The sharp sword light slashed on the body of the alien beast, making a piercing sound of golden and iron clashes, leaving no injuries except being repelled.

The snow-white alien beast named Spirit Eater was covered with long white hair, and while the hair was fluttering, one could faintly see the gleaming silver scales under it, extremely hard.

Roar!

Although he was not injured, the woman's counterattack made the spirit-eating beast crazier, and the bloodthirsty in his eyes became more intense.

The long sword in the white dress woman's hand trembled slightly, and the scarlet blood fell down her arm, falling on the snow, like plum blossoms.

"Junior sister, are you okay?"

More than a dozen women in white dresses slowly gathered together, back to back to each other, and their eyes scanned the white beast shadows constantly flashing in the dense forest.

"Master sister, I'm fine. It's just some skin trauma. What should we do now? These spirit-eating beasts are so fast that we can't get rid of their pursuit."

The master sister with the deepest cultivation base, his eyes swept across the exhausted crowd, and a wave of powerlessness rose in his heart. They had been chased for seven days and seven nights, and the true essence in their bodies was about to be exhausted.

"Junior sisters, we can't sit and wait for death. As long as we get out of this dense forest, we will be safe. Outside the dense forest is the sphere of influence of the Ice and Snow Palace. These beasts dare not cross the border!"

"After swallowing these pills, we will break through separately!"

The big sister's eyes condensed, now is not the time to be indecisive, and with a wave of her hand, she distributes the only remaining spirit pill to everyone.

"All to live!"

"Master sister take care!"

Everyone took the spirit pill, the color of determination flashed in their eyes, and they swallowed directly into their abdomen. The body surface suddenly rose with a strong spiritual light, which instantly turned into a light and shadow, and UU reading www.uukanshu.com shot in all directions.

"Huh! Damn beast!"

The big sister snorted coldly, and hundreds of sword lights were cut out from the long sword in her hand, and after drawing most of the spirit-eating beasts' attention to her body, her figure shook, looking for a direction and quickly fleeing.

Roar—

The spirit-eating beast was stunned for a moment, and then roared and began to chase in a diversion.

It seemed a useful move, but the senior sister knew in her heart that her junior sisters couldn't even beat a spirit-eating beast, so doing so was just asking for comfort.

They had no choice but to get together to face the siege was a dead end.

Whether it is life or death, it depends on their own destiny.
