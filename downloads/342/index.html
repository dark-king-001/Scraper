The light dissipated, and his sight resumed.

Bai Donglin looked around and found that he was already in a strange space. His perception was obscured and its size was unknown. The surrounding area was filled with faint blue light, and the colorful streamer passed in front of him from time to time, dragging a long light tail.

Like a dream, like a fantasy, like a fairyland.

Tread lightly under the feet, it is extremely hard blue crystal, faintly transparent, and the reflection is clearly visible.

"Sisters, come with me. It's the second time for me to enter the sacred pool. Just follow me."

A disciple with a steady demeanor and the strongest aura, spoke softly, drew everyone's attention back.

"Thank you, Senior Sister!"

Everyone looked grateful, offered their hands and saluted, and then they all followed the woman and walked slowly into the blue mist in front of them.

Bai Donglin nodded slightly. The atmosphere in the Ice and Snow Saint Palace was still very harmonious, helping each other, at least when there was no conflict of interest.

Lan Ling's eyes flickered, his feet moved slightly, and he followed the back of the crowd, his little head looked around, curiously looking at everything around him.

Soon afterwards, everyone faintly caught the sound of the water wave disturbance, their expressions were shocked, and the speed under their feet increased a little.

"Wow! So beautiful!"

"Well, is this the legendary sacred pool? It really is extraordinary!"

Passing through the blue mist, his eyes suddenly lit up, and Lan Ling's sight passed over the crowd and landed in a pool not far away.

The carefully carved steps of the blue crystal stretched down, showing a semi-circular arc, enclosing a dough ball of the sacred pond, spreading to both sides into the blue light mist, the length of which is unknown.

The sacred water in the sacred pool is rippling slightly, and in the absolutely bright and transparent water, there are countless dense blue light spots, like living things, shuttled back and forth in the transparent water.

Gorgeous and dazzling, like a galaxy falling here.

Bai Donglin's eyes trembled slightly, and Lan Ling's personality was concealed, and the self-consciousness regained control of the body.

The sacred pond of the Ice and Snow Shrine was indeed not simple, he had never heard of it before Sanguang Ice Soul Shenshui accepted the task.

Gently inhaling the water vapor in the surroundings, the spirit orifice in his body suddenly cheered, and the flesh and blood particles shuddered slightly.

The whole body was filled with hunger and thirst, and couldn't wait to swallow this divine water.

"Sisters, let's get started, everyone should work hard, hold on for a while, the more benefits will be for our practice!"

After speaking, the leading sister untied her belt, her long skirt slipped down, revealing Miao Man’s white body, her arms crossed her chest, and with a low laugh, she gently jumped into the sacred pool, excited. The starting point is a splash, and countless bright spots of light splash into the void.

"Gluck, sisters, let's go down too, I've been waiting for this day for a long time!"

Amidst the frolics, the true and vivid goddesses of the Ice and Snow Palace took off their long skirts one by one.

Barefooted little feet stepped on the blue steps, while playing around, stepped down the arc stairs and slowly walked into the sacred pond.

Bai Donglin frowned, his expression solemn, and his eyes fell on the long skirts scattered everywhere.

Haha, these holy palace women are nothing more than that, so sloppy and embarrassing!

"Junior sister, what are you doing in a daze? Come down soon!"

The woman who entered the water last saw Bai Donglin still stunned, so she kindly reminded her.

"Hey! Hao Le, Junior Sister, I'm coming right away!"

Bai Donglin agreed casually. Now that the task is completed, he is not too eager.

Slowly open the dress, look serious, meticulously fold it neatly, and place it on the steps away from the water.

Wow!

The figure jumped into the pool for an instant, a peculiar chill surged into his heart, and Bai Donglin couldn't help but shiver.

Opening his eyes, countless blue light spots flicked in the transparent body of water, swarming into his body, and the white and petite body seemed to be covered by a layer of blue gauze.

"This feeling……"

Bai Donglin was shocked, sensing the spirit orifices that were madly competing to swallow the blue light spots in his body, all of them burst out with brilliant divine light, and a trace of strange changes emerged in the spirit orifices.

Most of the blue light spots were swallowed by the spirit orifice, and some fish that slipped through the net were captured by spirits and flesh and blood particles.

The entire body is submerged by divine light from the inside out, and the light of the origin of life emerges automatically, circulates endlessly, and the endless vitality becomes more and more intense.

His body is sublimating!

This is a qualitative change. This life sublimation process should only happen when it breaks through to the Divine Source Realm.

Bai Donglin suppressed the shock in his heart. He didn't expect that the Sanguang Ice Soul Divine Water had such a magical effect. As expected, these great forces had a lot of roots. Mastering this strange thing, their influence would naturally be prosperous.

"No wonder this Shenshui feels so familiar to me, it is very similar to the strange energy effect obtained in Thunder Tribulation Ocean before!"

Bai Donglin stretched out his palm, his eyes bright, staring at the blue light spots lingering on it.

"What is this energy?"

After thinking for a while, Bai Donglin didn't see what made it happen, and couldn't help but shook his head slightly. His cultivation level was still too low, and he didn't fully understand many things.

But now is not the time to think about this, and finally entered this treasure land, but it is necessary to make a good profit, and it is not wasted to spend so much effort on oneself.

Bai Donglin surfaced, and his little head looked around. Those holy palace disciples had already swam to the depths of the sacred pond. The surface of the sacred pond was surrounded by light and mist. The deeper the inside, the blue light fog became denser. The figure was shrouded in light and mist, and it had become slightly invisible.

These Sacred Palace disciples are not running blindly, but according to their own strength, to find a water area that suits them best.

According to Youfeng's entrustment to Lan Ling, the effect of this sacred pool is not uniform, but the deeper it is, the better the effect on the monks, but the more fierce chill comes from it!

This is the power of the Sanguang Ice Soul Divine Water. If you want to get the benefits it brings to yourself, you have to endure this biting chill.

You Feng repeatedly asked Lan Ling to do what she could, and not to rush into it. In the history of the Ice and Snow Palace, there were many disciples who were overpowered, who were frozen to death in this sacred pond.

"It seems that the spring of the Sanguang Ice Soul Divine Water is in the deepest part of this blue mist..."

"With my number of terrifying orifices, these peripheral areas can't satisfy me!"

Bai Donglin showed a slight smile, the spirit orifice in his body was too domineering, and the blood and blood particles in his soul couldn't compete for it, and he was already jumping angrily!

As for the chill, he feels very little now, and it is far from being an obstacle to him.

"Before this, let me first see if it is what You Feng said."

His eyes were drooping, and a movement of his thoughts would spur the eternal immortal world. The spirit orifice that carried this world gleamed with light, but was disturbed by a powerful force, and the black and white light could never spread out.

"Sure enough, the ancestors of the Holy Palace are not fools~www.mtlnovel.com~ will not leave such a big loophole."

In this ice and snow temple, all laws of space are forbidden, whether it is storage magic weapons, or space magic spells, etc., they cannot be used. The essence of eternal immortality is closely related to space, and it is reasonable to be imprisoned.

He tried it, but he was thinking of whether the dimensional space could take advantage of the loopholes. The result was a failure. It also showed that the Ice and Snow Palace still had something, not that simple.

Bai Donglin didn't care too much about this either, his eternal light world was still too weak, and he would naturally not be in such a situation when he grew up in the future.

Pressing his mind, Bai Donglin turned into a ray of divine light and dived into the depths of the sacred pond.

His body is already hungry and thirsty. Since he can't take it away, he will swallow it fiercely!

His belly is huge.
