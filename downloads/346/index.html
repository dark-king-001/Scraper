In the dark sacred pond of the Ice and Snow Temple, a golden light gleams across the transparent body of water, stirring countless blue light spots at an extremely fast speed.

This light is naturally Bai Donglin. He has smoothly walked out of the spring's eyes. After obtaining the blue obelisk, he did not delay much in that dangerous place, nor did he intend to explore the female corpse.

There must be a big secret in the female corpse, and there may be great treasures and massive resources in her dantian.

But the unknown crisis should not be underestimated. The Sanguang Ice Soul Shenshui suppressed him too strongly, and his immortality was greatly restricted.

Bai Donglin was not eager either. Only he could enter the spring by burning the three lights of the human body. Afterwards, he can resist the cold of the divine water, and it will not be too late to explore. All the treasures will belong to him.

"Huh? What is that?"

The flying golden light suddenly stagnated, and Bai Donglin's figure appeared, hovering in the water body, surrounded by raging golden flames, with golden light shining in his eyes, looking down the pool.

He seemed to sense a faint breath of life just now, and it passed away in a flash.

His brows were slightly frowned, but he had just flew more than 20,000 miles away. This area still belonged to the depths of the absolute sacred pool, a terrifying life restricted zone. When he came, he had never seen any living creatures.

After thinking for a moment, Bai Donglin's figure swayed and dived down. The divine water chill here was a bit weaker. He could resist for a moment, enough to ignite the three lights a second time, and without worrying about life and death, he became bolder again.

After diving for hundreds of miles, Bai Donglin came to the bottom of the dark sacred pond, and stepped on the solid dark crystals with his feet. The golden flames all over his body made him look like a human-shaped torch, brightening the Baizhang area.

Glancing at the bottom of the endless crystal pool, a trace of pity flashed in his eyes. These dark crystals are precious treasures, one level higher than the blue crystals used in the construction of the temple complex in the Ice and Snow Palace.

Without storage space, he was not good at digging arbitrarily, shook his head slightly, pressed his mind, and walked away following the induction just now.

Boom!

Another wave rang out in Bai Donglin's perception, rippling waves, and his eyes lit up. This time he felt more clearly. It was the sound of heart beating, with an extremely faint breath of life.

The speed increased and turned into light to instantly reach the place where perception was locked, Bai Donglin's eyes condensed, this is—

There was a beautiful woman lying on the bottom of the pool, curled up, her body covered with dark ice, and her frowning eyebrows carried a hint of awe-inspiring aura.

"What kind of baby is this? It makes you reluctant to let go when you are about to freeze to death?"

With surprise on Bai Donglin's face, his eyes moved. The woman's enamel white jade held a colorful crystal flower tightly in her hand, firmly guarding her chest.

He stepped forward and observed it carefully for a moment, but still did not recognize the origin of this colorful crystal flower, and there was no record in his vast memory.

This woman resisted the cold of Shenshui and was able to walk to this depth. She was undoubtedly a powerful powerhouse in the ninth stage, so that even a powerful person could not bear to let go, and she could know with her heels that this crystal flower must be a good treasure.

A dim white light appeared in Bai Donglin's eyes. He looked at the woman and found that the three lights in her body were like candles in the wind, which would be extinguished at any time.

In the light of his will, Bai Donglin sensed a strong unwillingness.

This is a secret technique in the "light" of the eight parts, which can peep into the three lights of the human body.

"This woman should be a high-ranking member of the Ice and Snow Palace, risking her life to go deep into this place, just for this colorful crystal flower?"

Bai Donglin instantly saw the cause and effect. This sacred pond is the supreme holy artifact of the Ice and Snow Holy Palace. According to the rules of the sacred palace, there should be no blasphemy against it. Those who enter the sacred pond cannot carry any foreign objects except their own bodies. .

"Well, this time I got so many benefits, it can be considered as a tribute to your holy palace, so I will help you!"

The reason why it is to help, not save a life, is because this woman is a powerful and powerful person.

He insisted on not giving up, but was reluctant to bear the colorful crystal flower in his hand.

He leaned forward and gently picked up the curled up woman. The pitch-black crystal slowly melted when it touched the golden flames of his body. When the two skins were close together, Bai Donglin was shocked.

"I didn't expect it to be you!"

Above the Nu River, the bronze box, the cross-border passage, the evil alien powerhouse, and the finger that finally fell from the sky and obliterated everything...

The skin kin made Bai Donglin feel the woman's breath clearly, and the breath fluctuating deep in his memory was consistent with it.

"Hehe, I really have a fate for thousands of miles. I didn't expect to meet you here. At the beginning, you solved a problem for me, although I was also killed by you..."

"Helping you this time can be considered as an understanding of cause and effect!"

Bai Donglin smiled slightly, and secretly sighed the ingenuity of fate. When his figure moved, he led the woman into golden light and fleeed to the outer area.

—

Um? ! what is this…

So warm and comfortable!

Why? Didn't I look for the Seven Souls of the God Flower in the sacred pond, and then I was trapped in the restricted area, and I was about to freeze to death?

hateful!

Not reconciled! I finally found it...

But what's the matter with this warm feeling? Is it an illusion before death?

—

The corners of Bai Donglin's eyebrows twitched slightly, and the cheeks of the woman in his arms rubbed against his chest. One hand touched it, while the other hand holding the colorful crystal flower did not move.

Hehe, woman!

The speed suddenly increased by a few points, and after escaping more than tens of thousands of miles, the terrifying chill in the divine water weakened by a few points.

Bai Donglin's eyes were drooping, and there was a dim white light floating in the depths. Watching the Sanguang within the woman's body was recovering, he nodded slightly. At this position, her cultivation base should be able to resist.

Released his hands, opened the woman's limbs wrapped around him, and allowed it to fall to the bottom of the sacred pond. Without hesitation, Bai Donglin's figure swayed to the outer area.

During the journey, the figure distorted and changed, and he put on the blue spirit's skin again. He had to quickly absorb the strange energy. It is best to go out with the true disciples, so as not to stay too long and cause the high level of the Holy Palace to pass. Pay attention.

…

After one month.

"It's the limit!"

"The spirit orifice, the flesh and soul particles, all have reached a saturated state, and can no longer absorb a trace of strange energy..."

Click! Click!

Bai Donglin slowly opened his eyes, and the blue ice crystal on the surface of his body shattered and fell, and a horrible aura that was nearly 70% stronger than before was diffused, instantly shattering the vortex of light that enveloped his body.

The strange energy in this sacred pool was different from the energy he used to accumulate spiritual orifices in ordinary days, otherwise his infinite spiritual orifice would have no upper limit.

It is a peculiar evolutionary power, which is very similar to the peculiar energy that monks raise the realm and make the life level jump.

"It's just over a month, and the combat power has increased by 70% out of thin air. This time it's really profitable!"

Bai Donglin clenched his fists, feeling the surging terrifying power in his body and the spirit orifice particles full of Shen Xi's light, and his face showed satisfaction.

"Well, sure enough, Lan Ling's vest can't be abandoned yet. After breaking through the Divine Origin Realm, he can come in and devour it again."

Pressing down on his thoughts, the figure flees to the outside as soon as it moves, passing by a few true disciples who are immersed in cultivation.

In this peripheral area, the singular energy is extremely thin, and their physique is not comparable to Bai Donglin, so they can only slowly absorb it carefully, so it hasn't been saturated yet.

Wow!

Lan Ling emerged from the water, her long black hair attached to her white body, and slowly stepped onto the crystal stairs, the essence of the body boiled, evaporating the water stains on the body surface, turning into a hazy white mist lingering around her body.

"Oh? Junior sister, are you all right, too?"

Lan Ling glanced around and saw the senior sister who had been neatly dressed on the shore and led them in before.

"Lan Ling has seen Senior Sister, her body is already saturated, and she can no longer hold a trace of the power of the sacred pool!"

Lan Ling smiled sweetly, and replied with an obedient look. At this time, Bai Donglin's self-consciousness had disappeared, and it was Lan Ling's personality that was dominating the body.

"Well, Junior Sister Lan Ling, let me wait here for a while. When the other juniors come out, we will leave the temple together."

"Good sister!"

Lan Ling nodded in agreement, found his neatly folded dress, and put it on quickly.

After waiting for another three days, all the disciples left the Shenchi, and under the leadership of the senior sister, they twittered all the way out of the temple.

Inside the sleeping hall, You Feng looked at Lan Ling expectantly, and asked:

"Good boy, how did you get this time?"

"Master, please see!"

Lan Ling's gaze condensed, and his whole body became extremely cold in an instant, there were mysterious patterns in his eyes, and a faint blue ice phoenix appeared out of thin air behind him, spreading its wings soaring.

唳—

"Okay! As expected of Master's precious disciple, well, it's great!"

You Feng looked surprised, and squeezed Lan Ling's face dotingly.

"Hehe, it's the master who taught well, and the disciples dare not take credit."

Lan Ling's expression was obedient, and then the conversation turned around and continued:

"Master, you should also know that when Disciple came to the holy palace, there were five senior sisters who came together. Disciple hadn't seen them for so long, so I missed them very much!"

"Disciple, can I find them?"

You Feng was silent for a moment when she heard this. She was a young disciple, and it was normal that she could not let go of these sisterhoods when she first came to this strange place.

But now the identity between the two is like a cloud of mud, and the gap will only grow wider in the future. Cultivation is an endless journey. Companions who can't keep up will only drift away in the end.

These things are difficult for Youfeng to say clearly, she believes that Lan Ling will understand herself in the future.

"Well, that's okay, your senior sisters have already joined the inner door, and they are now in the mountainside area. You can find them by yourself."

"If you don't understand anything in practice, remember to come and be a teacher! Come on, take these things with you..."

After You Feng said, with a wave of his hand, several objects suddenly appeared in the void.

A splendid blue armor, a crystal sword, a token with the characters "Holy" engraved on the front, and a storage ring.

"This is the sect of the sect. You can go in and out of the mountain freely with this. And this, remember to take it with you. If you encounter danger, you can sacrifice your life."

You Feng took out another dark and deep jade medal from her arms and placed it solemnly in Lan Ling's hand.

"Thank you, Master!"

With a grateful expression on his face, Lan Ling stretched out his hand to take all the items, and Bai Donglin's self-consciousness nodded slightly. This Youfeng was really good to Lan Ling.

If he read the dark token correctly, there should be a clone of Youfeng sleeping in it. This thing is very troublesome to manufacture, and its value is immeasurable.

With Youfeng's permission, Lan Ling couldn't wait to turn into a streamer and flew out of the temple complex.
