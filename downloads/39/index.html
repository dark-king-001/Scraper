　　 Qi masters spells, body masters supernatural powers!

　　 Supernatural powers, this is the exclusive secret method of physical cultivation, or the supernatural powers of the soul, which is extremely precious.

　　Bai Donglin has no time to think about it, for fear of being ascended first by others, using his posture, leaving behind a long series of afterimages, and jumping off the stands.

　　 As the ripples surfaced, the figure disappeared.

　　 Bai Donglin felt like he had passed through a layer of bubbles, and instantly appeared in a wide dim space.

　　 In the center of the space, there is a huge stone table. On the stone table are suspended two light balls, each with a character.

　　 One is "Fortune", and the other is "War".

　　 Bai Donglin stared at the light ball, and there was information that came to his mind. It turned out that there were two ways to challenge this challenge, one to test luck, and the other to test combat power. Choose one method at will, and get rewards for passing.

　　 If you choose "Luck", there will be ten glasses of wine with exactly the same appearance, nine of which are poisonous wines that will kill you. As long as you choose non-toxic wine and drink it, the challenge will be successful. Of course, the wrong choice is to be poisoned and challenged to fail.

　　Choose "War", and a hundred monks of the same realm will appear in the hall.

　　Bai Donglin smiled slightly, but he didn't expect that this absolute palace had become quite humane now.

　　 But how did Old Man Liu pass such a difficult challenge? Is the difficulty of the challenge different for the precious rewards?

　　 It seems that this supernatural power is much more precious than dragon blood! It's not that he despise Old Man Liu, this kind of difficult challenge, it is possible that ordinary people will not pass without opening up.

　　 One is a life of nine deaths, the other is one enemy a hundred!

　　Bai Donglin has the confidence to face a hundred monks of the same realm and win the battle. He is immortal and has no fear of damage, and the least fear is the mass battle attrition.

　　 can win, but it is not necessary.

　　 He still chose a more labor-saving nine-death life, and reached out to grab the ball of light with the word "lucky".

　　 The ball of light breaks as soon as it is touched, and the word "yun" turns into a light spot and disappears, and the other ball of light also disappears.

　　 There was a flash of light on the stone table, and ten white jade cups appeared out of thin air, all of which were filled with clear drinks.

　　 Bai Donglin observes carefully, it is indeed exactly the same, no matter the color or the taste, it can only be chosen by luck.

　　 "As long as you drink non-toxic wine, you can pass the challenge, right?"

　　 Bai Donglin chuckled, and took out an iron bowl from Sumi's ring. This was something he was eating.

　　 Put the bowl on the table and pour all ten glasses of wine into the bowl. It is very painful to be poisoned, so let's do it all at once!

　　 Bai Donglin picked up the iron bowl and drank it without hesitation.

　　 "Good wine!"

　　 Before the words fell, the whole person and soul all turned into nothingness, and in a breath, the naked Bai Donglin appeared out of thin air.

　　 "Mom! This poison is too overbearing, right? It's nothing more than turning people into nothingness, even the little master's clothes are melted away?"

　　 Bai Donglin was stunned, and quickly picked up the Sumi ring. Fortunately, the material of the Sumi ring is known for its hardness. This poisonous wine is also mainly aimed at the human body, but it is not harmful.

　　 took out his clothes and put them on. At this moment, a wave of fluctuations appeared in the dim space, sweeping back and forth, as if confirming something.

　　 Ripples in space emerged, and Bai Donglin disappeared.

　　 In the Colosseum, unscathed Bai Donglin appeared out of thin air, and the black jade tube floated over automatically.

　　Bai Donglin took it excitedly, good stuff, cultivating the supernatural powers of the soul, this time I made a lot of money!

　　 put the jade tube into the Sumi ring, ignoring the shocked gaze of everyone in the stands, his thoughts disappeared, but he left the Colosseum.

　　 The challenge here can only be carried out once per person, and it’s useless to stay here, so I simply enter the next place, get this absolute palace early, or leave as soon as possible.

　　...

　　 The pictures flowed, and when Bai Donglin regained his sight, he had already appeared in a piece of ice and snow.

　　 stretched out his palm, caught the snow like feathers, the cold wind was blowing across his face, so real, this is indeed a real world.

　　 This sand sea absolute palace is probably really related to the corpse that fell from the nine heavens. The small world is included in the palace, so the method is incredible.

　　 Withdrawing his thoughts, Bai Donglin followed the guidance in his mind and walked towards the towering snow-capped mountains in the distance. The challenge of this small world is to climb to the top of the mountain.

　　 It doesn’t feel difficult.

　　 half an hour later.

　　 Halfway up the mountain, Bai Donglin was panting, his hair and eyebrows covered with ice.

　　 "What the **** is this place! It's so cold!"

　　 Bai Donglin was shivering with the cold, the thick blood in his body circulated wildly, and the temperature brought by the high-speed qi and blood barely maintained the core temperature in his body.

　　 If the core temperature in the body is too low, it will lead to organ failure and death!

　　 This place is too weird, and he doesn't know how cold it is, but it is definitely not easy to freeze him like this. This is not a naturally formed low temperature, there must be a formation to maintain all this!

　　 The only thing that satisfies him is that the low temperature here triggers the damage reversal, although there is only a small amount of energy.

　　 The damage caused by the low temperature here is very weak, at least not fatal. The only thing that makes him sick is that the negative effect is too terrifying.

　　 is not only sluggish action, he feels his brain freezes and his thinking slows down.

　　 Resting for a while, Bai Donglin continued to climb up. The mountain was extremely steep. He needed to insert his hands into the hard ice and climb up little by little, just like rock climbing.

　　 Not long after crawling, a gust of wind suddenly blew again, and Bai Donglin stuck to the ice wall deadly. The cold wind showed a quiet color, as if it could blow into the bones and souls of people.

　　Bai Donglin feels that his soul will be blown away!

　　 Along with the cold wind, an inexplicable aura descended. This aura seemed to come from this world, preventing him from climbing!

　　Bai Donglin felt that his consciousness was beginning to be addicted, and there seemed to be a voice in his mind saying give up, don’t resist, the power of heaven and earth is irresistible!

　　 Click—

　　 A crack appeared in the ear, which was blown off by the wind, and there was no trace of blood in the wound.

　　 The click of the ears awakened Bai Donglin. It was dangerous. Fortunately, he was determined, otherwise he would be recruited. He immediately stabilized his mind, strengthened his mind, and confronted the ubiquitous world.

　　 It won't work anymore, if you continue like this, you will fall. You can't die, but you have to start again. With one effort, it fades again, and it may not be able to climb up again if you do it again!

　　 Bai Donglin forced his spirits up and took out a fiery red stone from the ring of Sumi, and a thin layer of flame was burning on the surface of the stone.

　　 He didn't know what kind of stone it was. He got it from a lottery in the box. He only knew that the stone would emit high temperature.

　　 Bai Donglin wanted to open his mouth and swallow the stone into his stomach, but found that his mouth and throat were frozen, his muscles were also stiff, and he couldn't open his mouth at all!

　　 thought for a while, his thinking was already very slow at this time, and he had to think about things that he didn't need to think about at all at this time.

　　 took out a knife from the Sumi ring.

　　 Without thinking about it, he held the long knife with one hand and cut open his chest with a single knife. Thanks to the low temperature, his defense became weak and he could easily cut his chest.

　　Bai Donglin pressed the flame stone into his chest and placed it next to the heart, and a wave of heat was conducted into the heart. com flows through the blood all over the body.

　　 The blood temperature is getting higher and higher, and it runs faster and faster, and the whole body is warmed up!

　　Hu—

　　Bai Donglin opened his mouth and let out a sigh of white breath, and finally he could move again. The real cowhide almost froze to death.

　　 Feeling his hands regained his strength, Bai Donglin continued to climb up, not far from the top of the mountain.

　　 In the cracks in his chest, red flames flashed.

　　 Super Hero Steel Bones? Or Ultraman, the giant of light?

　　Bai Donglin feels that he is more like Ultraman now, and will never be defeated, especially when the red light is flashing, he can always burst out powerful forces to defeat the enemy.

　　 This is the will of light that will never yield!

　　 was suppressed by the general situation of the world, and almost froze to death by the low temperature. Not only did he not give in, but he could come up with such a perfect solution. He felt that he was a genius!

　　 Super recovery ability, tenacious will, coupled with the heat provided by the flame stone, Bai Donglin paid the price of two ears, one leg, and three fingers, and finally climbed to the top of the mountain.

　　 Unexpectedly, the temperature at the top of the mountain is very normal, warm as spring.

　　 Bai Donglin lay on the ground and panted, his body temperature gradually returned to normal, and his recovery ability gradually improved.

　　 Ears, fingers, and legs all grew out, and the internal organs squeezed out the flame stone, and the wound healed quickly.

　　 Within a dozen breaths, he was resurrected with blood.

　　Bai Donglin, who had recovered, got up and looked around, and found that there was a circular stone platform in the middle of this small mountain top.

　　 The stone platform is engraved with patterns, and there are countless fist-sized **** of light floating on the stone platform.

　　 The ball of light spins and dances, forming a huge vortex between the stone platform and the sky.

　　The white light is flowing and shining, brilliant and colorful.
