　　 Three days later.

　　 After a long journey, Bai Donglin and the Iron Bone Sect finally arrived at the main city of this land boundary.

　　 There is no national dynasty in the wasteland, all huge main cities dominate the vast land.

　　 is similar to the city-state system of ancient Greece in the past, but these main cities are all subordinate to the Supreme Sect. The city owners are all disciples of the holy sect, and they are regarded as the outer forces of the holy sect.

　　The area of ​​the wasteland is too vast, and how many people there are and how many ancient cities there are, even the native Iron Bone Sect people don’t know.

　　 Looking at the majestic Taishang main city in front of him, a vast, ancient atmosphere came upon his face, Bai Donglin was shocked, this is too majestic!

　　The city wall thousands of feet high is all made up of huge piles of black iron. The surface is covered with inscription arrays, which are extremely hard and leave no traces of the passage of time.

　　 The main city of Taishang is too huge. I don't know how many miles of the wall spread, and there is no end in sight. I just learned that the main city has a permanent population of more than 400 million people, and the area should be very large.

　　 There are no guards at the gate of the city, and you can enter and leave at will when the Grand Ceremony of Receiving the Apprentices of the Jidao Sect is held.

　　 Bai Donglin followed the flow of people and walked through a cave nearly three hundred meters long. The width of the city wall is also amazing.

　　 There is a forbidden air circle over the main city, except for the official disciple of the holy sect, the rest cannot fly. Therefore, even monks with advanced strength can only walk honestly.

　　 No one in the wasteland dared to disobey the rules set by the holy sect. It can be said that the ultimate holy sect is the sky of the wasteland and dozens of surrounding areas! The rules of the saints are the rules of the sky!

　　 just walked into the city, there was a huge shout around:

　　 "All the people who want to participate in the assessment come and gather, and go to the assessment location through the teleportation circle!"

　　 Bai Donglin turned his head and looked around. As expected, a huge teleportation circle was set up on an open ground, on which was already full of young people who were going for the assessment.

　　Bai Donglin and the few lads who participated in the assessment of Iron Bone Sect bid farewell to everyone, and then walked towards the teleportation circle together.

Bai Donglin was originally not eligible to participate in the assessment. The Iron Bone Sect had five recommended places. Unfortunately, a disciple died in the attack of the fierce beast, so this place was given to Bai Donglin as a reward. Of life-saving grace.

　　 That is to say, people who can come to the main city to participate in the assessment now have no idea how many screenings have been passed!

After 　　 waited for the staff outside the circle to verify their identity, Bai Donglin stepped onto the teleportation circle smoothly.

　　 This Taishang city is really rich and powerful. Although the main city is huge, it is not enough to use the teleportation circle to drive on the road. It seems that these main cities are extremely cautious about the affairs of the holy sect.

　　Before stepping into the teleportation circle, Bai Donglin felt a wave of fluctuation scanning his body. According to the Iron Bone Sect, this is the first test to check whether the examiner is under twenty years old.

　　 Ji Dao Shengzong’s apprenticeship ceremony is held once every 20 years, and anyone under the age of 20 can participate.

　　Bai Donglin is only fourteen years old this year. Of course, it’s okay. Even if you look at the soul, you can’t see any trouble. He is reincarnated in a serious manner, and his soul is not afraid of all kinds of prying eyes.

　　 After the people in the teleportation formation were full, the formation revolved, white light flashed, and Bai Donglin and others had already appeared in a huge square.

　　 The square is densely packed with people, but it is extremely quiet, and no one is making noise.

　　 There were many women in uniforms in the crowd that attracted Bai Donglin's attention. Each of these women was holding a child in his hand. They were of different sizes, ranging from newborn babies to several months old.

　　Bai Donglin thought for a while and understood what was going on. If these children wait for the apprenticeship ceremony in another 20 years, they may be over twenty years old, so they sent someone to hold them for the assessment.

　　The background of these babies is not simple. Ordinary people certainly don't have this treatment, and there is no need, because there is almost no possibility of passing the assessment.

　　 And these babies who can come in for the assessment show that their blood is special, their ancestors have had big people, and they have the possibility of awakening special physiques.

　　 And as long as you have a special physique, you can pass the test directly, so even babies can try their luck.

　　 As the teleportation circle lights up again and again, the square is getting more and more people, gradually filling up, Bai Donglin estimates that there are 100,000 people.

　　 At this moment, a young man suddenly appeared in the front stand, with a handsome temperament and a smile on his face, who looked like a very gentle person.

　　 The young man glanced at the square that was already full, nodded in satisfaction and said:

"Zai is one of the inspectors sent to the Taishang main city under the Jidao Sect, and is now responsible for the assessment of the 25th examination room of the Taishang main city. Please abide by all the rules of the assessment. If you violate it, you will be expelled immediately! "

　　 "The assessment officially begins now! Activate the bloodline physique induction array!"

　　 As soon as the man's voice fell, a huge formation covering the entire square was instantly activated.

　　 Countless golden runes jumped and flowed, and a burst of golden light flooded everyone.

　　Bai Donglin only felt the golden light flowing through his limbs, and every cell particle was sensed by the golden light. Then, as if nothing was sensed, he slowly withdrew from his body.

　　 He breathed a sigh of relief. Sure enough, immortality and injury reversal, these two abilities are not defying constitutions, but more advanced things that transcend conceptual dimensions.

　　 What Jin Guang couldn't find out was what he expected. He didn't want to expose his biggest secret for the sake of a small assessment. It was completely unnecessary, and he could easily pass the assessment with his strength.

　　 At this time, there were also changes in the square, and some people began to appear out of phases. This is the special physique being aroused!

　　 Everyone's different phases are different, and Bai Donglin has no knowledge of this aspect, so he can't recognize what kind of physique it is.

　　 The formation method slowly stopped working, the golden light converged, and the first assessment was over. A total of seven people were detected to have special physiques, and four of them were from those babies and how old they were!

　　 Everyone on the square looked at the awakened physiques with envious faces. This is a step up to the sky, it has surpassed them too much!

　　Bai Donglin nodded secretly, sure enough, something like physique is more of a bloodline passed down from generation to generation.

　　 It is still very rare for ordinary people to have physiques such as mutation back to ancient times.

　　 Seven people with special physiques were taken away on the spot. They no longer need to undergo the next assessment. They are already disciples of the Holy Sect, and geniuses are given special treatment everywhere.

　　 A large number of children and babies on the square also exited the square. Since they have no special physique, they will not be able to pass the next assessment, so they have to retreat. It can only be said that their lives are not at the right time.

　　 The young man in the stands nodded in satisfaction. There are seven special physiques that are still very good. The proportion is already very high, and then he said:

　　 "Extreme Dao Saint Sect can only be joined by real geniuses! But there are too many of you! Too many! Extreme Dao Saint Sect does not need so many people!"

　　 "Activate the heavy rock formation, will force the formation! Persevere until the last 100 people pass the assessment!"

　　 "The second test begins!"

　　 buzzing—

　　 With two buzzing sounds, two huge formations, one gray and one red, were activated, like two huge rune auras, covering all the more than 90,000 people in the square!

　　Bai Donglin only felt his body sink, as if gravity had increased, and he also felt a wave of pressure in his mind. But to this degree, it was just a breeze blowing on his face, without any discomfort.

　　 As time goes by, the power of the magic circle is getting bigger and bigger, ten times the gravity, fifty times the gravity, and one hundred times the gravity.

　　 Someone can no longer hold on when the gravity reaches one hundred times, UU read www.uukanshu. Com is equivalent to a 100 kg person carrying ten tons of pressure, and it still acts on every cell and every organ!

　　 Coupled with the devastation of a hundredfold coercion in the mind, these people can persist to the present, and they are all geniuses who can obtain the qualification for the assessment!

　　 When the formation was strengthened again, more than half of the people fell down, and the formation flashed instantly, and all those who failed were teleported out.

The 　　 formation continued to increase, reaching two hundred times the gravity, and 10,000 people remained.

　　 Three hundred times the gravity, there are a thousand people left.

　　 Five hundred times the gravity, with three hundred people left, Bai Donglin began to run the exercises.

　　 The remaining three hundred people are definitely the elite of the elite, whether it is physical or spiritual!

　　 It's a pity that there are only a hundred people, and the formation continues to increase. This gravity directly affects every cell. Fortunately, Bai Donglin's strengthening energy strengthens every part of his body at the same time, and there are no shortcomings and loopholes.

　　 This assessment method can be said that he is taking advantage. Even if other cultivators have begun systematic cultivation, it is impossible for him to strengthen every cell of the body to the same perfection.

　　 And his willpower has been tempered so much, it can be said that his will is tougher than his body!
