The firm voice seemed to be a certain announcement, rippling to the extreme distance of the dimension space.

King Mu looked solemn, instantly comprehending the meaning of the purple-gold flame, raised his hand to the room, and said in a condensed voice:

"Friends of Taoism, please go inside and talk in detail."

Bai Donglin nodded slightly, and entered the bamboo house with King Mu, and the two sat cross-legged.

"Fellow Daoist, you flame, magical powers, what price does it cost to use?"

King Mu directly asked the biggest doubt in his heart, which was his, or the common sense concept of the monks, deep in their bones.

For example, the force is mutual, and the use of magical powers also consumes energy. Whatever you obtain, you will naturally pay a price.

This purple-gold flame can kill the crime of killing so efficiently, and in ordinary people's mind, eliminating such a difficult and strange crime of killing will naturally consume something.

In Mu Wang's view, although Bai Donglin's purple-gold flame is against the sky, if the price is too great, it will be of little significance to the entire battle.

"Friend Mu, I know your concerns, but my ability is different from what you understand."

Bai Donglin shook his head, staring at the jumping flame in his hand, his eyes faintly continued:

"This is not a magical power, nor is it a secret technique, nor is it a creation of heaven and earth, a rare treasure..."

"This is my own talent, a peculiar talent for killing sins. There is no price to use it, but it will consume a lot of energy. As long as the energy is sufficient, I can continue to move without any worries!"

When King Mu heard this, his eyes were even more surprised, and he looked up and down incredible the unusually hot purple-gold flames. This is something that breaks common sense, but in this world, the talents are endless, and who can know what kind of strange talents will be born. ?

Pressing his thoughts, King Mu continued to ask: "So, will we pay a price for us to endure this fire?"

This is another important point. If this flame can cause huge damage, then how is it different from karma? This is impossible for the mighty to accept.

And as far as King Mu knew, talent is often immune to the releaser himself, but it is hard to say to outsiders.

"Friend Mu, you also know that the sin of killing penetrates every corner of the body. The divine power particles of the physical body, the spirit orifice, the will, and the soul, including your Qi cultivation form, are extremely difficult to remove."

"My talent will burn every corner of the body. The pain is sure to be intolerable, but the damage caused by the burning of sins will be returned in a strange way, as if those injuries are false illusions under pain!"

"This is serious!?"

"nature!"

Bai Donglin's rhetoric was eloquent, without blinking his eyes, he said that he would believe it himself.

"Friend Mu Dao, no matter how much you say, you might as well try it yourself, starting from the physical body, if there is a fake, you can easily stop it!"

"good!"

King Mu's eyes were shining, he and Bai Donglin had no hatred and no grudges, and they were just physical bodies, but he was not afraid that he would be unruly towards him.

Bai Donglin nodded slowly, the flames in his hand rolled to separate a purple gold thread, shot out, and inserted into King Mu's palm.

A huge pain appeared, and King Mu felt the subtle changes in his flesh as if he hadn't noticed it.

Unsuspectingly, the scorching purple-gold flame burned together with his divine power particles and the black fog of sin. The sin quickly dissipated, and his body was also severely injured, but in the next moment, a warm current flowed from the flame. Appeared in the middle, and then his physical body recovered as before!

it is true! Bai Donglin didn't lie to him!

"Fellow Daoist Bai, do your best! I believe you!"

Mu Wang opened his eyes fiercely, staring at Bai Donglin, and said word by word.

"Hahaha, good!"

This King Mu is a wonderful person.

Bai Donglin was no longer suppressed, and the purple and gold flames boiled instantly, like a Tianhe flooding, rushing into every corner of King Mu's body, will and spirits, etc., everything is not fortified, and the hot flames are allowed to spread wildly.

The purple flame seems to be a contradictory combination of vitality and destruction. The ultimate destruction of the black mist of sin and the powerful damage to its attachments are real, but that powerful vitality is happening at the same time as the damage occurs. Then it was restored perfectly.

Everything seems to be an illusion, but the crime of killing that has disappeared is true.

After a short while, the purple-gold flame that cleared all the sins and black mist retracted into Bai Donglin's palm, and King Mu slowly opened his eyes, feeling extremely relaxed all over his body, and extremely clear in his eyes.

"Thank you Fellow Daoist Bai for your help!"

King Mu gently bowed his hand to Bai Donglin.

"Ahem! Fellow Daoist Mu is polite, we are all for the human race, alas, but there are two problems before us!"

Bai Donglin sighed softly, his expression a little worried.

"If you have any difficulties with fellow Taoists, just speak up, we can work out a solution together!"

"Well, it's actually not a big problem, it's that this talent consumes a lot of energy, and my reserves are almost exhausted."

"But I have already thought of a solution. Fellow Daoists, do you know the sage's stone hidden in the core of the law of origin? I have a method to motivate, and I only need this..."

King Mu nodded slightly when he heard the words, and a flash of suspicion flashed in his eyes. They destroyed the world easily, and the energy consumed could be recovered by themselves, so they did not have the idea of ​​hitting the core of the law of origin. They have always made a clean and neat move to destroy the world.

For Da Neng, energy is not a rare thing, what they yearn for is the perception of the rules.

"This is a trivial matter. Anyway, it must destroy the world. It's just another way. I'm going to get the sage's stone for fellow daoists!"

After Mu Wang said, he pressed the long sword in his hand, and he was going to kill the small alien world.

"Friend Mu, wait a minute, one more thing, I don't know much about the great powers, so I can't contact them. Can you help me pass this news?"

"Let my human powers, bring the sage's stone, come here, so that I can clear them of the crime of killing!"

Because it is far away from the only real world, the instant messaging with the imprint of the battle soul is ineffective, and the great abilities of King Mu naturally have special means of communication between acquaintances. Once passed on, the news will soon be in the hundreds of billions of powers. Spread.

Moreover, the presence of King Mu, a living propagandist, can dispel the worries of the powerful people and save him a lot of tongue.

"If you don't tell me about this matter, I will do it again. The crime of killing will trouble the Daoists. Please wait here. I will go back!"

"No trouble! No trouble! Everything is for the human race!"

"Yes! Everything is for the human race!"

Mu Wang nodded heavily, and his figure moved, turning into sword light and disappearing.

"For the human race, but also for myself..."

Bai Donglin whispered, looking up at the depths of the dimension, his eyes are extremely deep.

What kind of talent Zijin flame, the core essence is still karma, the power to eliminate the sins of killing naturally comes from karma, but he has added a lot of things into it.

The harm caused by the sins of karma burning and killing is inevitable, and even he can only rely on his immortality to resist.

And if he wants to clear his sins without causing injuries to King Mu, like Zijin Flame just now, the first thing he must consider is how to solve the injuries.

Immortality is a gift that belongs to him alone and cannot be passed on to others, but this problem is not difficult for him.

This power of restoration basically used the undead and immortal talent, it was just a roundabout.

To achieve this, the two magical methods derived from the "Eight Parts" have played a very important role.

"Eight Lights" explains how to use the three lights of the human body to kill the will of living beings. The "light blade of will" comes from this infinite method. One of the many mysteries of the strong is "healing"!

Burning the light of life can heal physical injuries.

Burning the light of the will can heal the wound of the will.

Burning the light of the soul can heal the wounds of the soul.

The fundamental core is "burn yourself and illuminate others!"

Still he is immortal, this method can be used at will.

From comprehending this method, he had only used it once before, and that was to heal Fang Miaomiao's physical wounds on the battlefield of Tianjiao.

Today, it is much more complicated to recover the injuries caused by the karma fire. It is all-round and uses the three lights together.

This is not difficult, but the difficulty is how to conceal it, otherwise his inexhaustible three lights of the human body would expose the secret of immortality?

So I used the "Ba Bu Zhi Yu", the method "Yu", expounding the control of all things in the body, the fusion of energy, laws, spirits, etc., is extremely powerful.

Karma fire and the three lights of the human body are all very peculiar things that are not comparable to ordinary energy. It is not easy to integrate them. It depends on this mysterious "imperial" help. He has been experimenting a long time ago, and now finally Successfully merged them.

In this way, the Zijin Flame was born, which possessed the ability of karma, and while burning and killing sins, the three lights in his body burned, and the continuous healing power was input, which perfectly restored the injury.

It has not only achieved his goal, but also perfectly concealed the fire of karma and the three lights of the human body, which is perfect!

He pushed everything to the mysterious and unpredictable talent, no matter whether others could do it or not, he believed it anyway. But in any case, he possessed such a powerful ability, from now on, it can be said that he has been completely exposed to everyone's eyes!

When he easily decides something as difficult as the crime of killing, he naturally has strategic significance. Fortunately, he is not weak now and has a lot of methods, and he is not afraid of being caught by others for slicing and research. s Choice.

Bai Donglin has only one condition. It is easy to solve the crime of killing, but it must be exchanged for energy!

Although he won't lose anything at all, his talents are strange and unpredictable. Why not let him describe it? Who can question him?

Perhaps, with the idea of ​​consuming huge amounts of energy, everyone would better accept it. After all, there is no consumption to remove the sins of killing, which is even more shocking to the world.

"Hehe, it's worth taking some risks. Hundreds of billions of power can be used by me. The energy of these small alien worlds is all in my bag!"

Helping the human race is only incidental, and energy is his goal. The demise of the world of the gods is a foregone conclusion. Whether he does this will have no decisive impact, but it will speed up this speed and reduce the battle damage in the real world.

"I don't want to destroy the world now, and I have a steady stream of sage stones delivered to my door..."

There was a trace of pride in Bai Donglin's eyes. No matter how hard he worked, he would not be able to reach a hundred billion army!

If he doesn't do this, most of the small alien world will only be meaninglessly transformed into nothingness, and only a very small part can be used by him. Such a huge waste, just thinking about it, makes him feel so distressed. breathe.

Yin—

A sword chant came from the depths of the dimension space, interrupting Bai Donglin's thoughts.

A few streams of light fell outside the bamboo house, and King Mu appeared and walked into the house with five extraordinary powers.

This Mu Wang is so fast!

"Friend Mu Dao!"

"Friend Bai Dao, come, let me introduce to you..."

With a smile on his face, Bai Donglin greeted everyone politely. Are you polite? These are his treasure-sending boys!

The five great powers also looked up and down at Bai Donglin, their eyes gleaming, a little doubtful.

After the greetings were completed, everyone took their seats. King Mu flipped his palms and took out seven shining sage stones. While handing them to Bai Donglin, he said in his mouth:

"Friend Bai Dao, I met a small world on the way, so I cut it off and took this sage stone to ease your energy consumption."

"Brother Mu is interested!"

Bai Donglin was not polite, reached out his hand to take the Sage's Stone, then looked at the five mighty powers, and slowly said:

"Dear friends, who of you will come first?"

The five people looked at each other, the old man with white beard and hair nodded slightly, and he appeared in front of Bai Donglin when he moved his figure.

"Friends, trouble you."

They do not believe in Bai Donglin as much as they believe in King Mu. They are close friends among them. They are clear about King Mu's meticulousness and will never make fun of them.

"Nothing."

Bai Donglin spread out his palm, and a group of purple and gold flames emerged, shunting out, and submerged in the old man's body.

boom!

Stimulated by the flames, the crime of killing suppressed by the old man suddenly became restless, the pitch black mist rolled and roared, the magic sound was piercing, and the coldness was biting. The amiable grandfather just now had his eyes scarlet red, like a devil!

Bai Donglin turned a blind eye, and the purple and gold flames became more and more surging, and the flames rose hundreds of thousands of meters high. Fortunately, the bamboo house was so huge that it was spared.

After a while, the flame dissipated, and the black mist of sin dissipated. The old man opened his eyes in disbelief, and looked at his hands with clear and warm eyes.

"Next!"

Bai Donglin kissed for the first time in a voice, and his whole body was enveloped in endless mystery, showing a masterful demeanor.

Extremely compelling!

"Me! Me! I'm coming!"

"Let me try it!"

"Friends of Daoist choose me, trouble you!"

Bai Donglin waved his hand slightly, pointed at a great power at random, and said with a gentle and easy-going smile:

"Don't worry! Don't worry!"

"Come one by one, all have a chance..."
