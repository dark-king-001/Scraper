Da Neng's expression was faint, his breath was extremely cold, and his body was faintly sprayed with a dark mist. Obviously, the crime of killing in his body was too heavy to be suppressed, so he was so eager.

After he said that, he had to leave in his sleeves. With this time, he might as well just kill his sins honestly.

Li Wenhao is a powerful Confucian cultivator who has reached the pinnacle of the king. Haoran's righteousness also has a miraculous effect on the crime of killing, making him instantly lose the thought of leaving.

call out!

Li Wenhao bowed his hand, his expression was gentle and surprisingly polite.

"Naturally! How dare you play around with everyone here?"

busy!
