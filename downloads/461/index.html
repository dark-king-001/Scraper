"Teacher Dangan, please wait! We have already reported to our ancestors, you are different from those monster races, you don't have to leave!"

Liu Dongchuan spoke earnestly. He had a good relationship with Dangan when he joined the Dan League, and a lot of Dan Dao knowledge was imparted by him. Getting along for a long time, even willing to call the other's teacher, naturally understand Dangan's temperament, Dangan has long since left the dragon clan, is a real good old man.

"Yes! Teacher Dangan, you just wait and see how the ancestor responds."

Luo Dian, who has always liked to face Liu Dongchuan, is also rare to stand in the same position and speak out together to persuade him. This shows that Dan Gan is indeed capable of doing things!

The vast old voice like Tianyun came from the sky, interrupting Dan Gan's affectionate farewell, the void was twisted and shattered, and a white void appeared, and a golden decree slowly floated out.

The ancestors really responded to them!

Everyone was shocked, and then bowed to salute, Dangan also faded away from the dragon body and transformed into a human form, kneeling on the void.

"I've seen the ancestor!"

The golden light decree floated in the void, and the dense words on it, dragons and phoenix dancing, were names, and huge voices continued to be heard from the decree.

"Dangan, if you meet the requirements of our Human Race, you can continue to stay in the Human Race area, but you are not allowed to hold the position guarded by the Dan League."

After the last voice was finished, the law turned into a beam of light and shot into Dan Gan's eyebrows. A strange golden pattern appeared where the mark of the battle soul was originally engraved, which was a kind of identity certificate.

"Thank you ancestors!"

Dan Gan looked excited, and bowed his head towards the slowly healed white void. Although he could not stay in the Dan League, he was very satisfied to stay in the human race area. The wandering dragon race like him asked him to leave. He really didn't know where to go in such a vast world for a while.

"Congratulations, Teacher Dangan!"

The figures of Liu Dongchuan and others appeared around Dangan and congratulated. This is indeed worthy of congratulations. The monster race in the only real world is so vast that it can’t be counted. This shows how precious these eight thousand places are. , How difficult it is to pass the review of the ancestors, I am afraid that a trace of inferiority will be immediately brushed down.

"The ancestors are kind!"

Dan Gan also sighed that their mere 18,000 monsters could make the ancestors specially open a meeting. This was flattering. He was really flattered. The way of dealing with the human race was really different. It would not let any enemy or let it go. Friends who have contributed to the human race are chilling.

Of course, it is reasonable not to hold important positions in the human race, after all, the human heart is the most unpredictable, and no one can guarantee whether these monsters will betray the human race in the next moment.

If there are loopholes in some important positions, the loss can be great. Sometimes, when a building collapses, the problem often appears in the details. With the wisdom of the ancestors, it is naturally impossible to leave such a "pit". , Everything is drip-proof.

Liu Dongchuan was overjoyed, and asked inquisitively, "Teacher Dangan, what are your plans for the future?"

"Hahaha, the world is so wide, I want to take a good look, you guys take care, I will come back to see you!"

Dan Gan's voice is extremely cheerful, not only because he has won a precious quota, but also because of his contributions over the years, which is recognized by his ancestors, which is a manifestation of his own value.

"My old man, go too!"

In the middle of a huge island continent in the east pole of Minghai, the imperial capital of the Great Xia Dynasty, and Bai Donglin's sisters and brothers stood side by side on the observatory, looking up, perceiving the heavenly secrets of fierce change and surging.

Bai Donglin narrowed his eyes, perceiving the entire Qianyuan Realm through the ubiquitous battle monument, and naturally what happened just now in the Heart of Nine Rings was also seen by him.

"Well, this Dan does a good job and can be absorbed into the Tianluo space. Although the old man is a little older, I just like this kind of old good person. He will help me with my mission wholeheartedly..."

From the beginning of the Tianluo space, one thing has not changed, that is, every Tianluo host has a daily task that must be completed every day. If you don’t do it, Tianluo points will be deducted, the grade will be lowered, and even Being completely deprived of Tianluo space.

Of course, if the host needs to retreat or has something to do with it, he can apply for a mission postponement.

The reason why such a demanding task requirement does not arouse resistance from the hosts is because this daily task called "Does a Good Day in a Day" appears to the hosts to be strange, but without any difficulty.

In general, a certain place needs to be flooded with droughts for years, or a certain place needs to be solved by a volcanic eruption, an earthquake, or a tsunami, or it is to kill demons...

For them, these tasks are very simple, and it takes a little time to get Tianluo points, and they have already regarded this "doing one good day" as a welfare.

The Tianluo Host is currently close to 5 billion. Bai Donglin's small task seems normal, but with a huge base, the effect is terrible.

Every day, because of this task, the number of mortals who survived is no less than one trillion. With the accumulation of time, Bai Donglin himself does not know how many people he has saved, at least, much more lives than he killed.

This is the rule that he had pity for mortals when he broke the blood river sect's trafficking of human babies.

He is only treating the symptoms, not the root cause, and doing his best. If he wants to fundamentally change the pathetic status of mortals, he still has to look at his eldest sister.

Thinking of this, Bai Donglin couldn't help but open his eyes and looked at Bai Yuanzhen, who was imperial and majestic. If the Great Xia God Dynasty included the entire mortal beings of the only real world into the territory, how powerful would his older sister be?

"Little brother, do you think the eldest sister can develop into Qianyuan Continent?"

Bai Yuanzhen noticed Bai Donglin's gaze, and asked with his head sideways, with deep meaning in his eyes. Her little brother is even more powerful than her, and he has reached the point where he is now just by relying on his own efforts. It is extremely mysterious, so she takes Bai Donglin's advice extremely seriously.

When Bai Donglin heard the words, frowned and pondered. At this time, the Bird Scale Armored Demon Race withdrew from the Qianyuan Realm, and there was a large land of no owner above the Qianyuan Continent. Normally, this was indeed a good opportunity for power expansion.

But Daxia Shenchao is different. What his eldest sister needs is not a broad territory, but a population!

Regardless of the fact that the major forces of the human race usually don’t pay much attention to the mundane world, the "eyes" all over the place also show their attitudes. The massive population of the mundane world is an important channel for the birth of genius by various forces. The Great Xia Dynasty wants to swallow others. The cradle of genius, this is undoubtedly a conflict.

He used to worry that Bai Yuanzhen would attract hostility due to the dispute over luck. Now this point has been circumvented by Bai Yuanzhen cleverly using the "power of faith" to replace the "power of luck", but the population dispute is again on the table. superior.

"Sister, if you want to fight alone like this, you have to face the entire traditional practice world, which is undoubtedly very disadvantageous."

"Actually, the contradiction between the Great Xia Dynasty and the traditional cultivation system is not so absolute. Those forces want geniuses, and the eldest sister, you want population, and there is no requirement for qualifications. Just give those geniuses to them, and you can even help select them. !"

Bai Donglin looked at the sky with his hands on his back, countless characters passing by in his eyes. To make the most essential and correct analysis, the first thing is to have an understanding of all the information, that is to say, the insight must be outstanding and lofty. After Bai Donglin was promoted to the Jidao Councillor after digesting a lot of information, he met this condition. The nature of the world was at least more thorough than Bai Yuanzhen could see.

"Sister, the first thing you have to consider now is how to obtain a position that can negotiate with the major forces on an equal footing. The current Great Xia Dynasty is still a bit short..."

Although Daxia Shen Dynasty has developed so well, it is far behind those old forces. To cooperate, you must at least have the qualification to talk with it. Unfortunately, Bai Yuanzhen does not currently have this qualification. , I'm afraid it will be swallowed so much that there are no bones left.

"So, what should I do?"

Bai Yuanzhen frowned, and Daxia Shenchao had now entered the bottleneck of development. She had to take advantage of the powerful springboard of Qianyuan Continent to develop into the depths of the starry sky. As long as this difficulty passed, there would be a vast ocean and sky behind.

"Sister, have you heard of the Civilization Development Department?"

Bai Donglin's eyes were smiling. With him on the go, naturally he had already figured out a way out for his eldest sister.

"Civilization Development Department?"

Bai Yuanzhen thought for a moment, and shook her head in doubt. She started from scratch from start to finish. For the acquisition of secret information, she might not even be as good as Bai Jiange, let alone compare with Bai Donglin.

"The Department of Civilization Development is an organization that belongs to the God Court. Before the era of chaos, this was an extremely violent and powerful organization. Compared with the current inspection department, it is far inferior."

"It's a pity that after the era of chaos, the UU Reading www.uukanshu.com human race forcibly interfered in the direction of the development of civilization and caused a catastrophe. The Department of Civilization Development has taken a lot of responsibility for this and has since fallen."

Bai Donglin's tone was faint, his eyes seemed to span time and space, and he saw the rampant and lonely end of the behemoth of the Department of Civilization Development.

"Sister, you need a platform for you to flex your muscles. This Civilization Development Department also needs an opportunity. I think you can work hard together."

"Haha, but..."

"All this will be done after my body comes back, otherwise someone will bully you, eldest sister, huh, I won't suffer from someone else."

Bai Donglin narrowed his eyes slightly and his tone was confident. Although he had lost contact with his body, he couldn't help himself. He would surely upset the world of the gods and return to the real world again. Everything was definitely different.

"Brother is mighty and domineering!"
