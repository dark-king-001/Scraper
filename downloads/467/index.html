Bai Donglin's will carefully looked around the goddess of war. At this time, the goddess phantom was sober. There was a dense stream of characters passing by in his wide-open eyes, but he turned a blind eye to Bai Donglin who was close at hand.

"Hey, it's the old way, let you know everything through you!"
