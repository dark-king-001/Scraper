Jidao Shengzong, the origin law of the sea.

Bai Donglin went deep into retreat and practiced with all his strength. Another year passed without knowing it. The rules of Dao Ji are changing every moment. This is a growth visible to the naked eye.

The time to open at any time accelerates, outside one year, in fact, he has been practicing for another two to three hundred years, the sage stone has been exhausted, but he still has not broken through the gods and demons.

The reason why it is so slow is because the number of foundations of the rules he established is too high and the quality is too high. The limit in ordinary monk's cognition is to smelt nine kinds of laws, and he has smelted thirteen kinds of laws on each of the foundations.

However, the birth of the power of distance rules is already only separated by a layer of film.

Break through the film and survive the tribulation of the gods and demons, you can enter the ninth realm of the gods and demons and become a mighty power!

"Huh? This is..."

Bai Donglin's eyebrows trembled slightly, and he slowly opened his eyes, looking at an unknown place, an extremely distant coordinate.

"Huh!? It's weird!"

Bai Donglin pinched his fingers again and again, but for some reason there was an unexpected situation, which was not the same as the plan he had made at the beginning.

"According to the back hand I left, after the birth of Lan Ling, he will immediately break the mystery in the fetus and awaken the memory of previous lives. And the back hand left in the true spirit will also immediately determine the dimensional coordinates to lead me to come!"

"But why is it delayed for six years?"

Bai Donglin raised his brows slightly, and he paid more attention to Mother River in his heart. He thought his plan was perfect enough, but he almost fell short.

If Lan Ling dies again, then the true spirit will return to the mother river, the methods he left behind will be completely meaningless, and even the true spirit of the blue spirit will never be recovered.

"Huh! Mother River, I am really more and more curious about you."

Bai Donglin pressed his mind and didn't have the intention to continue cultivating. He only had a kick. If he wanted to, he could make a breakthrough immediately, without having to spend time in retreat.

The thoughts are numerous, but they are just moments. This is the "thinking freezing time and space" that belongs to him alone. In a breath of time, he can use the speed of thinking to extend it hundreds of millions of times. This is a kind of "bullet time" in thinking.

Click!

Raising his hand to open the passage, he didn't care if it was the hinterland of the monster race in front of him. Today, he has no place he dared to go, stepping out, he has already arrived in that desert.

The invisible breath permeated, and everything around, including the law, fell into stagnation.

The hot wind blowing, the falling sand, the hideous and ugly monster race, the palm of the hand was reaching Ling'er, everything fell into a stagnant time and space.

Only the sobbing Ling'er, and Bai Donglin walking out of the crimson crack, were not stagnated by time and space.

The light in the eyes flickered, and the will poured out, completely covering this side of the star world.

Causes and consequences, all things, regardless of size, were all read by Bai Donglin's mind, and a glimmer of understanding flashed in his eyes.

Compared with the plan, the result is a distortion that cannot be ignored.

"Tsk, the time is wrong, the place is wrong, and even the race is only half right. The mother river is still too difficult to figure out, and of course, it is also the reason why my methods were too naive."

"To achieve this level, I still rely on Lan Ling's special true spirit, and I don't know what it will gain."

A glimmer of expectation flashed in Bai Donglin's eyes. Originally, according to his plan, Lan Ling would not reincarnate in a few years, and the location was in the Qianyuan Realm, the same human race, but the facts were hugely distorted.

Reincarnated as a blue spirit bird!

Although this blue spirit bird is a bit special, it has awakened a certain powerful bloodline, and it is in the shape of a great road once it is born.

This star realm has a core continent and seven stars of life, all of which belong to the "Blue Spirit Bird" line of birds, and they are not big or small forces on one side.

Don't look at this blue spirit bird as if it has no other advantages except for its good-looking appearance. In fact, their ancestors still have a little history.

It is said that the phoenix breeds nine chicks, namely Golden Phoenix, Caifeng, Fire Phoenix, Snow Phoenix, Blue Phoenix, Peacock, Dapeng, Thunderbird, and Gale.

This blue phoenix is ​​the ancestral vein of the blue spirit bird. It is now mixed like this, only because the power of the blood vein is weak, but there are special circumstances, such as this blue spirit.

The Dapeng bird, which is also a nine young chick, is still a royal family with a line of birds. It can be seen from this that the blood of the Blue Phoenix will not be weak.

"This blue spirit still hasn't awakened the memory of previous lives. The trigger of the dimensional coordinates was only caused by the intense emotional fluctuations that caused the spirits to turmoil."

Bai Donglin thought for a moment, but gave up on waking Lan Ling forcefully, because doing so might cause some information to be lost, and it was far from waiting for her to wake up, and he didn't lack this time.

"Uuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuu...

Ling'er was immersed in grief, completely ignoring everything in the outside world, not even paying attention to the arrival of Bai Donglin and the stagnation of time and space.

"Your father is dead."

Bai Donglin just glanced at it and saw through the situation of Zhang Yun's remnant, a glimmer of appreciation flashed in his eyes, he could be regarded as a man, at least a good father.

Burning everything about himself, the flesh, the soul, the form, everything that can transform energy, was burned by Zhang Yun.

He himself often does this kind of thing, and he naturally knows the pain contained in it. He is a strong man. If he can, he doesn't mind saving his life.

It's a pity that Zhang Yun's true spirit has been taken away by the mother river, and there is nothing he can do about it at the moment.

"No! I don't listen, I don't listen! Father promised me that he will not die and will not leave me alone!"

Bai Donglin shook his head helplessly, and he really didn't know how to comfort the little boy who was rolling around Sapo. After excluding the modification of the method of erasing the memory, he could only give him a strong medicine.

"Ling'er, your talent clearly tells you that your father is indeed dead."

"You can choose to live in a false self-deception, or you can choose to face the reality and avenge your parents. Haha, I will help you for the sake of our fate."

Bai Donglin didn't use any means to interfere with Ling'er's will, just a plain tone, but Ling'er's small body trembled, stopped crying, slowly got up, looked at Bai Donglin with scarlet eyes and said:

"What you said is true? Will you help me get revenge?"

Ling'er clearly knows how powerful the Blue Spirit Bird clan is. This huge star realm belongs to them. There are countless strong people in the clan. Let alone revenge, she would die under the chase under normal circumstances.

The blood of the Blue Phoenix was taken away, and he died tragically.

"As long as you avenge me, I am willing to give everything!"

Little Ling'er was only six years old, but after the death of her parents, she matured instantly. Such examples are not uncommon. In this cruel world, there are not many warm times for children to enjoy.

"Hahaha!"

"Your name is Lan Ling, right? From now on, you will follow me."

"Introduce again, my name, Bai Donglin, remember it?"

Bai Donglin remembered the first meeting with Lan Ling, it was immediately interesting, and when his thoughts moved, he thought of how to arrange Lan Ling.

"Yes, Lord Bai, Linger remembered it."

Xiao Ling'er bowed in a salute. Although his small body was clumsy and funny, he was extremely serious and meticulous.

"Tsk, what is your name..."

"Call my brother later."

After Bai Donglin said, he condensed the overflowing breath, and the stagnant time and space suddenly flowed.

"Who are you!?"

A few bird monsters were lost for a while, but they didn't notice it, only a flash of sight, and suddenly a stranger appeared in front of them.

"Ling'er, you can do it yourself."

Bai Donglin didn't even glance at a few ants, the law chain emerged automatically, binding the monsters in place.

"Omen of death!"

The monster body is hard, even standing still, it can't be hurt by Linger who has never practiced before. Give Ling'er the omen of death reduced to a dagger, and its built-in destructive power is enough to kill these harpies.

"Ok!"

Ling'er nodded earnestly, and took the pitch-black dagger. These people were the culprit who killed his father, and it was they who had exhausted his father to death!

"Yeah! Go to hell!"

Ling'er held the dagger high, the blue light in his eyes shone, and he saw through the body of the bird demon, and directly named the deadly demon pill.

Swish.

After a few knives fell, the bird monsters were all crushed by the demon pill, and the scarlet destructive power flashed through and turned into nothingness.

"Yes, I will give you a present."

Seeing Ling'er's clean movements, Bai Donglin nodded in satisfaction, and then handed over a white ring.

"This is?"

Ling'er showed doubts, and she felt a familiar breath on the ring.

"I felt an extremely strong obsession in your father's corpse. It was an obsession to protect you. To be honest, this obsession is difficult to appear in the practitioners of the Dharma Realm."

"So, I added some materials and refined your father's body together with obsessions into a protective magic weapon, so that your father can still protect you after his death."

Bai Donglin's face was calm, telling the origin of the ring.

Linger's expression changed when he heard the words, and turned around to look, and sure enough, his father's body was gone.

"My father..."

Ling'er held the ring tightly, and tears began to appear in her eyes again. He didn't expect that after her father died, she was still worried about her safety.

"Let's go, get out of this place."

Bai Donglin stretched out his hand and hugged Ling'er. There was no way that the height difference was so great that he couldn't even hold his hands. Stepped out in one step, and came to the void of the universe.

"Brother Donglin, where is this?"

Ling'er put away the ring, raised his eyes and looked out, it was a continent, seven stars, and a big day. This is the first time she has entered the void of the universe, everything is so strange.

"Don't you want me to help you get revenge? Have you seen these mainland stars?"

"Above them, the blue spirit bird clan lives."

Bai Donglin squinted his eyes slightly, his tone was indifferent, and he couldn't keep things that he could do easily.

"Vengeance? Now!?"

Ling'er looked dumbfounded, the blue spirit bird clan, but very powerful!

"You just look at it."

Bai Donglin stretched out his hand to explore the void in front of him, crossing the void, and the star below was plucking a leaf.

Emerald green leaves, ordinary, can be seen everywhere.

The law of matter floated slightly, and the leaves were immediately decomposed into the smallest particle state, the dimensions were distorted, the same particles were realized, and they began to superimpose crazily.

With the size unchanged, the mass of the last remaining particle has surpassed a trillion tons, and Bai Dong, who felt almost like it, stopped the superimposed particles.

"Ling'er~www.mtlnovel.com~ Please watch a firework."

call out!

With a flick of the fingertip, the particles were shot out instantly, soaring to a thousand times the speed of light.

The void was extinguished, a ray of light flashed across, and seven stars were penetrated and exploded in an instant, followed by the big sun, which was broken into pieces by the particles. The particles were casted unabated, and they slammed into the core continent, which contained extremely powerful kinetic energy. The particles, having completed their mission, exploded suddenly!

"Okay, awesome!!"

Ling'er's sapphire blue eyes widened, and only then did she understand what a terrifying figure this Donglin brother was.

With a flick of a finger, pick the leaf and destroy the star.

Annihilated one side of the star realm, but in a blink of an eye.
