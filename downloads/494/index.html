"Are you going to kill this person?"

An ancestor of the Yaozu standing in the depths of the dimension, holding a scarlet long sword in his hand, his eyes have been locked tens of thousands of light-years away, Bai Donglin, who is fighting fiercely in the robbery cloud, can be killed if he wants to.

"No."

"There is a message from the Lord of Thunder that this person has a special life-saving method, and he has resisted a blow without injury. According to preliminary calculations, it should be a treasure similar to the lost puppet."

"The puppet can't die for the gods and demons."

"Look at it for the time being. The Lord of Thunder's situation is very bad. The Shou'er catastrophe has signs of coming early. This divine and devil catastrophe is very weird. Did he run away?"

"It can only be so."

The power of the ten realms is immense, and one galaxy can be wiped out with a gesture, but they dare not attack the Jieyun at this time, for fear of attracting unknown. After all, a strong ten can be more valuable than a barren realm.

If you want to destroy it, just let you destroy it, take out a transcendent galaxy cluster, hundreds of billions of galaxies will destroy you, you can add more if it is not enough, this is the inner thoughts of the ancestors of the monster race.

Damn it! Rumble!

The raging Jieyun does not stay for anyone's will. It is unceasing, endless and endless terrorist attacks, falling one after another, and within a million light years, it has become a forbidden zone of life.

"kill!"

Bai Donglin looked awe-inspiring, and his figure flickered in the sea of ​​thunder, fighting wildly with many powerful emperors. Relying on immortality, all means are used, either to suppress or defeat the opponent's divine body, but it is difficult to completely kill it. Anyway, these are the powerhouses who have reached the top.

"Are you coming?"

Bai Donglin's eyes narrowed slightly, and most of his attention was in the azure blue robbery cloud. A hot breath was condensing, and an invisible sense of crisis lingered in his heart.

boom!

Scarlet, gray, brilliant gold, three groups of flames of different colors bred out of the robbery cloud, beating and burning slightly, these flames seem to be the natural enemies of life, so that the great energy far beyond the robbery cloud can see. I feel an invisible fire burning in my body.

"This thing is..."

Bai Donglin sensed the abnormality in his body, his expression changed slightly, and he quickly stopped the behavior of burning his body to increase his combat power.

"This flame is an attack against the Three Lights of Man!"

In the ice and snow temple, he has experienced the ice divine water that can freeze the three lights of the human body, and these three-color flames are just the opposite, which can burn the three lights of humans.

Hum—

The three-color flame in the robbery cloud trembled slightly, then disappeared, and in the next instant, he appeared strangely in Bai Donglin's body.

"Perception and reflection?!"

Bai Donglin cursed secretly, this Jieyun was too ridiculous. This attack method involved the law of cause and effect, and there was no way to evade it.

If you perceive it, you will be directly attacked, and even shielding perception is useless, because there is a sequence. You can only perceive its existence before the idea of ​​shielding perception arises. It is already too late.

boom!

The scarlet flame is attached to the light of life, the gray flame burns the light of the soul, and the golden flame burns the light of the will. It spreads wildly and is abnormally hot, melting and decomposing all the glory into nothingness.

Bai Donglin's divine body trembled and shrank to the size of particles. At this time, he had no more energy to fight fiercely with those powerful emperors, so he could only use extreme speed to dodge, and go all out to deal with the Sanguang incineration in his body.

Fortunately, I stopped these secret techniques of extreme sublimation in advance, otherwise I would have to break the critical point of immortality and be burned to death in an instant.

"Just take this opportunity to break through the sixth state of will!"

Ordinary monks will be burnt to death in a moment when they are caught in this terrifying flame. There is no good way to deal with it. Bai Donglin is not afraid, and his immortal recovery ability is enough to balance the speed of his burning.

Moreover, this flame is different from the way he usually burns himself. Although it can't increase the strength of the burning brilliance, it has another characteristic, tempering!

Every time Sanguang is burned and restored, it will become more pure, the impurities in it seem to have been burned, especially his will on the verge of breaking through, the feeling is more obvious.

The fifth state of the will is "distorted reality". As long as the will is strong enough, it can affect the reality perception of tampering with everything in the world, and it is well understood that it affects the perception of living things. To tamper with the will of all things, such as burning a piece of paper with a ball of flame, you can use the will to tamper with the will of the paper, making it think that the flame is extremely cold, then it will not be burned, but will be frozen.

The sixth state of the will is "absolute reality", which is the sublimation and transformation of the will, and the will of the illusory concept will be transformed into a real and immortal substance with various powerful powers.

Will is a very powerful force, and even such powerful characters as anglers attach great importance to it.

Under normal circumstances, the monk will only ignite the fire of will when he breaks through the ninth stage, and Bai Donglin has just begun to cross the calamity, and he is about to break through the sixth stage, which is true against the sky.

From this we can also see the metamorphosis of this divine and devil calamity, using this terrifying flame to burn the robbers, if it is not immortal, it will not be able to survive, and it will undoubtedly die.

"The will is the heart, the will is me."

"All things and everything are for intention!"

Bai Donglin, who was as small as a particle, was taken over by the mind of God and I, again and again, avoiding the attacks of the emperor's power in a thrilling and perfect way.

All thoughts are immersed in the light of will, perceiving the unswerving transformation, the invisible and intangible brilliance under the burning of the flame, gradually turning into reality in countless births and deaths.

"It's finished! The sixth stage, absolutely real!"

hold head high-

As soon as the mind moved, the brilliance of the will suddenly turned into a chaotic beast named Gourmet. With a big mouth swallowed, the terrifying golden flames were instantly sucked into the abdomen and wiped out.

Rumble!

The tri-color flame was unable to make merit for a long time, and was even wiped out. Jieyun suddenly became angry, becoming more and more violent, rolling and boiling, all kinds of attacks fell frantically, and the purple Jieyun spreading millions of light-years, the color became deeper and deeper.

"The tenth catastrophe is about to end..."

Bai Donglin's eyes flickered, and his expression became more and more cold. He knew that there were many strong monsters waiting outside the robbery cloud at this time, waiting for the end of the gods and demons to capture him.

He is also waiting, waiting for the power of this robbery to rise to the limit.

Yin—

In the tenth purple robbery cloud, a crisp sword chant suddenly resounded, and a boundless, huge sword light emerged, and the purple light was faint, and it was extremely awe-inspiring.

"Cut Dao!"

Seeing this purple sword light, the sound of Taoism resounded in his heart at the same time, and his expression closed his eyes in amazement, shielding his perception, but no matter how it was used, or even cut off the memory just now, that terrifying sword light still suspended. Above my heart.

"Huh! Want to cut my way? You can try!"

There was a sneer in Bai Donglin's eyes, his way, whether it was born or passed, was determined by his will.

Without me, there is no way, there is me and the way.

His way, can one side of the robbery cloud be wiped out?

"come on!"

Bai Donglin looked fanatical, not retreating but advancing, facing the purple knife light that was smashed and shot out. In his eyes, this path is very large and very small, neither real nor virtual, between existence and absence, no way. The hearted person can't even observe this knife light.

It was born to cut the Dao, and it is unobservable for the innocent.

The light of the knife swept across, ignoring all obstacles, including the spatial distance and time dimension, between the moving thoughts, it was already cut on Bai Donglin's Dao Heart.

clang!

The clash of the two intangibles made a real and true loud noise. There was a monster with an unstable Dao heart. Hearing this sound, there was a sound of cracking in his heart, and the Dao heart was broken.

"Hehe, that's it? Keep cutting me!"

With so many catastrophes, this last and strongest cut-off catastrophe, on the contrary, poses the least threat to Bai Donglin, and can be completely ignored.
