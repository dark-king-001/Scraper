"My lord, this should be done by the human race. They killed your heirs for no reason. We will never let this matter go!"

"No, it's not the time to go to war with the human race."

The human will tone is indifferent, but full of endless majesty and irreversible. The supreme leader of the Witch God Realm has supreme power, and his words represent the entire Witch God Realm.

"This……"

Several ten-level powerhouses looked at each other, but they still took their orders to retreat. After all, it was not their son who died, and they weren't in a hurry.

However, the witch god's ruthless indifference also made them admire, killing his wife and son was like a joke.

"Next……"

Bai Donglin was worried about the long spear in his hand, with a sense of comfort in his eyes. This kind of dimensionality reduction strike, one shot at a time, is really cool.

Xuetu's brows were slightly furrowed, and the ten-level powerhouses were able to spur ancient artifacts because they possessed the power of good fortune and the power of mixing elements, these two high-level energies, and they only met the basic conditions for using ancient artifacts. , Often still need to pay a greater price.

"What a terrible kid! Maybe..."

A glimmer of anticipation flashed in Xuetu's eyes. Bai Donglin was undoubtedly the most enchanting monster he had ever seen. The most important thing was that he was extremely cultivating, and perhaps the rise of cultivating depended on this person.

After a long time, Bai Donglin put away the spear, and the notebook in his hand was turned to the end.

"All kinds of blood and deep hatred have been pressing on my heart, and it has always made me sleepless and sleepy. Now that I have been cleaned up, I can finally sleep peacefully!"

Bai Donglin sighed, and he couldn't help it. This old problem of holding grudges could never be corrected.

"The crime of slaughter has been burned to the end. This big drama can be over."

As soon as his mind moved, the karma fire that had become violent and powerful instantly retracted into his body. After being resisted by many calamities, Bai Donglin gave up his resistance and immediately turned into ashes and dissipated.

The weird will that is neither life nor death, even this powerful Jieyun can’t perceive his existence. According to the established rules of operation, the robber has "dead", Jieyun stagnates for a moment, as if he was sensing something, and then began Slowly dissipated.
