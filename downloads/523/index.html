After the will entered the Styx River, Bai Donglin clearly felt the difference from the last entry, and the will spread towards the direction of the abnormal wave, and in an instant he arrived at the terrifying battlefield.

The fierce battle between the powerhouses of the ten realms is extremely terrifying, not to mention the fact that they sometimes pay the price to activate the ancient artifacts. After observing the source of the mutation, Bai Donglin no longer hesitated, and with a single thought, he came to the bottom plane, the Wanyuan Plain, the birthplace of the Styx.

"These abyss monarchs really have small moves, do they want to pack the entire endless abyss, together with the Styx?"

Bai Donglin's will ignores all obstacles, shrouds it, and observes carefully.

"Why is this thing so familiar? Isn't this..."
