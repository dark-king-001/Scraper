This bizarre ancient artifact broke Bai Donglin's cognition. It had just changed hands, and there was no sign of rejection at all. Not only was he allowed to spy on it, but a practice method was also recorded in it.

The inheritance of this practice method does not pick people at all, and they rushed to the front and took the initiative to pour into Bai Donglin's mind.

According to records, the way of cultivation of the human race is obtained from ancient artifacts, but the ancient artifacts that Bai Donglin came into contact with have no inheritance, and the dimensional world is different. Above, it has nothing to do with the "dimensional world" of the ancient artifact body.

As for the dark stone tablet, it may not be an ancient artifact.

After a cursory glance at the inheritance that emerged in his mind, Bai Donglin's expression became more and more strange.

"The Supreme Book - "Ultimate Dream Shattering Knife"!"

"Can you go directly to the Eternal Realm on the other side? This realm sounds like it is more powerful than the only realm of the real me. Could it be the higher twelve realms?"

The supreme treasure book that can directly reach the twelve realms is indeed surprising. After all, even eleven realms in this world only exist in legends.

"Whoever likes to practice this shit, I can't practice anyway, or "The Pregnant God Casting Demons" is more fragrant!"

Bai Donglin looked disgusted, and swept all the green characters in his mind into the garbage. It is very likely that Bafumet has already cultivated, otherwise how could he become green, but unfortunately, it is obvious that no matter how high the level of the practice method is, it still cannot get rid of the blockade of the mother river on the realm.

"However, it's really amazing. The world is so big and full of wonders. Anything can happen. Even a mere "Dream Shattering Knife" can be transformed and sublimated to this level!"

"I don't know which ruthless person in the world of the heavens its creator is... Awesome! Awesome! It's really awesome!"

Bai Donglin was full of admiration. He thought he was cruel enough to himself, but compared with the creator of "The Ultimate Dream Breaker", it was nothing.

This is the real ruthless man!

Taking green as a way, there is no way to take all kinds of avenues, and only love and obsessed with the green hat sword method.

Looking at the flickering green hat in his hand, Bai Donglin hesitated for a moment, but put it away. Although it was a bit disgusting, it was an ancient artifact after all.

In addition to recording a treasure book, this ancient green hat also has two characteristics. One is that as long as the wearer is angry, the strength will rise wildly. Green light can protect the whole body.

This is also the reason why Bai Donglin chose to take the initiative to enter Bafermite's body. With the defensive blessing of the green hat, even a spirit slave can't erase his true spirit in an instant, and when he perceives a crisis, Bafermite will definitely flee immediately.

Only by breaking through from the inside can you bypass the defense of the ancient green hat and achieve a one-hit kill.

"Both of these characteristics are good, but the conditions for this incentive are too that!"

Bai Donglin pouted, this bizarre ancient artifact must be worn on the head in order to stimulate its power. I am afraid there is some terrifying curse contained in this broken thing to have such bizarre requirements. Anyway, he decided that he wouldn't use it if he died, and he was going to trade with the high-level Terran in exchange for what he needed.

Pressing his thoughts, Bai Donglin looked around, and there was nothing in the plane of nothingness except what he thought, and the inexplicably disappeared Taoist Master still did not appear.

"What the **** happened?"

"It's better to go and have a look, but don't make any changes, and Styx can't lose..."

As soon as his thoughts moved, Bai Donglin immediately self-destructed, and his strange will descended down the Styx River, shrouding the entire endless abyss in his perception.

At the end of the abyss, where the River Styx originated, the bald corpse that crossed knees again and again in the ancient times has not changed, and the twisted black shadow circling in front of it has not left.

The only change is that there is an extra white void on the top of the corpse's head.

With a move in Bai Donglin's heart, his will spread out along the white void, and he sensed the boundless outer dimension space and the dimensional storm of life and death.

"It turns out that holding two crystal skulls can not only manipulate the Styx to summarize the abyss plane, but also open the Styx to extend to one end of the dimensional space."

With his thinking, and the will has been perceived at this time, there are a few more powerful and evil figures in the abyss plane. These are not the abyss monarchs, but the ten-level powerhouses of other alien races.

"These abyss monarchs seem to be unable to swallow this breath. It is not enough to pack up and escape. They also want to collude with other aliens and surround the ancestors of the tribe."

At this time, the abyss plane has almost been wiped out, and the endless particles transformed by the plane have also been returned to the River Styx.

The perception of will continues to spread, sweeping through battlefields everywhere, except for the ten realms in the long river of time and space, the ancestors of the human race left in the world are already at a disadvantage, and the reversal of offense and defense is completely thought that these unexpected appearances appeared Alien powerhouse.

At this time, Dao Master Xuyou disappeared inexplicably, and only a hundred breaths passed. If it continues to delay, it is impossible to say that there will be casualties on the side of the human race.

The battle between the powerhouses may last for a long time, or they may decide to die in an instant. After all, they have all set foot in the realm of time. Who knows that in the frozen time node, the time outside the world is beyond the river of time. , the fierce battle lasted for thousands of years.

"Fortunately, due to the restraint of various battlefields, there are not many ancestral powerhouses that aliens can use silently. Otherwise, under the mental calculation, most of the ancestors of the human race are likely to be killed or injured!"

"The entire endless abyss is also isolated by the means of the powerhouse..."

The will continued to spread to the upper layer of the abyss, crossing a dark light film. The army of people and demons behind the light film was still fighting fiercely, but everyone at the bottom of the abyss knew nothing about the mutation.

Bai Donglin thought for a moment, but gave up and immediately closed the net. At this time, the big fish ran away.

"Prepare with both hands, at least ensure that the ancestors will not be killed or injured..."

After making a decision in an instant, Bai Donglin prepared to avoid the pitch-black light film that cut off everything, resurrected with the spirit of Wuwei as the basis, reported the situation to Battle.net truthfully, and then returned to the bottom of the abyss to use spirit slaves to help the ancestors.

"Huh? It seems that I've been thinking too much. The human race has a deep heritage, and it really isn't so calculated..."

"Tuguang devil cubs!"

"Protect the Endless Abyss! Drive these human invaders out!"

Roaring, roaring, the human race and the abyss demon have been fighting to the point of white-hot, on the boundless battlefield, countless creatures have fallen every moment, blood is flowing, and the Stygian River is stained more and more scarlet.

Boom! Click!

The area where the abyss crack was located suddenly erupted with a loud noise, which overwhelmed the shouting and killing on the battlefield, and a huge temple smashed the crack and descended on the plane.

In the shrine, there are several figures with powerful breath, and while looking forward, the infinite coercion makes the void whine.

"Humph!"

The headed man snorted coldly, and two breaths, black and red, spit out from his nose, circling and dancing, merging into a huge sword light, which instantly swept across the void.

The sword light flashed, and whether it was a human race or a demon on the battlefield, there was a chill in the heart, and the boiling blood seemed to solidify.

The screams of killing disappeared, and the entire battlefield fell into a stagnation for a moment. The black and red sword light flashed away behind the army of the abyss demons at an imperceptible speed. Billions of abyss lords, countless low-level demons, In an instant, all particles were cut off, and he died completely.

"Sword master, let these little scumbags train their hands below. The most urgent task is to relieve the troubles of the fellow daoists below."

The warm and melodious female voice came from the first family of Kun Mojie, the ancestor of the Yin-Yang family, and the master of Yin Kui.

At the same time as he opened his mouth to persuade, the Lord of Yinkui couldn't help but sigh in his heart. The Sword Master deserves to be known as the person with the most murderous intent among the human race, and is on a par with the blood slaughter ancestor who is the first in suffocation. For these little minions, the ten-level powerhouses generally disdain to shoot, but the sword master kills extremely calmly, and there is even joy in his eyes. This is just a simple love of killing.

The Sword Master, who is in charge of the Four Swords of Immortal Execution, and Xue Tu, who is in charge of the Divine Slaughtering Spear, each possess the most murderous ancient artifact of the clan, and their personalities will be affected to some extent, but the ancestors understand it very well.

"That's fine too."

The sword master nodded slightly, but the killing intent in his heart did not weaken in the slightest. He just agreed with what the Lord of Yin Kui said, that these little minions are indeed good tools for practicing.

The strong are all born in the fight, didn't he also survive on the battlefield at the beginning? The Ten Realms hardly intervene in the battles between the low-level monks. The most fundamental reason is that the big waves wash away the sand, and the last thing left is the gold.

The reason why he did it just now was just his anger at the calculation of the Endless Abyss. If it wasn't for the insider of the Witch God Realm to inform the news, I still don't know how much loss the Human Race would have suffered this time.

Boom!

The temple did not stop, and with an unstoppable power, it quickly fell to the bottom of the abyss, and the layer of black light film that cut off everything was directly smashed into pieces.

"Kill ah-"

The battlefield was silent for a moment, and then there was a boiling cry of killing, and endless human soldiers rushed up, constantly strangling the abyss demon.

The abyss demons have been frightened, and their troops are not as good as the human race. At this time, they were slaughtered by the inexplicable sword light, and they were even more invincible.

What terrified them the most was that the ten-level powerhouses of the human race slaughtered them, and the abyss monarch did not respond. Obviously, they were abandoned.

Abandonment and betrayal are common things among the abyss demons. After all, demons are ruthless and unrighteous creatures. In the eyes of the abyss monarch, as long as there is Styx in his hands, endless demons can be born, and they can be replenished sooner or later. .

Bai Donglin's will spread to this point, seeing the murderous sword master, he realized that the ancestors of the human race were already worry-free.

He has also heard the name of the sword master. There are four sacred places in kendo. In each era, a ruler will be selected from the sacred places. Big killer approval.

The Scarlet Master is the ancestor of the Dao Slashing Sword Sect, and the trapping sword in his hand is also given by the sword master. If the sword master needs it, he can recall it at any time.

There is also a rumor, it is said that each generation of sword masters carries the same task, that is to understand the "Zhu Xian Array"!

"I don't know if this generation's sword master has comprehended the secrets in the Immortal Execution Array. If you can arrange the first killing array of that door, it will be amazing."

"How many aliens are not enough to kill!"

Bai Donglin's thoughts were erratic, but he was very aware of the origin of the Four Immortal Execution Swords. This is definitely a big killer that can be ranked among the worlds.

"With the sword master, the killing god, the ending of the endless abyss is already doomed, it's just the difference in how much it can escape."

"Hey, I can't be idle either, going to fight Qiufeng, maybe I can pick up one or two ancestral corpses..."

The strange will spread disorderly, and they took one step before the sword master, and instantly came to a battlefield and resurrected out of thin air.

"Lord of the Court of God, be careful!"

"Junior came to help you!"

Clang!

The spear swung across the void, and the endless silver light shone brightly.

The whole body was shrouded in the fire of chaos, the golden flames in his eyes rolled endlessly, and the hot eyes glanced at the abyss monarch unscrupulously.

This is the look of the hunter looking at his prey, which is unbearable for Bafermet.
