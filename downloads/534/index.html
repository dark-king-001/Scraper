"Jiange, have you seen it? On the pale mountain, there is the mother body of the Silent Demon Sword on display!"

Bai Jiange and Jian Chi were both wrapped in thick gray robes, and their faces were hidden under the hoods.

"Senior Jianchi, since this magic sword is so powerful, why didn't the powerhouse who killed the aliens master it himself, but put it here?"

Bai Jiange swept his eyes obscurely around, and from time to time, a group of gray-robed people with strange breaths passed by him. The world of death and silence is the most sparsely populated alien race. Just like this lonely world, it is rare to meet a living person after walking for a long time. .

The reason why there are so many annihilated aliens here is also because of the annihilation magic sword on the Pale White Divine Mountain. Surrounding this mountain, there is a huge city at the foot of the mountain, which gathers many annihilated aliens.

"Moreover, the status of this annihilation demon sword is extremely special, look at them..."

Jian Chi raised his head slightly and glanced at the gray-robed man on the street who was kneeling devoutly towards the Pale God Mountain.

"These guys have regarded the Silent Demon Sword as their faith, and a force called the Holy Sword Sect has also been born, and this city is under the control of this force."

"We have to find a way to bypass their blockade if we want to get close to the sword of annihilation. Just in time, we are coming at the right time, and it is approaching their holy day of sacrificing the sword once every 10,000 years."

Jian Chi looked solemn, and couldn't help but tighten the long sword behind him. The two of them sneaked into this place and took great risks. If they exposed their traces, they might be besieged to death.

The long sword he carried behind him was the one he used to destroy the ancient world of Mingyu. It was a powerhouse of the annihilation of alien races. He collected the uniform breath of the mother body of the annihilation magic sword, and gave birth to the magic sword.

It is also the breath of annihilation emanating from this sword that can disguise them as aliens of annihilation and hide from the perception of those in gray robes.

"Senior Jianchi, do you really believe in this guy Moruo?"

Bai Jiange frowned slightly, he always felt that Moruo was too evil and untrustworthy.

"Of course I don't believe him!"

Jian Chi's eyes showed extreme anger. He had already been tricked by Moro once. This bald donkey promised him, but he didn't do it at all.

From the beginning to the end, he was the chess piece of Moruo and the world of death and silence. Moruo not only used him to destroy the ten villains in the ancient world, but the sword behind him also controlled his mind.

It was only a little bit, and he almost became a sacrifice to purify the mother body of the sword of annihilation, just because he had the special physique of the sword of congenital robbery, and he was the most friendly to the sword of annihilation.

It was a sad and worthless death, how could it not make him angry? It's a pity that he has a deadly handle and is held in the hands of Moro...

"Jiange, the true spirit of my close relatives is now in Moruo's hands. I didn't perceive it wrong. He did try to retrieve the true spirit. I had to do what he said."

"Humph! Moruo is still too arrogant, don't worry, even if the magic sword is handed over to him, he can't use it."

The two secretly transmitted their voices, and they walked into a huge restaurant during the conversation. It was not far from the holy sword festival. The believers who came from all over the world made the city seem extraordinarily noisy and prosperous.

The two rented a small courtyard and laid out a lot of isolation formations, and both breathed a sigh of relief.

"Senior Jianchi, what is the origin of Moruo? To be able to salvage the silent true spirit from the mother river, this kind of anti-sky method is really incredible!"

Bai Jiange's eyes were shocked. Until now, he has not digested this information. The mother river is supreme, and he does not know where it lives and is everywhere.

Ordinary people only know that all true spirits must belong to the mother river, and after being washed by the mother river, they will re-enter reincarnation. Really smart.

"Alas, this world is very big, full of unknown secrets, there are some unknown things, and it is normal."

Jian Chi sighed slightly, then took out the jug, filled Bai Jiange, raised the glass and drank it, his eyes were lonely, and he continued to say:

"The Mother River is great and mysterious, and what we humans lack most is the curiosity to explore."

"For endless years, the research on Mother River and True Spirit has never stopped, and some achievements have been made. The Mother River Corpse Hunter is one of these achievements, a group of very secretive guys."

"The conditions for their selection of inheritance disciples are extremely harsh. The most basic thing is to have the body of reincarnation and the spirit of insomnia. This is the most basic requirement. It is difficult to find a few people in an era, and Mora is one of them. ."

"No matter how sophisticated things are, there may be loopholes, and the corpse hunters are the loopholes in the mother river. They can go in and out of the mother river without being noticed by the mother river. This is the case with Mora. Words are like drinking water, and he also relies on this ability, I don't know how much secret knowledge he has acquired, he is a very scary and cunning guy!"

Jian Chi's expression was dull, he couldn't figure out why such a powerful ability that defied the sky had to appear on the evil guy Moruo.

"Jiange, your senior sister is very important to you, right? It's really embarrassing for you to be willing to follow this old man on an adventure."

Hearing this, Bai Jiange was stunned for a moment. He reached out and brushed the pot in his arms, feeling the familiar faint breath. His heart warmed, and his sharp eyes became much gentler.

"Yes, senior sister is very important, very important. She fell here to save me. I owe her. As long as I can save her, what if I kill Bai Jiange?"

His senior sister's true spirit was severely injured, and the chances of being able to successfully reincarnate are very small, let alone awakening the memory of his past life. I am afraid that once he enters the mother river, he will be like the relatives of senior Jianchi, and will sink forever in the depths of the mother river.

After hearing the conversation between Mora and Jian Chi last time, he realized that there is still the existence of the mother river corpse hunter in the world. Although Mora helps, it will definitely allow the senior sister to re-enter reincarnation and live another life. .

Therefore, he joined this plan without hesitation, and came to the world of death and silence to steal the sword of silence.

"Hahaha, the two of us are really the same people. We obviously hate Ji Moruo, a dog thief, but we have to be controlled by him. It's really..."

"Don't talk, don't talk, come, drink and drink!"

Jian Chi cut off the distracting thoughts in his heart, and as the glasses of spirits entered his stomach, the killing intent in his eyes became more and more fierce.

No matter what the final result is, he has to make a break with Mora this time. His hope lies in the sword of annihilation. No one knows this sword better than him. This villain.

Bai Jiange's spirits are in his stomach, and the alcohol vapor is instantly evaporated. This is his habit. Those who use swords must always stay awake.

In the depths of his soul, the swordfish flying around the true spirit became more and more active as his swordsmanship improved.
