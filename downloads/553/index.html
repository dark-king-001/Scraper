Chapter 553: The Terrifying Information on the Remnant Monument

Monument, a huge stone monument!

It is square, pale in color, and there are many scars on it, and even a corner is missing from the upper right corner.

Incomparably ancient, incomparably vast, and waves of strange fluctuations overflowed from the monument, forcibly pulling the sight of all true spirits.

"War text!?"

Bai Donglin was shocked. He didn't expect that at the end of this reincarnation, on a strange stone tablet, he saw the words of the Zhan clan.

The towering pale monument, among the many scars, is engraved with a few vague words. Above the seemingly ordinary fonts, there are incomprehensible and indescribable terrifying fluctuations. in every true spirit.

Bai Donglin was silent, imprinting this picture into his memory, and interpreting the meaning of the text with difficulty.

These war texts are very different from the text knowledge he acquired from the inheritance of the battle monument, which are more complicated and esoteric and difficult to understand.

The quantum cloud of thinking shines fiercely, with incomplete strokes, which have been combined and deduced countless times...

"...eternal ban..., block..."

"...Heng...leave,...don't, respond..."

"...Black Disaster,...Destroyed..."

Boom!

As the last surviving font was understood, it seemed that a certain established mechanism had been triggered, and plausible pictures flashed by, reflecting in Bai Donglin's mind.

In an unknown place of nothingness, Pan Heng has two vortexes, one white and one black. The place where black and white are intertwined is dead gray.

The inexplicable picture made Bai Donglin feel a little bit of doubt in his heart, his eyes suddenly pulled in infinitely, and he fell into that gray and dead place.

"This is……"

Bai Donglin's will is sluggish, the light spots, the endless black and white light spots are colliding and disappearing, and every light spot melts, there is a terrifying roar.

"All heavens and all worlds! Every light spot is a side of all heavens and all worlds! Uncountable, endless..."

"Every moment, countless heavens and worlds are being wiped out... Such a terrifying battlefield, what is this place?!"

"Black disaster? These dark heavens and myriad worlds are black disasters?"

Whoa!

The strange sound of water sounded again, and the stagnant true spirit continued to be pushed, passing over the pale stone tablet, and pouring into a large silver-white hole one by one. This is the exit of reincarnation, and the next step is reincarnation.

The sound of running water woke Bai Donglin from the shock, seized the last time, and tried his best to look back.

Not far from the pale stone monument, Bai Donglin saw a section of darkness, as if it was left by the truncated monument body?

There used to be a stone tablet in this place?

He has been reincarnated, and now he is in the mother's body, which is Lan Ling's life.

The information recorded later is no longer valuable, Bai Donglin moved his mind and immediately broke away from the strange perspective.

Perceiving the long years in the past, the outside world has only passed a moment.

Bai Donglin's eyes were lowered, and his thoughts were agitated as he sensed the light spots in the depths of his mind that had turned into nothingness.

He seems to have seen a lot of secrets this time, and he seems to have seen nothing. At least for Muhe, he has not found any traces of the other party, but just experienced reincarnation.
