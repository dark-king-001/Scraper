Text Chapter 556 Suffering is good medicine

In the vast wilderness, in the middle of a green and secluded primitive jungle, a bare rocky land suddenly appeared.

The cliffs, the sharp and strange rocks, and the strong demonic energy lingered, and the beasts roared continuously in the boundless jungle, but they dared not approach this rocky land, and had great fear of it.

The girl frowned, with a look of worry on her little face, looking at the strange mountain peaks in the distance lingering with strong demonic aura, her eyes flashed with a hint of sharpness.

hum!

The void trembled, and the endless brilliance gathered in the girl's eyes, and the extremely fierce heat burned the void to such an extent that it boiled violently.

"Snake demon! Quickly let go of my eldest brother, otherwise, your snake scorpion lair will surely be levelled today!"

The girl's voice was cold and stern, the ground cracked as she stepped, and her petite and exquisite body rose wildly. In an instant, she went deep into the clouds, reached out with one hand, and grabbed towards the top of the fallen mountain.

A charming woman with a snake tail, swaying an enchanting figure, slowly drew out from the cave on the top of the mountain, holding a human-shaped thing in her hand.

The whole world, everything in the world, seems to have been pressed the pause button.

Xiao Zi, who was hanging on the vine, shook with some doubts. For some reason, she felt a faint impulse in her heart, as if something was quietly sprouting in the depths of her body. She felt it carefully, but it seemed like it was just an illusion.

crunch —

"Xiao Zi, let's eat!"

Pfft! As the car was familiar with the road, the man slashed his wrist with a knife, and the scarlet blood gurgled out and dripped onto the roots of the vines.

"Big brother..."

Seeing the young man's face gradually turning pale, Xiao Zi stopped wandering, and swore in her heart that she must grow up quickly and protect her eldest brother for the rest of her life.
