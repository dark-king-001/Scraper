"So, Lianxin must become a wallbreaker, responsible for everything is empty talk..."

"They don't seem to pay much attention to the size of the territory. Although they are a little excited about the resources of the only real world, they are not to the point of losing their minds. This is completely unexpected and is the root cause of my failure in this trip."

Nether Torch Dragon frowned and poured another glass of wine into his mouth, feeling a little bored. He felt that their thinking was not on the same frequency as the alien race, boundless territory and abundant resources!
