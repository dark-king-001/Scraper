You must know that, as the most taboo cultivation secret place of the Ji Dao Sect, it has been inherited for a very long time, dating back to the beginning of the establishment of the sect in the ancient times.

Bai Donglin pondered for a while, recalling what he had experienced when he used the Hall of Prayer to strengthen his will, and his eyes could not help flashing a sudden color.

It turned out that the countless self perceived by the will at that time turned out to be real, and came from his will on other world lines. The strange power of the Hall of Prayer for the Dao was not because it was located on the node of any world line, but is from itself.

"Is the Hall of Prayer a part of the Bronze Immortal Hall?"

The wandering emperor was stunned for a moment, and then he realized how stupid his question was. He was not a person of extreme Taoism, so how could Bai Donglin appear here.

A sure look flashed in the fierce eyes. Among the physical cultivation, the ratio of males and females was seven to three. Men were born better at physical cultivation than women. They could go farther, and most of them climbed to the top.

Since the ancient times, the holy sect of the extreme way, there is only one woman who keeps the bell in all dynasties, and that is Di Su in the ancient times, and Xilifeng can take out the small golden bell, which has explained everything.

Bai Donglin glanced at Fierce speechlessly. He naturally knew that the ancestors would agree, and he never thought to really stop Xilifeng. He hasn't got the "compensation" yet, so he said this, wouldn't he cut himself three times? Knife.

"This thing is called Dao Wuzhu, which is an extremely rare wonder of heaven and earth. I see the rules of Daoist friends looming. This is a precursor to condensing the origin of the rules. Dao Wuzhu can help Daoist friends to enter the ten realms as soon as possible."

Looking at the Dao Enlightenment Pearl in his hand, a hint of warmth flashed in Xilifeng's eyes. This was the first gift that Master gave her after accepting her as his apprentice. Now that she has completed her transformation, this treasure has no effect on her. Instead of letting the pearl dust, it is better to make the best use of it to enhance the strength of Bai Donglin, the wallbreaker. For the battle of the wall, it is a help that cannot be ignored.

"Hehe, how embarrassing is this? Everyone is a wallbreaker. You are so polite, it's too outlandish!"

The mouth is nice, but Bai Donglin's movements are not slow at all, "Haha" has just been said, and the palm has already taken over the Dao Enlightenment Orb.

Although it is said to compensate for the loss of the face of the holy sect of the extreme way, but as the most outstanding disciple of the extreme way, he represents the face of the sect, and it is right to give him things.

The Wandering Emperor pursed his lips slightly, and felt a little awkward in his heart. What Jinghong gave him was roast chicken legs, but what he gave to this kid was a personal treasure, and the gap between people was too big.

"Wait a moment, the two of you, I'll bring it to the Hall of Prayer."

With the benefit, Bai Donglin no longer entangled, and with a flick of his figure, he instantly appeared in the restricted area.

"In the name of the master, solve!"

Bai Donglin raised his left hand, and the Ji Dao bracelet slowly emerged, shooting out a dazzling golden light, and the Qi Dao Hall, which was closely integrated with the guardian sect's eyes, automatically detached, shrunk to the size of a fist, and fell into his hand.

To be able to take away the Hall of Prayer so easily, only the high-level jidao who has been promoted to dominate has this authority.
