Zero and Lin were speechless.

After Lin learned the truth, her thoughts were complicated and inexplicable, her expression was erratic, she didn't know what she was thinking about, sometimes she smiled, and sometimes showed a trace of sadness.

As for Zero, he focused on the world, but he didn't know what he was waiting for.

Lin shook her head and broke free from her personal emotions. The current matter is the most important thing. She organized her words and said slowly:

"Father, do we have to do this? Although I don't know what is hidden behind the wall, so you have to come this far, but those dormant ancestors have all made outstanding achievements for the human race in their respective eras. contribution.”

"Is this too cruel?"

Lin's tone even carried a little pleading. Under the influence of the human race's history of education, every human race has great respect for the ruler of the ten realms. Deep down, she is a god-like existence. At this time, she wants her to banish the gods. , this is really difficult to accept.

"cruel?"

Zero shook his head slightly, a flash of memory flashed in his eyes, and his expression was a bit complicated and unspeakable. After a moment of silence, he continued to say:

"Once, being a father held the same thing as you, but that lord persuaded me."

"Blindly avoiding and hiding, such a passive, who can guarantee that it can last forever?"

"What is this world afraid of?"

"Who is he hiding from?"

"Will we be exposed in the next moment!?"

As he spoke, his expression became heavier and heavier, the aura in his body was tumbling, and he faintly lost control. Obviously, his emotions fluctuated greatly.

Seeing Lin fell into contemplation, she paused in a zero tone, her voice softened a lot, and said inexplicably:

"This world binds all my human beings together. We are like grasshoppers on the line, walking on the steel rope tremblingly. If we are not careful, we will fall into the abyss, and it will never be recovered!"

"So, blindly hiding is not advisable, we must take the initiative, break the wall, and smash this cage!"

"Even if it is a dead end, at least I have worked hard for it, instead of being in a hurry. Until the world takes us to bury with us, we still don't know who the enemy is and where it came from."

After listening to Zero's words, Lin showed a thoughtful look. At this moment, she understood the unyielding and decisive will of the broken wall party, and at the same time, she also understood the irreconcilable hiddenness of the broken wall.

"But... is there no other way than to exile them? If you can convince the masters to join the broken wall, isn't this a huge force?"

"Convince? Hehe, daughter, you are too naive."

Zero shook his head and smiled bitterly. The young man was still too young, and his thoughts were always so shallow.

"Those old and stubborn, with extremely pedantic thinking, who are both masters, and their will is tenacity and cannot be easily changed. How can other people's words have any effect?"

"Lin, you shouldn't have too much burden in your heart. Banishing them is not just cruel, but a kind deed, so that they can avoid the calamity of death."

"The side that breaks the wall with the presence of adults is so powerful that you can't imagine it. The hidden side is undoubtedly hitting a stone with an egg, and exile is a form of protection. After all the dust settles, they will naturally have a chance to return..."

"Um!?"

Ling Wei closed his eyes, suddenly opened, and suddenly got up and looked at the sky, carefully perceiving for a moment, and then slowly said:

"The world is chaotic, the confusion is unclear, Jiutian Xuannv, has it begun?"

"Nine-headed mysterious girl? Who is she?"

Lin also imitated her father's appearance and looked up, but her cultivation was still shallow, so she couldn't perceive the huge changes between heaven and earth.

"Jiutian Xuannv is the disciple of your uncle. She is a strong person in the ancient times. I don't know how many lives she has reincarnated. Jiutian Xuannv is just her name in the Huntian era."

"Uncle's disciple..."

Lin nodded slightly, a thoughtful look flashing in her eyes.

"Lin, the time has come, let's get started."

Zero's expression became solemn. The court of God was the base camp of the hidden party. What they did was naturally intolerant and naturally needed to be covered.

Jiutian Xuannv urged the bronze immortal hall, summed up the world line, heaven and earth fell into confusion, all kinds of connections and perceptions would be interrupted, it was the best opportunity for him to do it.

"Then... the daughter will also be banished forever along with the 33rd Heaven. Father, won't that be very lonely?"

Zero's fingers trembled slightly, and she slowly turned her face to the side, unable to bear the gaze that Lin looked up at him.

Loneliness, more than loneliness!

Although Lin is the reincarnation of the spirit of the 33rd Heaven, she is also his own flesh and blood, blood relatives!

No matter how high his cultivation base is, and no matter how long he has passed, he did not expect that there is still a soft place deep in his heart.

"Father, I understand!"

Lin looked at her father's lonely back, and suddenly grinned brightly, her big eyes narrowed into crescents.

"However, Father, can you agree to a request for my daughter? Can you give me the 'Duanwangya', I want to keep it by my side, because the uncle once said, tell me to wait for him there, he will come to see him mine……"

"Can!"

Zero Heavy nodded, stretched out his hand, and crossed the transcendent teleportation hub. Under the shocked gazes of countless monks, he descended to the headquarters of the inspector of the Kuihe Transcendent Galaxy Cluster, and deep behind the main hall of the inspector stood an extremely tall building. The tall mountain peaks were pulled up.

"Don't panic! It's the breath of the Supreme Director!"

"Here!"

Covering the sky tightly grasped the towering mountain peak, and slammed back into the depths of the secret realm. The huge sacred mountain has been compressed into a mini mountain, and in the vigorous hands, it slowly rotates.

"Thank you, Father!"

With little stars shining in her eyes, Lin reached out her hand to gently take over the mountain, and hugged her tightly, full of love.

"Father, I'm ready, let's start!"

Zero took a deep breath and didn't dare to delay any longer. He was afraid of hesitating, so he couldn't bear to do it because he was soft-hearted. Resolute flashed in his eyes, his hands fluttered, countless spells were pinched out, and the rules of Taoism appeared and condensed. into the void above.

Boom!

The void trembled, and a huge shadow bound by countless chains swayed away the fog and slowly revealed its figure.

ding ding ding-

This turned out to be an incomparably huge octagonal pagoda with thirty-three floors. Above each sharp corner, there was a bell hanging, swaying slightly, with a very clear and pleasant sound.

Each floor carries a mysterious space, boundless and unpredictable, exuding invisible coercion all the time, and the void trembles.

"My dear girl, I'm sorry..."

Looking at Lin's firm eyes, a trace of unbearable flashed in Zero's eyes, and then pressed down with a palm.

Lin's body instantly turned into a soup of particles, and a golden sound was suspended in it. The particles revolved and condensed, wrapping Lin's soul, turning into a dazzling ball of light, a miniature mountain, dripping around the ball of light. turning.

At the moment when the light ball appeared, the octagonal pagoda seemed to sense something, trembled violently, and then spurted out a thick beam of light, which enveloped the light ball and swallowed it in an instant.

As the ball of light entered, the Octagonal Pagoda fell into a momentary stagnation, and the whole body flashed with dazzling light, and then it trembled violently, like a behemoth, and woke up.

Click! Click!

The void was annihilated, and the chains that bound the pagoda were continuously and violently breaking through the secret space and spilling out in all directions.

"stop!!"

"Zero! What are you doing!?"

boom! A big hand smashed the space, one black and one white, and two figures shot in, raging with anger, changing the color of the world, this is the guardian of the divine court.

The black and white old men looked at each other, and were shocked by the violent aura emanating from the Thirty-Three Layers of Heaven. They didn't have time to think about it.

chant—

A sword light like a star river pierced through the void and instantly cut off the terrifying big hand.

"Black and white Su Lao, the matter has come to this point, don't do anything unnecessary."

"you……"

Old Black and White Su's pupils shrank, and looked down in disbelief. Zero, who raised his sword fingers, became stronger and stronger in that body, so the two had to be cautious.

"Who are you!?"

"I'm not the top director of the audit department, and the guardian of the thirty-third heaven, zero!"

Zero smiled slightly, the long years of repression made him a little unfamiliar with his own power.

"You hide your strength!? Hidden in my divine court, what exactly do you want!?"

The corners of the old man's brows twitched. The inspector and the Thirty-three Heavens, both of the most important institutions of the Divine Court, were actually sneaked in. This is an unimaginable major flaw.

"Hehe, don't be so hostile, the second old man, I'm here to help you escape..."

"absurd!"

"kill--"

Black and white Su Lao's eyes flickered with cold light. In any case, Zero who hides his strength is no longer trustworthy, and to suppress the Thirty-Three Heavens, he must first solve the stumbling block of Zero.

Boom!

The terrifying confrontation destroyed the secret realm in an instant, and the unintentional aftermath caused the octagonal pagoda to break free from its shackles faster.

click-

"What's the point of thinking about this now? The most urgent task is to notify the Lord of the Divine Court as soon as possible, and report to the Supreme Council, the loss of the Thirty-third Heaven.
