Bai Donglin heard the words, his expression changed, and the battle for the divine stone was nothing more than that, after all, this was something Xilifeng promoted. But now it seems that even the appearance of the Immortal Execution Sword Formation was expected by this woman, which is a bit unbelievable.

"What is the relationship between the will of the Dao and the Mother River? Why are they hostile?"

"Fellow Daoist Bai, you must also understand that this world is morbid, and the thing called true spirit should not exist."

"A long time ago, when the world had not changed drastically, the will of the Dao was the maker and controller of all the rules."

"The will of the Dao of Heaven controls the great world, and the will of the Dao is above it, controlling the entire boundless world of the heavens and the world."

Speaking of this, the Wandering Emperor paused and couldn't help but glanced at Xilifeng, all the information was given to him by the other party, and then continued:

"This situation continued until the time of the change, an unknown war, the will of the Dao was wiped out, and the world was also devastated."

"After that, Mother River appeared, and He took the opportunity to take over the control of heaven and earth. Fate, time and space, cause and effect, reincarnation, etc., all fell under His control, and modified the rules to create the so-called 'true spirit' to To achieve the goal of completely controlling all beings and imprisoning all beings!"

"One is a thief, and the other is a master who has been stolen from heaven and earth. You said, can the two of them not be hostile to each other?"
