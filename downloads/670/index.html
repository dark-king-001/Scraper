The highest meeting of the human race lasted for several years.

The content discussed is very broad. The biggest controversy is still about how to distribute the leftover war materials, fronts, war weapons, and troops in the major battlefields.

Under the circumstance that the foreign aggression has been eliminated, the two sides have completely torn their faces, and no longer care about it, they are red-faced in the fight for resource forces. After all, these things can greatly enhance their own strength in the future, even if they can't get them, they can't be cheap. each other.

"Okay! This deity doesn't want to quarrel with Er et al! Immediately, all the rights to use the Battle Spirit Network will be withdrawn!"

Emperor Zun's expression was slightly cold, and he couldn't bear to continue the endless wrangling. After saying that, with a thought, with the supreme authority of the Chaos Pearl controller, he instantly stripped away the authority of many existences on the side of the wall.

It mainly includes the master and the billions of gods and demons to which his forces belong. As for the lower-level monks, there is no need. The authority they can use is extremely meager. The most important thing is that there are too many people involved. If they take away all of them, the human race will be in chaos.

Buzz! !

The rulers of the broken wall all moved their eyes slightly, looking at their slowly dissipating imprint of the battle spirit, and at the same time, the "vision of master authority" appeared in the back of their heads, flickering and turning into nothingness.

"As compensation, Tianwaitian will be yours."

For Emperor Zun, this is not a compromise. Although the Battle Soul Network was created by him, the Wall Breaker has also made a lot of contributions, especially the will aggregates in the Chaos Pearl, but all races dominate. Faith after death.

With his arrogance, he naturally didn't want to take advantage of the broken wall.

Moreover, the reason why Tianwaitian has become the core of supervision and control of the entire human race is that the Battle Spirit Network has played a decisive role. Without the Battle Spirit Network, it is just a rather strange space.

"Hehe, since that's the case, I would like to thank the Emperor."

Feng Jun raised the corners of his mouth and pulled out a smirk, but it wasn't a big surprise. This was all to be expected. Without the Battle Spirit Network, wouldn't they still have Tianluo space? No problem!

"Humph!"

The emperor stood up suddenly, flicked his sleeves, and stepped out of the temple, and the rest of the hidden masters also got up quickly and followed closely behind.

As the crowd left, the dazzling throne, which symbolized the supreme status and authority of the human race, instantly collapsed and turned into a splendid spot of light.

The emperor's figure was slightly stagnant, standing at the door of the temple, his expression became more and more cold, and his tone was indifferent: "The next time we meet, we will face each other."

"Wait, do it yourself!!"

boom--

A dazzling beam of light fell, shrouding Emperor Zun and others, the brilliance dissipated, and no one was left.

"Hey, the battle of ideas, how can you give in?"

"Emperor, your mood, the old man understands..."

A trace of complexity flashed in the eyes of the masters, and then his will turned into a knife, cut off distractions, and his eyes were firm and bright.

"Next, what should we do?"

Hearing Feng Jun's question, everyone looked at the highest seat of the gods in unison, but at this time, in addition to Bing Miao, who had a lazy expression and squinted and dozed, there was also the figure of Bai Donglin.

"Uh, Senior Bing Miao, what about Master Bai?"

"Meow-"

Bing Miao yawned, stretched out her claws and bowed, and after a while, she said in a clear voice: "A year after the meeting, when you were arguing, Lord Bai said, 'It's so boring' and disappeared. ."

"Meow~ don't you know?"

Everyone looked at each other in dismay, and there was a hint of surprise in their eyes. What realm has this Lord Bai reached? It turned out to be silent, blinding the perception of all of them.

"As for the five-color divine stone, we still have to ask Mr. Bai. According to what the emperor said before, if it is not bad, Mr. Bai should know where the three remaining divine stones are."

"Even, in the hands of Lord Bai, it is not impossible."

When everyone heard this, they nodded slightly. In their hearts, Bai Donglin was already omnipotent, which was much stronger than the description of Bai Donglin in the memory information obtained from "His and My Will".

The sword master stopped stroking his sword and said slowly, "Perhaps, you can try it at the White God Realm Realm Master Mansion."

Xuetu took over the conversation. In fact, they all knew in their hearts that if Bai Donglin was willing to see them, these two places were the most likely places, but if they didn't want to see them, it would be futile to search the ends of the earth.

"Let's go."

This is to discuss countermeasures, not to fight, just go to a few people who can talk.

At the foot of Yujing Mountain, a little blue-flowered demon was taking a nap against the towering jade tablet.

The squinted eyes were in a trance for a while, and suddenly realized that a few figures with strange robes had appeared in front of him, and instantly woke up.

"Who are you!?" The tone of voice was vigilant. This was the first time she looked at the door. Although there should be no intruders in the Supreme Dao Sect, it was still necessary to pretend.

"Haha, don't panic, old man Fengjun, came to visit Yujing Mountain Master, I wonder if your master is here?"

Although it was strange why the handsome young man in front of him called himself "Old Man", this gentle voice instantly made the little demon relax his vigilance.

"My master is here! Xiao Lan saw the master walking in front of the main hall this morning!"

When the masters heard the words, their eyes lit up, and they had already fluttered in the World Lord's Mansion, and this time it should be fine.

At this moment, the void next to the jade tablet suddenly rippled, and a vortex channel slowly emerged.

"Friends, please come in."

It was Bai Donglin's voice, everyone looked solemn, cupped their hands, and stepped into the passage.

"Who are these people? It's strange, Yujingshan, but it's rare for people to visit..."

Xiao Lan muttered, scratched her not-so-smart little head, and continued to doze off against the jade tablet.

In the vast white jade hall, everyone saw Bai Donglin, who was casually lying cross-legged on the ground, and his expression was in a trance, as if he had seen the center of heaven and earth, his eyes blinked, but he looked like an ordinary mortal.

"Everyone, you're welcome, please take a seat."

Everyone looked around, the white jade hall was empty, and the ground was smooth and clean.

"I know your intentions. Those three divine stones are indeed in my hands."

"grown ups……"

Ah Tai was in a state of disgust and excitement, his body was pitch black and thunder kept jumping, and the fighting spirit in his eyes was unbearable.

"The time has not come."

Bai Donglin's fluttering voice suddenly woke everyone up, and the turbulent emotions were calmed down.

"My lord, why? With your strength and the many rulers of our broken wall, what time do we need to wait for?"

There were doubts in Feng Jun's eyes. In their opinion, the ten realms of their own side had come to an end, and wasting time would not be able to improve their strength any further, so it would be better to strike while the iron is hot.

"There are two reasons. First, I haven't figured out what the broken arm is secretly plotting. Second, I'm at a critical point in the exploration of the bottom of the mother river, and it will take some time."

Bai Donglin's eyes closed slightly. In fact, there was one last point he didn't say, that is, his own deployment and the improvement of his strength have not yet reached the most perfect state.

"Hundreds of billions of years of waiting have passed, and we are not short of these tens of thousands of years, aren't we?"

Hearing Bai Donglin's words, everyone's expressions suddenly loosened. It turned out that it only took tens of thousands of years. They thought that they would have to wait hundreds of millions of years, and tens of thousands of years, that is, taking a nap, it really doesn't matter.

"Hey, Master Bai, it's not bad to drag on for hundreds of millions of years. Anyway, we have adults to extend our lifespan, and Emperor Zun and the others may die of old age!"

The Lord of All Saints smirked in a low voice, causing Bai Donglin to roll his eyes secretly. How can things be so simple?

"The idea is good, but the reality is not so beautiful. Emperor Zun and the others have the gift and blessing of the mother river. Shouyuan is no longer a shackle. It doesn't make any sense to live longer than anyone else."

"Uh, okay."

The Lord of All Saints smiled embarrassingly, glanced at the few people sitting beside him, and scolded in his heart, it seems that he has really broken his mind by training his body, these guys must be laughing wildly in their hearts, right?

"In short, within tens of thousands of years, everything will be understood."

Bai Donglin secretly estimated the time, tens of thousands of years, and some things can also be accelerated by time, which is almost enough for him to prepare everything.

There is only one chance, so if you are fully prepared, you can't go wrong.

"Black Cang and Chi Ming, let's keep them first. I'm not sure what the distance of the divine stone is, so I can't store them centrally for the time being."
