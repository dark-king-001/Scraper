Bai Donglin's eyes flickered slightly, and he instantly thought of the battle monument. At this time, the seventh piece of the battle monument had not been merged, and after so long in the future, the eighth piece had not yet been found.

"Master, everything is normal in the outside world, and it did not attract their attention."

Tianluo's body is a big ball of light, shining brightly, and it has entered a high-speed operation.

"Well, the beginning of everything is difficult, and the subsequent energy transmission will be more secretive, and it will be difficult for them to detect it."

Bai Donglin nodded slightly. What the enemy doesn't know is called a hole card. If it is exposed in advance, it will be difficult to play a one-hit kill.

To this end, he made a lot of preparations and deliberately placed the energy-transforming spirit world in a remote area far away from the only real world.

Mother River and Broken Arm are fighting to the death in the only real world at this time, and they will not pay attention to the depths of the outer dimension of nothingness.

He is not worried that the rapid consumption of dimensional power in the outer dimensional space will attract the attention of the mother river, because the dimensional power is too vast, even if he completes the plan, what he devours is only a drop in the bucket.

If an infinite chaotic world is compared to a closed crystal ball, the total amount of energy of the heavens and worlds opened up within it, whether it exists or is destroyed into nothingness, remains unchanged.

Simply put, the meat is all rotten in the pot.
