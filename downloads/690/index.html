The Mother River controls time and space, fate, cause and effect, and reincarnation.

The power of the Dao Reincarnation Origin is the power that Bai Donglin is most afraid of. In order to break the situation, he has put in a lot of efforts, countless explorations, countless deductions, gathering all the information, and finally found the only way.

Emperor Bai Xuan's eyes moved slightly, flickering for a while, and he came to the pale stone tablet. He reached out his hand and stroked it gently, feeling the bulge and depression of the fuzzy words, his expression was extremely complicated.

"Finally... I'm back here again, my birthplace!"

Emperor Bai Xuan slowly turned his head and looked not far in front of the pale stone monument. On the flat ground, there was an irregular pitch-black stone mark.

There, there was originally a stone monument, a pitch-black stone monument, which had been staring at him for endless years, until...

Emperor Bai Xuan looked dazed for a while, and his thoughts returned to the endless years ago.

One day, a heroic man in a black robe suddenly appeared here. The man's eyes were extremely firm and vicissitudes, and he said to him:

​

"Interesting, a stone tablet actually gave birth to consciousness."

"You want to get out of here, right? I sense your desire for freedom, um, how about making a deal?"

"A new era of the future..."

​

"If you think he can successfully break the cage, you can help him..."

​

He wanted to respond, but he only had a hazy consciousness, unable to speak.

After the yingwu man finished speaking, he stretched out his hand to hold the pitch-black stone tablet, and with a violent force, he broke it off, and then the figure flickered and disappeared.

"Master Bai, it's time to start!"

"okay!"

With a voice that couldn't wait, Moruo pulled Emperor Bai Xuan back from his memory. His expression became solemn. He squeezed the ball of faith light with a slight force in his palm.

"Ksitigarbha King Bodhisattva! Mahasattva! Reincarnation Hell, No Mysterious Realm!"

The black lotus space isolates all fluctuations, just in case, attracting the attention of the mother river.
