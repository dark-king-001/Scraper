An extremely thick, engraved Dao pattern catalogue, surrounded by the sun, moon and stars, was slowly pushed open.

Immediately, the endless treasure light soared into the sky, illuminating the dark night sky into a brilliant white.

"My lord, this is the treasury of the Prison Bird Empire. The treasures of resources that have been accumulated over the years are all stored here."

Prisoner Long's eyes flashed with pain, looking at Bai Donglin's back, he was about to cry in his heart, but he still forced a smile.

"not bad."

Bai Donglin nodded, stepped into the treasure house, looked up, countless treasures, shining with divine light, golden elixir flying around, top-quality spirit stone fairy crystals, like stars, suspended in the void , the long river of spiritual fluid that twists and turns, can't see the end at a glance...

"I've just arrived here, and I have no long possessions. I need to get some treasures for use. Xiaolong, do you have any opinion?"

"No, no opinion, you can take whatever you want, my lord!"

The corners of Prison Dragon's mouth twitched, revealing a smile that was uglier than crying, and his fingers couldn't help shaking slightly.

"Hehe, Xiaolong, you are okay, open enough."

While speaking, Bai Donglin slowly spread out his palm, a chaotic vortex emerged, and with a sharp turn, the entire treasure house space was pulled and pulled, and began to collapse.

The prisoner dragon only felt a white light flashing in front of his eyes, and the incomparably wide treasure house space was wrapped in all the treasure resources, turned into a ball of light, and was sucked into the palm vortex.

OK, so cruel!

The treasury space of the Prison Bird Empire spanned millions of light-years from left to right, and the countless resources accumulated in it were packed and taken away.

"Sir, this..."

"What? You said you let me take it casually, but you regretted it so quickly?"

Bai Donglin's expression turned cold, and he saw Prisoner's heart tremble, and quickly explained.

"Sir, don't get me wrong, it's just... these treasures are not all from the Prison Bird Empire. Every 10,000 years, we will pay tribute to the upper peak, which is the Tiangan God Territory. You took all the treasury away. Make it difficult for us!"

"Okay, I get it now."

Bai Donglin nodded, turned his head and walked out, what was in his stomach, and why did he spit it out?

What the **** is God's Domain, it's none of his business, just come to him.

‘As expected of a complete universe of the heavens, the accumulation of endless years is really rich, a mere empire of prisoners has scavenged such a huge amount of resources, and it has to pay tribute every 10,000 years. ’

‘These cosmic kingdoms are very interesting! ’

Bai Donglin walked aside, thinking to himself, he was not worried that what he did would attract the attention of the source of information.

The supreme existence who has stepped into the Twelve Realms is no longer interested in these things outside the body. After all, it is possible to create something out of nothing with will, and how can it be lacking in these things?

Not to mention the twelve realms, it is the eleven realms, and they do not rely too much on these external objects. For them, the improvement of realm perception is the most important.

It's just that he is special, because to nurture the spiritual world, the thirst for resources is endless, even if he breaks through to the Thirteenth Realm.

Bai Donglin pressed his thoughts down, glanced at the prisoner dragon who was not guarding his house, and said, "Xiaolong, besides you, isn't there another ten-level prisoner in your prisoner bird empire? Why doesn't he come to see me?"

The Eleventh Realm can peep the timeline and easily understand this information, which is considered to be "omniscient" to a certain extent, but it is not an emergency at the moment.

"Return to your lord!"

"My eldest brother, he is not currently in the empire. He represented the empire to the God's Domain, and went to the ancient Hongxian Dynasty with the adults of Tiangan to attend an important meeting."

The prisoner dragon pressed his mind like a mess, and did not dare to hide anything, and explained it one by one.

"Oh?"

Bai Donglin's expression changed, and he suddenly thought of something. That was some information that he glimpsed when he was looking at the long river of time and space when he broke through.

"Do you mean the important meeting that you are referring to, matters related to the 'Zhentian Daoyan'?"

"That's right! It's the Daoyan of the Heavens, which happened once in a small Yanji. This is the most important event between heaven and earth at present! Everything else has to make way for it!"

Yanji is a unified expression unit for the time scale of all the heavens.

One thousand moments is one breath, nine hundred breaths is one quarter of an hour, eight quarters of an hour is one hour, twelve hours is one day, three hundred and sixty-five days is one year, one trillion years is one small period, ten thousand small years. Ji is a great Yan Ji...

This is the unified measurement of time for all the heavens. It is said that there is a larger unit of time above the "Dayan Period", but this kind of thing is no longer something that ordinary existence needs to know. Only the strong who jumped out of time need to use those units and use this to measure the endless years they have spent.

Time, for the existence above the eleven realms, is already a dispensable thing.

"Zhentian Daoyan..."

A thoughtful look flashed in Bai Donglin's eyes. He only had a glimpse of this thing, but he felt that those supreme beings, the Time and Space Administration, the source of information, etc., all attached great importance to it.

Obviously, there must be something remarkable in this, which attracted the attention of the twelve realms.

"Xiaolong, tell me everything you know about the Daoyan of the Heavens."

"Yes!"

The prisoner dragon straightened his expression. Although he wondered why this lord asked about this "well-known" matter, he didn't dare to think too much. After organizing the language, he continued:

"As we all know, the heavens are not the only heaven and earth, they are endless, and each has its own characteristics, but they are all boundless, bred endless creatures, and are entrenched in many supreme beings."

"The endless heavens are not static, they converge into an indescribable vortex, the vortex of the heavens as a whole rotates clockwise, doing the eternal rotation, and the vortex of the heavens to which the black disaster belongs, It's doing the opposite counterclockwise movement..."

"In this terrifying vortex, the endless heavens are running irregularly. Although the general direction is drawn by the vortex, there are no rules within a small range."

When Bai Donglin heard the words, he nodded secretly in his heart, not to mention the information he observed in the long river of time and space, the details about the "vortex of the heavens", he had seen the image of the brand in the eight-color battle monument, and had seen it for a long time. arrived.

One white, one black, the indescribable vast vortex, entangled and strangled each other in endless years.

"The heavens in the side where we are, every other Xiaoyanji, will collide with the adjacent heavens, and the will of the respective Dao will converge at that time, resulting in indescribable changes..."

"This is the Daoyan of the heavens!"

Prisoner Long's eyes narrowed slightly, his emotions could not help being agitated, and his tone was a little hot, and he continued: "It is said that these heavens are involved in the mysteries of the Thirteen Realms, so they, the supreme source of information, will deal with this matter. so important."

"If in this grand event..."

As he talked, Prison Dragon's eyes gradually became blurred, and he fell into some kind of fantasy.

Bai Donglin raised his brows, his face was speechless, this guy really has a higher heart than the sky, even the eleventh realm is still 108,000 miles away, so he began to imagine the realm of detachment, this is really, better than him dream.

"Hehe, hehe..."

"My lord, eh? My lord!?"

The prisoner dragon came back to his senses, wiped the non-existent Harazi at the corner of his mouth, and looked around, there was no figure of Bai Donglin.

...

"Zhutian Daoyan, this is a rare opportunity. If you miss it, you have to wait another trillion years. This is really too long!"

Bai Donglin returned to the Prison Bird Temple, crossed his knees in the color of the throne, frowning in thought.

He is not like a prisoner, thinking of the mystery of detachment. He knows his own weight, not to mention that he does not know what the so-called mystery is. Even if he does, how can he communicate with those who have already set foot on the other side of eternity? the existence of contention?

His gaze was to see what was behind this matter. The heavens collided and the wills of the avenues converged. Can we take this opportunity to cross the boundaries of the heavens?

His observer's will state can break through the absolute blockade of "war". It is reasonable to say that it is not impossible to lurk in the will of the avenue and cross the border.

There is no special channel, and with his current strength, he cannot break the walls of the heavens and leap into other heavens. This kind of ability is estimated to be only available to those above the twelve realms.

"Sprinkle the clone into other heavens and leave the resurrection anchor, and I can travel back and forth at will!"

Bai Donglin's eyes flickered slightly, thinking frantically to deduce the probability of success. If this happened, it would be of great significance.

More worlds represent more resources, as well as endless knowledge and information, and even traces of those who transcend can be found.

This is a great help for him to break through the realm and nurture infinite spiritual orifices.

"This matter needs to be discussed in the long run. There is only one chance, and it must be foolproof..."

After making a decision in his heart, Bai Donglin's eyes suddenly became very firm, this is his temperament. I am afraid that there is no goal. Once there is a goal, even if we use all means to break through all obstacles and difficulties, we must complete it.

Just like his original layout broke the wall.

"There are still more than 10 million years before the opening of the Daoyan of the Heavens, which is enough for me to complete the layout."

Bai Donglin lightly tapped the armrest of the throne with his fingers, his eyes were deep, dark and cold, and a nebula whirlpool could be seen in the depths of his eyes.

"Li You."

Whispering in a clear voice, it was instantly transmitted thousands of miles away, Li You's figure stagnated, and he immediately controlled the prisoner Ansheng next to him, and took him to break through the void.

"The disciple has seen the master!"

Li You knelt down on the wide hall, out of the corner of his eyes, watching the figure on the throne.

"Haha, boy, since I've already said what I said, there is no reason to take it back."

Bai Donglin shook his head and laughed, and with his insightful eyes, he saw the unease in Li You's heart.

"From now on, you will be a disciple of this seat. Remember, there is a senior sister and a senior brother above you."

Li You's body was shocked~www.mtlnovel.com~ clenched her fists fiercely, and tears rolled down from the corners of her eyes.

Bang bang bang!

With nine bangs, they slammed on the hard ground, and the cracks in the skull spread, leaving dark red bloodstains.

"Teacher Li You, meet the master!!"

This world, how vast, how cruel, small as an ant, finally, finally has a place to live.

"Dare to ask Master, what is the title of our sect?"

Bai Donglin slowly got up and looked at the galaxy, his eyes were deep and bright.

"Bai Yujing."

"Heavenly Bai Yujing!!"

The faint words are imprinted on the long river of time and space, and will last forever.
