The recent Prison Bird Empire was very lively.

A neutron star warship with a length of one million kilometers, constantly jumped out of the void, streaked across the sea of ​​stars in the sky of the imperial capital, and suspended above the imperial city.

The imperial city was shielded by a strict formation, and outside citizens could not peep into the abnormal behavior of these warships, and could only guess in private.

Some people think that this is a coup d'etat within the empire, or the collapse of the country's lord, and the princes are fighting for the throne. Others say that they saw the figure of the eldest prince who was supposed to be serving, and it was rumored that he had nose and eyes.

There are also rational people who refuted that, judging by the abnormal collapse of the space around the battleship, the quality of each battleship is astonishingly large, and its load has reached its limit. If it is to carry an army, it is absolutely impossible to reach this level, and there should be some extremely heavy cargo inside.

The neutron star warships are constantly rising and falling, and they have not stopped for three years. According to statistics, hundreds of millions of ships have descended on the imperial city.

"Phew! I tried my best to replenish the treasury to half of what it was before. Well, I can barely cope with the tribute to the Heavenly God Realm."

Prison Ansheng stood outside the treasury, watching the battleship in the void constantly spewing treasure resources into the portal, secretly relieved in his heart. The depletion of the national treasury is of great importance, and the rumor must not be leaked, so he can only take care of it himself.

After sending the last battleship away, the prisoner secretly used the magic trick to close the heavy gate to death, and then frowned with a look of thought.

"Calculate the time, it's less than three days, and the three-year period is full. The lord hasn't summoned me yet. Did he change his mind?"

Thinking of this, my heart trembled.

"No! It's better for me to take the initiative to stay outside the hall. If your lord is delayed by something, at the last moment, I can also remind you."

A matter of life and death, prisoner Ansheng could not sit still, stepped out in one step, tore through the void, and rushed towards the towering palace in the center.

In the empty hall, there is a jade pagoda and a **** seat. On the towering **** seat, there are a stone tablet, a crystal ball, and a white jade gourd.

Suddenly, a strand of hair on the stone tablet trembled slightly, and then the void distorted inexplicably, and a figure appeared out of nothing.

"Good times are always short-lived."

Bai Donglin had a smile in his eyes, and his expression was softer than ever before. It was obvious that he had spent a very relaxing time with his family during this time.

The strong will not stay for the present comfort, just keep this beauty in the bottom of my heart. Only with the power over everything can we protect this beauty forever.

"Time to do the right thing."

Bai Donglin's expression became solemn, the softness in his eyes disappeared, and he returned to secluded indifference.

"Li You."

His eyes cast down, penetrating the jade pagoda, and Daoyin went deep into his heart, awakening him from the retreat.

"The disciple has seen the master!"

Li You opened his eyes fiercely, and with a flick of his figure, he was already kneeling in the hall respectfully.

At this time, Li You was completely different from three years ago. With a strong cultivation base, he had reached the top of the Seven Realms. Such a breakthrough speed was truly terrifying.

This kind of violation of the system is indeed worthy of its name.

"Report to Master, my disciple has devoured all the ancient relics in the treasure house and the breath of time contained in them."

"The system has stored a large amount of time values, enough for the disciples to use for a long time, so..."

"You want to go out for a walk?"

Bai Donglin's eyes moved slightly, he saw through Li You's mind, thought for a moment, then nodded and said, "Yes, behind closed doors, it is difficult to achieve great things."

"Your will, there is no sign of igniting at all, it is time to experience life and death."

The system is not omnipotent. For example, the most fundamental and most important "self-consciousness" of living beings can only be comprehended and practiced by oneself, and between life and death again and again, grasp the eternal true meaning of the mysterious and mysterious will.

The way of id consciousness, external things cannot help.

"Master Ming Jian!"

"Teacher, I also want to go to the battlefield with you!"

Li You fiercely raised his head, and his eyes showed a burning meaning. A few years ago, he was most worried about serving in the battlefield, but now he wants to choose to serve in advance. It can be seen that his heart has undergone earth-shaking changes.

"Oh? You're not afraid of death?"

Bai Donglin showed a smile, staring straight at Li You, full of scrutiny.

"Hey, isn't this the presence of Master, your old man, you definitely won't watch your disciples turn into cannon fodder..."

Li You had a mean smile on his face, but his eyes were extremely firm. Obviously, he was mentally prepared. He had the belief of resurrecting his parents to support him, and he was not afraid of death.

"Slippery!"

"Well, it's up to you."

Bai Donglin shook his head slightly, then raised his hand and pointed, and a light spot of information shot out, sinking into the depths of Li You's eyebrows.

"Master, is this?"

Li You's mind was dazed for a while, and he was shocked by the massive amount of information, and his consciousness was in a trance for a moment.

"This collection, called "Intention", goes from the simple to the deep, from the complex to the simple. It explains in detail how to cultivate the self-consciousness and understand it well. It can help you ignite the fire of will as soon as possible."

"Intention" is Bai Donglin's most important collection besides "Pregnant God Casting Demons". In his heart, even the inheritance of "Ba Bu" derived from "Zhan" is slightly inferior.

Every moment, the mind is deducting and polishing "Yi", and it has reached a perfect and bizarre realm.

‘Drip drip! ’

'Unknown inheritance detected, suspected to be the twelfth-order supreme treasure book, can be submitted to the system, value: seven trillion years value! ’

‘Host, do you choose to sell? ’

Hearing the system prompt sound resounding in his mind, Li You's mind went blank, and his mouth opened subconsciously to the limit.

"No, no!! The system, warn you, don't touch my master's things!"

Li You came back to his senses, his expression froze, and he immediately refused, with a little anger in his tone.

This was also the first time he knew that the system actually had the function of reclaiming inheritance. He thought about it, how could the low-level exercises he practiced in the past get into the eyes of the system.

The twelfth-order collection is worth seven trillion years. Li You was so shocked that his scalp was numb, and his throat felt astringent.

Big deal!

It is worthy of being a master, if you don't make a move, it's already a big deal!

He finally understood what the Master meant when he said that he was too lazy to teach.

'This kid, he's still a bit conscientious. ’

Bai Donglin nodded secretly and didn't pay much attention to it. His long-standing concept was that reusable things such as inheritance of knowledge can be shared. As long as it is not an enemy, one can learn his way and accommodate more creatures' wisdom, and the inheritance will progress.

It is not advisable to be self-sufficient.

Therefore, even if the source of information reads "Yi", he will not be dissatisfied. Anyway, he has already planned the knowledge of the other party. There is no "stealing teacher" between the strong. This is called mutual study.

Ignoring Li You who was wandering in the sky and smirking, Bai Donglin looked outside the hall and said softly, "Prison Ansheng, come in."

The prisoner, who had just arrived for a moment, looked solemn, straightened his robes, bowed and paced, and walked into the hall.

He looked at his toes, did not dare to look around, knelt on the ground, and kowtowed respectfully.

"The prisoner has seen the adults in the dark! The adults are holy!"

"Well, get up and talk."

Bai Donglin's expression moved slightly, and around the prisoner's body, he saw the condensed and undissipated Baoguang Qi, and he turned his thoughts, and then he showed a funny color.

"Prisoner Ansheng, did you buy goods into the treasury again?"

"Uh uh!"

Prison Ansheng's figure was stagnant, the corners of his mouth twitched, he couldn't speak, and he cried out in his heart.

"Hehe, you really have a heart."

While speaking, Bai Donglin reached into the void with one hand and retracted it instantly. The palm of his hand was already holding a space bubble, which was vaguely visible, and there were countless treasures and resources in it.

"Big, my lord..."

Prison Ansheng's brows jumped, a look of embarrassment that he wanted to say something, but didn't dare to say it.

These urgently transferred resources have already wiped out the princes all over the empire. In a short period of time, it is impossible to collect such a large amount of resources. If they are taken away again, they will definitely not be able to complete the tribute target.

"You don't seem to understand one thing."

A sneer flashed in Bai Donglin's eyes, and with a grip of his palm, he threw the space bubble into the chaotic vortex in his body.

"The Prisoner Bird Empire no longer belongs to your prisoner family. Everything in the empire is the property of this seat."

"What the **** is God's Domain? Dare to let Lao Tzu pay tribute? If they want to make trouble, let them come directly to this seat."

Bai Donglin's abacus was so loud that the small prison bird empire could not satisfy him. With the tribute, he gave the Tiangan Gods an excuse to go crazy. At that time, he could take over it "justifiably". Then there is the Immortal Dynasty, and even spread to all the countries of the universe...

With such a supreme existence as the source of information, it must not care about these things, right? He Bai Donglin used it for a while without destroying his experimental field.

"Uh, what the lord said is very true, what he said is very true!"

Prison Ansheng wiped the cold sweat on his face, and quickly responded. Prisoner Long's ancestor didn't know where he died, and he didn't seem to care about the matter. The will is it, and now it is still a matter of life.

"Come on, go to the battlefield."

Bai Donglin raised his hand and waved, and put the jade pagoda crystal ball and other treasures into the newly created "Infernal World" within his body, and then glanced at Li You.

Li You nodded solemnly, he had already arranged everything, the lame uncle's injury had also been cured, and he was taken care of by prisoner Miaokong, so he had no worries.

"Follow your orders!"

Prison Ansheng bowed to take orders, stretched his hand into his arms, took out the black iron token, held the magic formula in his hand, and said silently in his mouth.

"Senran black prison, ten thousand catastrophe is empty."

"I, prisoner secretly, with a service number... now apply to return to the battlefield."

Buzz!

The black iron token trembled for a while, and then spewed out dark rays of light, circling and converging, condensing into a densely patterned decree.

"My lord, I have obtained the customs clearance law, which can be used to cross the universe and reach the parallel universe of 'Binghai Qiqiwu'."

Under normal circumstances, private crossings between parallel universes are not allowed. For these, there are clear and strict regulations in the "Code of Life".

The long river of time and space radiates all parallel universe dimensions and time and space. Bai Donglin doesn't need to be so troublesome. He can walk on the long river of time and space and directly reach the target universe, but after all, it is the first time. It is not bad to take a formal channel. .

"Well, let's go."

A ray of divine light shot out from Bai Donglin's body, wrapped the two prisoners, and flew away.

Pass through the passages of time and space, and quickly escape to the cosmic link hub.

"Hey, this killer, finally left."

The void was rippling, and the prisoner dragon stepped out and fought on the empty hall with a worried look on his face.

"Hehe, Xiaolong, are you afraid of this seat?"

The prisoner dragon staggered and looked back with a dull look, only to see Bai Donglin sitting on the throne with a smile on his face, as if he had never left.

"Sir~www.mtlnovel.com~You..."

"Relax, Heavenly God Realm, I will handle it."

What Bai Donglin left behind was just a clone, a clone with 99% of his strength.

The avatars of 99% of the particles, including the world of spiritual apertures, were controlled by a trace of self-consciousness that he split.

There is immortality and immortality that can restore everything in an instant. In theory, as long as his id consciousness can be maintained, it can be divided infinitely.

This is his unique ability to defy the sky.

Its foundation is built on immortality.
