"Friend Daoist has immeasurable magical powers, we are convinced, and we dare not speculate on our own, but..."

"The Book of Words is of great importance. According to the regulations, we will truthfully inform all Dao Zun adults of everything that happened here. I hope you will think more about it."

"Farewell!"

After all, the Taihao powerhouses bowed in awe, and did not give Bai Donglin a chance to respond. With a flicker of his figure, he stepped into the long river of time and space and disappeared, and he has returned to the Taihao heavens.

Dao Zun is one of the honorary titles for the achievement of the eternal other side of the twelve realms. It is also called the Lord of the Great Dao of the Heavens, the Master of Order, the Source Lord...

Many appellations differ slightly depending on the difference in the cultivation system or the understanding of living beings, but the core meaning is the same. The masters of these taboos are all the top supreme beings in the heavens.

When Bai Donglin heard the words, he raised his brows indifferently. How could the eleventh realm be so fooled? They are all existences with their heels full of brains. It is not difficult to guess that the Book of Words is in his hands.

This matter can't be concealed, even if all the people present are killed. At this moment, those big bosses on the other side probably know everything.

Seeing Bai Donglin's silence, Chi Ming's mind moved, and he took two steps forward and said, "Brother Bai, please rest assured, this matter..."

"I'm not worried."

Bai Donglin shook his head slightly, with a calm expression. Since he dared to take down the Book of Words, he was naturally prepared to deal with everything. The worst result would be to open another hole card.

"Hehe, those beings, since they made their promises, they won't break their promises, right? If you make fun of us little guys, it's impossible to say."

He is not easy to figure out the thoughts of the twelve realms, but with the black disaster, the great enemy watching, there should be a bottom line for the open and secret battles within the heavens. Who dares to serve you?

This is a speculation based on logic. Whether this is the case or not remains to be seen.

"Hahaha, as expected of Brother Bai, I admire my strength and temperament!"

Chi Ming secretly admired in his heart that if he were to be placed in Bai Donglin's place, he would never be able to do so lightly. I don't know much either.

"Brother Bai!"

Chi Ming's expression suddenly became solemn, his eyes were burning, and he looked directly into Bai Donglin's eyes.

"I, Chi Ming, now with the will of the dimensional sword holder, I invite you to join the organization!"

buzz-

As Chi Ming's voice fell, time and space were violently distorted, and endless radiance surged out from the unknown, circling and dancing, condensing into a behemoth that towered over the universe.

This is a dark-gold long sword engraved with Dao patterns, surrounded by colorful divine light, the blade is downward, suspended behind Chi Ming, above the long river of time and space.

The long sword hilt is in the shape of a balance, with "Justice" written on the left and "Guardian" written on the right. A disc like a universe hangs at each end. Many stalwart figures. Where the handle and blade meet, a scarlet vertical pupil is inlaid, shining brightly, as if it can see through everything.

"This is……"

Bai Donglin's expression was stunned for a moment, and the fiery will that escaped from the long sword in his heart stirred a little ripples. He seemed to see a horrible existence, one by one, with their backs to all beings, facing the black disaster.

"Chiming, is this a bit too sudden? Is it so easy for the dimensional sword wielder to join? There is no need to test me or something?"

Bai Donglin is naturally willing to join the dimensional swordsman, no matter how he thinks it is a good thing that is beneficial and harmless, but Chi Ming is so direct, it is true that he will not be able to deal with him.

"Brother Bai, my Jianxin told me that you are a trustworthy person."

"Moreover, the dimensional sword wielders do not have so many terms and conditions, they are free. Only one thing needs to be made clear, that is, when facing the black disaster, they have the will to fight against it at all costs. enough!"

The black disaster is a nightmare that haunts the heavens. No existence dares to ignore it. It seems a simple request, but it is also very harsh. After all, not everyone has absolute fearlessness.

"In this case, then Bai is disrespectful. I am willing to join the dimensional sword holder!"

As soon as the words fell, the dark gold sword shone fiercely, with endless brilliance, converging in the red vertical pupils, and a river of light poured out, covering Bai Donglin.

This is to determine if I am a black plague creature?

Bai Donglin keenly sensed the meaning of peeping, but his eyes were very knowledgeable. He just confirmed his identity and immediately retreated without taking the opportunity to spy on his secrets.

Guanghe came and went quickly, shrinking and condensing together in an instant, turning into a golden sword print, falling slowly, and printing on Bai Donglin's sleeve.

What a powerful tool!

A flash of surprise flashed in Bai Donglin's eyes. This sword print is not as simple as being printed on the robe. Looking at the past, at every point in time, the sword print was printed on his sleeve. This is a direct brand. Above his "existence"!

chant—

The long sword hummed, pulled up violently, and slashed towards Bai Donglin.

Bai Donglin didn't move at all, and a long sword that was bigger than Daqianjie suddenly slashed. Although this vast scene was terrifying, he did not feel any danger.

The dark gold long sword pierced through time and space, and when it approached, it quickly shrank, took it lightly, and slowly placed it on Bai Donglin's shoulder, paused for a moment, and then disappeared abruptly.

This should be some kind of ceremony, just like when Liu Dongchuan drew his sword to pay homage, this is a romance and etiquette belonging to sword cultivators.

Ok? Could it be that the existence of the "dimensional sword-wielding organization" also uses swords? All indications are that it is very likely.

"Brother Bai, we will be a family from now on, hahaha!"

Chi Ming laughed heartily, reached out and patted Bai Donglin on the shoulder, his eyes were very kind.

"Hehe, Chi Ming, you are a 'senior', take care of me as a newcomer in the future."

Hearing Bai Donglin's joke, Chi Ming's eyes widened.

"Don't, Brother Bai, your strength is far stronger than mine, you should take care of me!"

Maybe one day, they will fight side by side again. This "care" is not a joke, but a fact that has a high probability of happening.

"Brother Bai, now that you have the identity of the dimensional sword holder, you don't have to worry about the book of words, hehe, the power of our organization is extraordinary, and no one in Taihao dares not give face, this is , you'll know later."

Chi Ming looked mysterious, and his tone showed pride.

Bai Donglin was thoughtful, and it seemed that he made the right move. Since Chi Ming dared to say such a thing, the organization must not be as simple as being based on Tae Ho.

"what?"

"Has this little guy completed his transformation?"

Bai Donglin sensed the change in the Infernal World, his eyes signaled Chi Ming to wait a moment, raised his hand, and an egg of chaos covered with mysterious patterns appeared in his palm.

Click! !

The egg of chaos trembled slightly, and then it shattered into dense cracks, and two small white hands protruded violently, tearing the extremely hard eggshell directly.

"Ooooooooooo!"

A force of swallowing came, and the eggshells formed by the condensed energy of chaos were all swallowed into a small mouth, chewed slightly, and swallowed into the abdomen.

"Yah~ Lord Father God! I miss you!!"

The miracle the size of her little finger at the beginning has become the size of a fist, and her whole body is full of rays of light. The most striking thing is that on top of her small head, she wears a crown that glows with divine light. The moon and stars, the crown formed by the condensed terrifying celestial bodies.

The fist-sized babble, stepped out in one step, smashed the void, and instantly lay on Bai Donglin's face, squinting and dawdling, and his little face was full of attachment and admiration.

"This is... such a powerful inborn being!"

Chi Ming looked surprised, and looked up and down, he could be sure that the power contained in this small body had reached the tenth realm!

It is very rare to be born in the ten realms. As we all know, the innate gods and demons born in the chaos are only the strength of the nine realms in the beginning, and all the great powers in the later stage are also acquired through hard practice.

"She's called Miracle."

Bai Donglin's expression was light, and he pulled Eiya off his face. He had evaluated Eiya's talent from the very beginning. Under the nurturing of his endless chaotic energy, he was expected to be able to innate ten realms.

"This is Uncle Chiming, please say hello."

Although Yiya has powerful power, it is still a blank white paper and doesn't understand anything.

"Hello, Uncle Chiming, I'm a miracle, you can just call me Eiya."

Yiya raised her little face, milky voice, very well-behaved, occasionally turning back to peek at Bai Donglin, revealing a look that was just about to move.

"Uh, how are you?"

Chi Ming's eyes shone brightly, but he still couldn't see through the babbling heels, which did not match all the creatures he knew.

"Hey, I'm now teaching you the basic etiquette of being a human being. The first point is that all intelligent beings have to wear clothes, and they are not allowed to show themselves naked at any time."

At this time, Eiya was shrouded in the rays of the sun, and although it could cover her body, it was not considered clothing.

After Bai Donglin finished speaking, he stretched out his hand to probe, and fell into a large particle of thousands of particles not far away. He retracted it in an instant, and in the palm of his hand, a sea of ​​stars that spanned hundreds of millions of light-years had already been captured.

A few strands of hair fell and shot into the sea of ​​​​stars in the palm of his hand. Smelting, compressing, the outside world has passed for a moment, and the palm of your hand has been frozen in time and space for ten thousand years. A small skirt condensed with endless stars slowly floats out and automatically wears it on Yiya.

"Yah~ so beautiful! Thank you, Father God!"

Eiya looked up and down at the sparkling skirt, and loved it very much. At a young age, the instinct of women has been awakened.

"Hehe, I didn't expect that Brother Bai is not only powerful, but also the needlework is not bad."

Chi Ming sneered, evaluating the skirt's defense in his heart, and found that he had to cut three swords to break it open. Once again, he saw Bai Donglin's strength, which can be achieved with a few strands of hair.

"Yah, when we met for the first time, Uncle Chiming also gave you a small gift."

Chi Ming flipped his hand and took out a blue long sword, reduced it to the size of a matchstick, and put it on Eiya's little hand.

"Thank you, Uncle Chiming!"

Ye Ya drew his sword and slashed, and the long river of sword light roared past, tearing apart the distant particle Daqian into a huge ravine.

An ancient artifact with complete origin!

Chi Ming is really generous.

"Chiming, I ask you one thing."

"I want to explore the Void Realm. It's not very convenient to be with me, so please take care of it."

The Realm of Nothingness is very dangerous. There is no doubt about it. Bai Donglin is not sure whether the "Uninterrupted World", which was once invincible, can withstand all kinds of dangers. He is immortal and immortal.

"Lord Father God..."

Ye Ya is pitiful and full of reluctance, but she is extremely well-behaved and doesn't say much.

"Such trivial matters are naturally fine, but Brother Bai, are you sure you want to go to the Land of Nothingness alone?"

Chi Ming frowned, the realm of nothingness is not comparable to the interior of the heavens, even in the eleventh realm, the probability of falling is very high~www.mtlnovel.com~ I have made up my mind. "

Bai Donglin's eyes are firm and his will is unshakable.

"Understood! Brother Bai, don't leave Taihao too far. If you encounter danger, there is a way out."

Every strong man has his own way to go, he has no reason to interfere with Bai Donglin's choice, at most he can only make some suggestions.

"Hehe, don't worry, I'll be fine."

"Take care of yourself!"

Chi Ming stretched out his hand and held his eyes with tears in his eyes, babbling reluctantly, his figure moved, and he stepped into the long river of time and space and disappeared.

"I'm the only one left..."

Bai Donglin looked around, only the endless darkness and silence accompanied him.
