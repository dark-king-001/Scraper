"What is your name?"

In the dark void, Bai Donglin's voice echoed endlessly, but there was silence in response to him.

The blue flowers that covered hundreds of millions of light-years, twisted roots and swayed petals, seemed to be a dead thing that only knew how to operate according to the established rules.

Bai Donglin would not be confused by this appearance. He sensed clear fluctuations of consciousness in the deep source space.

"Submit to me, do things for me, and spare your life."

After tasting the sweetness of the energy crystal, Bai Donglin still valued this flower, and wanted to subdue it and transform it to help him devour the wreckage of the heavens.

Take a break! !

Countless transparent roots were pulled out from the void and stabbed towards Bai Donglin fiercely, with extraordinary power.

boom!

These attacks were too weak, Bai Donglin didn't dodge or evade, let him attack, but he couldn't even break through his breath force field.

"You whole murderer!"

"My master is dead, you still want me to surrender to you? Dreaming! Go to die!!"

The crisp anger escaped from the depths of the flower. The next moment, the nine petals folded together, wrapping Bai Donglin in it, and the wave of destruction slowly spread out.

The blue flower is about to explode.

"Hehe, not bad, quite loyal."

boom--

The fierce explosion engulfed Bai Donglin, and the power of endless destruction was still unable to reach his body surface.

"If it weren't for your usefulness, I would be too lazy to care about you."

Helplessly shaking his head, Bai Donglin stepped into the long river of time and space and returned to the moment before the explosion.

"Submit to me."

boom--

"Hey, it's pretty stubborn!"

After millions of times of self-destruction in a row, the blue flower finally stopped self-destruction. It was a little scared. It didn't know how this eccentric did it. The memory of each self-destruction and death was deeply engraved in its memory. .

"You, what do you want!?"

"Submit to me! I will be a better master than Kuer's greed, don't you know what to do! There are so many people in the world crying and shouting to surrender to me, to be the **** boy, I am all Disdain."

"You! You made a lot of money!"

The blue flower was silent for a while, but this weirdo was much stronger than the previous owner, and it was reasonable to have followers.

Sensing the loosening of the opponent's will, Bai Donglin raised the corner of his mouth, and continued: "I have seen through your roots, you are a congenital **** and demon born in chaos, and you were unexpectedly subdued by Kuer's greed, your feelings are really true. Is it that deep?"

"Hehe, you are not a black disaster creature, so be grateful for this, otherwise, I would have pinched you to death."

The blue flower is a kind of plant-based chaotic **** and demon called "Nine Yao". It was born in chaos. It has the strength of the nine realms innately, and is basically possessed by nine kinds of origin supernatural powers.

Its roots, which can extend infinitely, cross dimensional space dimensions, and even world lines, are three of its characteristics.

In Bai Donglin's view, the role of Jiuyao is somewhat similar to that of Little Green, the Heart of Dimension, but with stronger heels and greater strength and potential.

"Jiuyao, have you ever heard that in front of the strong, the weak have no right to die."

"If you still want to play, I can accompany you, 100 million times, 1 trillion times, it doesn't matter, you can blow yourself up as much as you like, anyway, I will resurrect you."

"Until you surrender to me completely."

Bai Donglin looked comfortable, lying directly in Jiuyou's flowery heart, which was the center of self-destruction.

Uh, flowers seem to be the reproductive organs of plants, right? Cough, no, how can the chaotic gods be compared with ordinary plants? Definitely not that stuff.

"You are shameless!"

"I don't have the air of being a strong man at all!"

A ball of light flashing with nine-color brilliance slowly floated out and fit between Bai Donglin's eyebrows, a strange wave flashed, and the contract was signed.

What the two signed is an equality contract, and the only limitation is that they cannot hurt each other.

Jiuyao signed the same contract with Kuer's greed before, so Kuer's greed's death did not affect Jiuyao in the slightest.

The corners of Bai Donglin's mouth rose slightly. He was not surprised at all that he signed an equality contract, and he didn't ask for more, he was just joking.

Chaos gods and demons have very eccentric temperaments, advocating absolute freedom, and have reached the point of paranoia. It is impossible for them to be slaves and servants, even death.

Even the equality contract is not very willing to sign. After Kuer's death, Jiu Yaoben regained his freedom, but he would rather explode himself than submit to Bai Donglin.

"Isn't that right, follow me and keep you delicious and spicy!"

The consciousness of the Chaos Gods and Demons is very special and cannot be forcibly tampered with by rude means. Moreover, only the willing Jiuyao can control the empty-devouring towers made by the black disaster creatures. This is also the reason why Bai Donglin is calm and uses the Huairou method.

"Jiuyao, your future work is to devour the remains of the heavens for me and convert them into energy crystals. Here, this is your reward."

Bai Donglin flipped the palm of his hand and took out a shining diamond crystal, which exuded a strange aura. When any creature saw it, the desire to devour it would appear in their hearts.

stop!

The nine-color ball of light flashed, instantly swallowing the crystal in Bai Donglin's hand, the brilliance trembled, and Jiuyao was so comfortable that he moaned.

"What, what is this!?"

Jiuyao no longer has the calmness just now, and his tone is full of disbelief. Obviously, he has benefited from the crystal.

"You don't need to know, you just need to understand that every time I devour the wreckage of the heavens, you can get 10,000 crystals, the same as just now."

"Well, the Yarrow heavens that are about to be devoured can be counted as an entry benefit for you."

"make a deal!!"

Jiuyao agreed decisively, and he was completely different from seeing death not long ago.

Bai Donglin nodded with satisfaction. This crystal was formed by condensing "enhanced energy". Damage reversal was a talent that had the same root as immortality and immortality. Naturally, the transformed enhanced energy was not that simple, and it was of great benefit to living beings.

"Can you still control those empty towers?"

After all, these things are the creation of the black disaster, and the number is extremely large. Although he can make the same thing in the same way, the cost of time and resources is too great, and it is best to use it directly.

"Well, all the empty-devouring towers, the control authority is in my hands, and the death of Kuer's greed has no effect."

"Very good, but before the construction starts, the Empty Devouring Tower has to be remodeled. Otherwise, even if it goes smoothly, it will take at least 10 million years to devour the wreckage of the heavens, which is too slow!"

How vast the heavens are, this speed is actually very fast, but it is related to the improvement of energy and strength, naturally the faster the better.

Moreover, after 10 million years, the "Zhentian Daoyan" will be opened. Isn't it only possible to swallow the wreckage of one side of the heavens? He wants to improve his strength as much as possible before returning to Tahoe, and it is best to cross the border and fight against the enemy's Twelve Realms!

"This is already the fastest speed to run at full capacity without considering the wear and tear of the Empty Eating Tower. In addition to maintaining the Empty Eating Tower from time to time, 10 million years is already the most ideal speed."

Jiuyao is noncommittal. It has lived for a long time, and its concept of time has become like a lake. In its eyes, 10 million years is not a long time. The concept is naturally different from Bai Donglin, a "young man".

"As for the transformation, let's forget it. The Empty Eater Tower is a black disaster that has spent a lot of effort. The treasures refined have reached the limit, and it is difficult to improve."

"Black disaster is not the authority of everything."

Bai Donglin glanced at Jiuyao, this guy is estimated to have lived with the black disaster for a long time, and subtly, he highly respects the black disaster.

"The essence can't be improved, wouldn't it be enough to give them a plug-in support?"

While speaking, Bai Donglin spread out his palm, and a world of spiritual apertures flashing purple light slowly emerged.

That's right, this is what he created in the only real world that devoured the power of the dimension. He called it "Hongmeng Lingzhu" because he inherited the characteristics of Hongmeng Purple Qi.

With the improvement of his strength, he has improved and enhanced the Hongmeng Spirit Orb a lot. In addition, the Spirit Aperture World, which is a raw material for manufacturing, is also stronger than before, and its power to devour and transform is extremely amazing.

"What is this?"

"Are you curious baby? Why 100,000? You don't need to know the principle. I have thoroughly understood the rules of the operation of the empty tower. This bead is a plug-in that I made for them. You place it in the empty tower. Just swallow the center of the vortex, and it will automatically activate."

Bai Donglin glared at Jiuyao, it was just an equality contract, and he wanted to know his secret?

"Got it~www.mtlnovel.com~ If you don't say it, don't say it! What's so fierce..."

Watching Jiuyao return to the source space, the huge body also began to move, Bai Donglin clasped his palm upside down, and countless Primordial Spirit Orbs spewed out, submerged into the flower heart, and instantly transmitted to each and every one through the transparent roots. In the swallowing vortex of the empty tower.

boom--

With the addition of the Hongmeng Spirit Orb, the swallowing vortex is like a chicken blood, the rotation speed is accelerated, and the coverage is constantly expanding.

Its power of devouring and transformation has skyrocketed wildly, and its efficiency has increased more than a thousand times.

"That's right, like this, 10,000 years is enough to swallow up the wreckage of one side of the heavens!"

Bai Donglin nodded in satisfaction, the fact that things were so troublesome was because his strength was not strong enough.

If the supernatural realm can cover the entire heavens and directly freeze time and space, where do you need to worry about taking too long?

Or, his chaotic vortex can directly swallow the heavens in one bite...

In a word, Jiuyao's devouring system is only used as a temporary transitional thing. When the strength is improved, it will naturally be eliminated.

Everything was arranged properly, Bai Donglin sat cross-legged in Jiuyao's soft flower heart, closed his eyes and fell into practice.

His power is skyrocketing too fast, and it needs to be well integrated before it can be used perfectly.

At the same time, the three corpses that had just been obtained, as well as the broken origin universe, were also quickly digested in the chaotic vortex, and the spiritual world continued to grow stronger.

"Well, Curacao, the blade of weeping blood, should wake up from the self-slumber..."

"This knife is much stronger than ordinary ancient artifacts, even if it is an ancient artifact with complete origin."

His fists were so hard that even ancient artifacts could be blasted, and ordinary divine weapons were a little less interesting to him.

It's still interesting in Curacao, not only can kill people, but also give birth to cubs!
