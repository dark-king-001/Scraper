Huang Nan's consciousness was agitated for a while, and he came back to his senses in an instant, revealing the color of anger, and the endless mighty power in the body boiled up. It was Huang Nan, a stupid woman, who wanted to look at him rashly, but her skills were inferior to others, and she was directly crushed by the fluctuations of consciousness that escaped from him unintentionally.

"Huangnan, in the end, the other side gave you a lot of skills, but it didn't teach you how to behave in the world."

"Today, I'll give you a good lesson, what is meant by the sky outside the sky, and there are people outside the world!"

Bai Donglin slowly stretched out his palm, and the hair came first. Two fingers crossed time and space, pinching the extremely sharp sun and moon wheel tightly, not allowing an inch to advance.

Click!

With a slight tremor between his fingers, the smooth surface of the sun and moon wheel was instantly covered with cracks, and a genuine ancient artifact was scrapped.

"what!?"

Huang Nan's heart was terrified, his brows were tingling, his mind and spiritual sense were drowned and swallowed by the shadow of death, and his figure froze uncontrollably in the same place. The next moment, a slender and powerful palm was already pinched on her like a white jade. On the warm and white neck.

Ten colors of divine light flowed out, covering and lingering on the surface of Huangnan's body, completely suppressing and suppressing its power, even being isolated from time and space, the source of existence was forever frozen in the world, and the powerful life-saving ability of roaming time in eleven realms, deprived.
