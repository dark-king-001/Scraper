The will of the observer, all poured into the dark and profound blood of detachment, and spread it out with all his strength.

Darkness, distortion and confusion, this is the weirdness that subverts the concept of space.

Bai Donglin calmed down and avoided all kinds of negative anomalies by ignoring everything. In an instant, the will that spanned tens of thousands of supernatural thoughts had stretched to the limit, but he still couldn't touch the edge of the abstract and hazy space, and he couldn't help feeling a little Powerless.

"It can't be wrong, it really is the blood of the transcendence!"

The same feeling of powerlessness reminded Bai Donglin of the detached blood he met in the statue of the Corpse Continent. He couldn't see the difference between the two. The only feeling was that it was unfathomable and indescribable.

The difference from the last time is that his strength is worlds apart, and the area covered by his will is thousands of times larger, so he should be able to capture more things.

"coming!"

Bai Donglin's spirit was shocked, and as expected, the indescribable character that could overload his mind just by forcing memory appeared again.

They seem to be ghosts with life. In the abstract and hazy boundless space, they shuttle back and forth, and with a slight flash, they can escape the perception range of the will.

"What are these things? What are their forms of existence? What are their functions?"

"This should not be as simple as the power of ordinary concepts!"

"Huh? Could it be..."

Bai Donglin didn't know what to think, the light of aura emerged in his thinking, and a lot of information was intertwined in his heart. The inexplicable stirring intuition locked one out of all kinds of ideas.

"The Narrative Transcendence!

"

Narrative, metaphysics, for narrative-level detached people, they are above the existing real narrative world in which they live, and all known and unknown are within the inclusion and control of narrative.

"Gene Era"

Narrative detachment not only refers to the bottom of the narrative that initially breaks the fourth wall, but is the highest level of proof, it is at the top of the narrative echelon, or even detached from the narrative.

As for the rest, more…

Bai Donglin frowned, he didn't know, his understanding of the narrative level was only so far, and it was the ambiguous words summed up by trying his best to collect information over the years.

He imagined that this drop of blood came from a narrative-level transcendence, so it could make sense why these jumping "characters" were so incomprehensible. He only touched the power of concepts not long ago, and it was absolutely not so mysterious and unpredictable.

It can be seen that this should be the power of narrative, another kind of terror.

"If it is the power of narrative, it may be possible to change self-perception and observe from another perspective..."

Bai Donglin's will was agitated, and through his understanding of the narrative level, he began to distort his cognition. This is a very dangerous behavior. If you can't return from the chaotic cognition afterwards, it will lead to the loss and collapse of the self.

However, in the observer state, he doesn't have to worry about encountering such a bad situation.

boom-

After a brief blank in consciousness, Bai Donglin's memory was temporarily isolated, and he tampered with his cognition from the root of everything in the past.

At this time, in Bai Donglin's eyes, everything has become false, endless heavens, all races, black disasters, transcendence, everything that happened in the only real world, and even the novel called "Bai Donglin" figure…

His self is detached with the distortion of cognition, and everything in the real world has become the false world depicted in the book, whether it is Bai Donglin or a detached one.

And he, just an ordinary mortal, accidentally opened this book and saw this vast and bright false world, and also saw the meaning of the "words" represented by the so-called blood of the transcendence.

Distorted cognition!

Simulate a narrative perspective!

"Well, the narrative-level transcendence - Po Lun Kshadi, the blood left behind carries the power of sacrifice."

Whoa!

The sound of turning the pages of the book resounded strangely in my heart.

"On the continent of corpses, the blood left behind is..."

There was a hint of doubt in the distorted cognition. At this point, Bai Donglin didn't know who the blood of detachment in the statue came from. It was naturally impossible to break the logic and see the specific details about "her" from a simulated perspective. information, so there will be distortion and lag.

"The meaning of that character - suppression, isolation!"

boom-

After seeing through the meaning of the characters, Bai Donglin triggered the backhand in his self-distorted cognition, randomly falling from the narrative perspective, and his cognition was put in order, overlapping the real world.

"Hoohoo~ so dangerous!"

Bai Donglin was horrified. He was a little too embarrassed. It was only the first time that he used the method he imagined to this extent. If it wasn't for the observer's state of ignoring all the negatives, he probably wouldn't be able to find it in his confused cognition. Back to myself.

Although it was only a short moment, it was even more dangerous and terrifying than falling into the "real illusion" at the beginning. This was completely self-destruction.

After a long time, Bai Donglin regained his senses, and there was still a little bit of movement in his heart. That kind of weird feeling, he really didn't want to try again, the sense of emptiness of losing one's self and losing his existence, which made him feel very uncomfortable. People go crazy.

"It turns out that the function of this drop of transcendental blood is sacrifice!"

Bai Donglin understands the meaning of those characters, but that's all, it doesn't mean that he can understand them and master their power, which are completely two different things.

It is easy to understand that, by tampering with the "he" who has distorted cognition, and observing everything from a narrative perspective, then everything in the real world becomes a textual description in the book, which is false and only contains literal meanings.

For example, a certain immortal emperor used a trick to "transform him into freedom". The reader knows that this trick calls him into freedom, but how can they know the principle of this trick? How is it performed?

It is completely impossible. What the narrative perspective can see is only the superficial awareness of the text, just like his distorted cognition just now, what he sees is just a description of the text - "The Poluksha Emperor left a drop of blood. , UU reading www.uukanshu.com is used to sacrifice to the nine evil lands, and then there is nothing else."

It is already very good to be able to do this. Thanks to the collection of information, it is like the drop of transcendental blood that suppressed the Ruthless Great Emperor. Because there is no more information, Bai Donglin cannot see the transcendence. name.

"I'm sorry, I didn't expect that the Abandoned Land would involve a narrative-level transcendence..."

Bai Donglin felt a little bitter in his heart. Regarding the description of the power of the detached, he roughly knew that there were only three types: concept, narrative, and logic.

The logical detachment, according to some secret sayings, doesn't exist at all, it's just an assumption.

Then, the narrative-level detachment is already the overlord among the detached, and there are not many existences that can be stronger than them.

"Hey, in this way, Dr. Chen who confronted the Poluksha Emperor is also a narrative level? Tsk tsk, worthy of being a fellow, amazing, amazing!"

By this time, Bai Donglin was still in the mood to ridicule, but he did not regard the situation in front of him as a boundless desperation.

It was the same, how big a foot wears a big shoe, if he can handle it, he has to fight hard. But if the development of things completely surpassed his ability, it would be fine to just run away, and the sky would fall, and there would naturally be those who were detached.

He is just a mere other side, very weak and unable to take on big responsibilities.

"All right!"

"I want to see what this so-called sacrifice is!"

Bai Donglin pressed his thoughts down, his will finally glanced at the misty space, and then backed away, he couldn't do anything about this drop of blood.

"Before that, I have to meet the phantom left by Dr. Chen and see what his plans are."

With a thought, he locked a clone of him and me in the first murderous place, the Prison of Nothingness, and came back to life.
